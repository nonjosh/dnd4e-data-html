Gusting Wind
Terrain
Level 7 HazardXP 300

Detect Perception DC 16

Initiative +8

Immune attacks

Triggered Actions

Attack At-Will

Trigger: A creature enters the area of the gusting wind.

Attack (Opportunity Action): Close burst 2 (creatures in burst); +10 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d6 + 4 damage, the target is pushed 2 squares downhill and toward the ground, and its movement ends.

Miss: Half damage, and the target is pushed 2 squares downhill.

Countermeasures

Evade: Acrobatics DC 23 Success: The creature takes half damage and is pushed 1 square.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 189.
