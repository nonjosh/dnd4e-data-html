The Thing in the Mirror
Object
Level 1 TrapXP 100

Detect Arcana or Perception DC 9

Initiative —

HP 1

AC 10, Fortitude 10, Reflex 5, Will —

Immune all conditions, necrotic, ongoing damage, poison, psychic

Triggered Actions

Attack At-Will

Trigger: A creature adjacent to the mirror looks into it.

Attack (Free Action): Melee 1 (triggering creature); +4 vs. Will

Hit: The target is stunned (save ends).

    First Failed Saving Throw: The target is removed from play until the mirror drops to 0 hit points, at which point the target appears in a random unoccupied square adjacent to the mirror.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 211.
