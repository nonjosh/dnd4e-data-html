Trigger: A creature enters the warded area without saying the password.

Special: Once the glyph is triggered, it disappears and cannot be triggered again.

Countermeasures

Disable: Arcana (trained) or Thievery DC 20 (standard action). Requirement: The character must be adjacent to the glyph. Success: The character disables the glyph. Failure (15 or lower): The character triggers the trap.
