Sphere of Annihilation
Object
Level 29 TrapXP 15000

Detect automatic

Initiative -

Immune attacks

Triggered Actions

Attack At-Will

Trigger: A creature enters the sphere’s square or the sphere enters a creature’s square.

Attack (Opportunity Action): Melee 0 (one creature); +32 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 6d6 + 10 damage, and ongoing 15 damage (save ends). A creature reduced to 0 hit points or fewer by this attack is destroyed, reduced to a pile of fine gray dust.

Countermeasures

Control: Requires talisman. Intelligence DC 30 (move action). Success: The creature can slide the sphere one square for every 5 points of check result above 25 (30–34 = 1 square, 35–39 = 2 squares, and so on). Failure (29 or less): The creature instead pulls the sphere 1
square.

Published in Dungeon Master's Kit, page(s) 223.
