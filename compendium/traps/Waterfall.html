Waterfall
Level 11 HazardXP 0

Detect Nature or Perception DC 13

Initiative -

Immune attacks

Triggered Actions

Attack At-Will

Trigger: A creature enters a waterfall square.

Attack (Opportunity Action): Melee 0 (triggering creature); +14 vs. Reflex

Hit: The target falls prone and slides 3 squares toward the nearby ravine. If the target goes over the edge of the ravine, it falls 80 feet to a shallow stream unless it makes a saving throw. Climbing back to the ravine requires a DC 20 Athletics check, and a DC 25 Athletics check to climb through the ravine opening.

Miss: The target returns to the last square it occupied before entering the waterfall square and its movement ends.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 161.
