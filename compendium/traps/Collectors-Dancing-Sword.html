Collector's Dancing Sword
Object
Level 23 HazardXP 5100

Detect automatic

Initiative +19

Immune attacks

Traits

Opportunistic Blade

The sword can make opportunity attacks.

Standard Actions

Attack At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +28 vs. AC

Hit: 3d8 + 18 damage.

Move Actions

Move At-Will

Effect: The sword flies up to 6 squares. It can hover.

Countermeasures

Delay: Arcana DC 37 (minor action). Requirement: The character attempting the skill check must be within 5 squares of the sword. Success: The sword cannot attack until the end of its next turn. Failure (32 or lower): As a free action, the sword flies up to 6 squares and attacks the character.

Parry: Athletics or Acrobatics DC 37 (minor action). Requirement: The character attempting the skill check must be adjacent to the sword. Success: The character pushes the sword up to 3 squares. Failure (32 or lower): As a free action, the sword attacks the character.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 204.
