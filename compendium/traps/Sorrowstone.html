Sorrowstone
Hazard
Level 4 Warder
XP 175

This huge stone obelisk stands 10 feet tall and has been carved with the names of House Madar ancestors. A palpable aura of grief and loss surrounds the stone in a heavy shroud.

Hazard: This stone, imbued with grief and rage, has become a battery of negative emotion created by the tragic events that befell House Madar.
