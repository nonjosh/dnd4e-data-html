Blood Tree
Hazard
Level 9 Obstacle
XP 400

An ominous tree rises from the grassy floor. Cruel barbs extend from its black bark. Blood-red leaves rustle despite the absence of a breeze.

Hazard: A blood tree fills a square and is blocking terrain. All squares adjacent to the tree are difficult terrain. Once a character succeeds on this check, it is not targeted by the blood tree’s attacks for the rest of the encounter.
     The blood tree has AC 14, Fortitude 19, Reflex 14, hp 100, and resist 10 poison. When the hazard is reduced to 0 hit points, it is destroyed.
