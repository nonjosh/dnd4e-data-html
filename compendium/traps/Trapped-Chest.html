Trapped Chest
Object
Level 10 Minion TrapXP 125

Detect Perception DC 22

Initiative —

Triggered Actions

Attack (poison) Encounter

Trigger: A creature opens the chest without disarming it or depressing all three buttons at once.

Attack (No Action): Close blast 3 (triggering creature in the blast); +13 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 15 poison damage.

Countermeasures

Disable: Thievery DC 26 (standard action). Requirement: A character must have thieves’ tools. Success: The trap does not attack when opened. Failure (21 or lower): The character triggers the trap.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 215.
