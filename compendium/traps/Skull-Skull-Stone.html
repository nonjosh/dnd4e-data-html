Skull-Skull Stone
Trap
Level 1 Blaster
XP 100

This sludge-covered stone is tied to a long rope that hangs from a hook in the ceiling.

Trap: When a kobold throws the stone, it attacks a target and then returns to the kobold on the opposite platform.TriggerA kobold uses a standard action to attack with the skull-skull stone. It can be used by two different kobolds in each round.AttackStandard Action      Melee
Target: One character in the marked area on the map.Attack: +8 vs. ACHit: 1d8+2 damage and push 2 squares.Countermeasures     A character in the marked area can ready an action to attack the rope (AC 14, 10 hp, and resist 5 to all damage).
     A character can make ranged attacks against the rope.

Published in Dungeon Master's Guide, page(s) 215.
