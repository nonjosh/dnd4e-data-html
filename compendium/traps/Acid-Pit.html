Acid Pit
Trap
Level 23 Obstacle
XP 5100

Acid fills this deep basin, burning all that falls within it.

Trap: The acid attacks any creature in a trap square.Perception     No check is necessary to notice the depression fi lled with acid.TriggerWhen a creature enters or starts its turn in a trapped square, the trap attacks.AttackOpportunity Action      Melee
Targets Each creature in a trapped squareAttack: +28 vs. ACHit: 3d10 + 5 acid damage, and the target takes ongoing 15 acid damage (save ends).    Aftereffect The target is dazed until the end of its next turn.Countermeasures     A creature can make a saving throw to avoid being pushed into the acid pit trap by the altar of compassion attack. Unfortunately, the sides of the acid pit are slick and all such saving throws take a -2 penalty.
