Traits

Mesmeric Aurora (charm, zone)

The zone covers the entire bridge. Any creature that can see that ends its turn in the zone is dazed (save ends). A creature can be dazed only once by the zone.

Downpour

Until the storm passes, all squares inside it are lightly obscured by heavy rain.

Standard Actions

Attack (lightning, teleportation, thunder) At-Will

Attack: Ranged special (one creature on the bridge); +16 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d10 + 10 lightning and thunder damage, and the target is teleported 1d4 squares in a random direction.

Miss: Half damage, and the target slides 1 square in a random direction.
