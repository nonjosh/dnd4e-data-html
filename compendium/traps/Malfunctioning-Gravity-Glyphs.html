Malfunctioning Gravity Glyphs
Area
Level 3 HazardXP 150

Detect Arcana DC 21

Initiative +5

Immune attacks

Standard Actions

Effect At-Will

Requirement: Five or more creatures must be in the room.

Effect: Each creature in the room makes a saving throw. On a failure, the creature is thrown against the floor or ceiling, taking 2d6 damage.

Countermeasures

Avoid: Arcana or Insight DC 13 (standard action). Success: The character or one of its allies gains a +2 bonus to its next saving throw made against this hazard.
