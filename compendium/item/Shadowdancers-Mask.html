Shadowdancer's MaskLevel 10 Uncommon

This featureless black mask aids your efforts to fool or hide from your foes.

Head Slot        5,000 gp

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You make a Bluff check or Stealth check and dislike the result.

Effect: Reroll the check with a +3 power bonus and use either result.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 105.
