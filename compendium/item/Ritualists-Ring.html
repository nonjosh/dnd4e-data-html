Ritualist's RingLevel 24 Uncommon

This engraved mahogany ring allows you to cast rituals more quickly and easily.

Ring Slot        525,000 gp

Property

Gain a +2 item bonus to checks to perform rituals.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Reduce by half the time necessary to perform a ritual.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, also reduce the component cost by half for non-Creation rituals.
