Keoghtom's OintmentLevel 12 Uncommon

This tiny jar magically creates a dollop of potent healing unguent each day.

Wondrous Item        13,000 gp

Power (Healing) Daily (Standard Action)

Apply this substance to yourself or an adjacent ally. That creature automatically ends one disease or poison effect that a save can end or regains one healing surge (your choice).
