Potion of Lesser HasteLevel 10 Uncommon

When you consume the silvery liquid of this elixir, your body begins to blur as your movement quickens.

Consumable: Potion        200 gp

Utility Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Effect: You drink the potion and lose a healing surge. You gain an extra move action to use either during your current turn or your next turn.
