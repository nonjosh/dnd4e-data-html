Horn of Undead EnmityLevel 17 Uncommon

The haunting sound of this bone horn forces undead to turn against their own allies.

Wondrous Item        65,000 gp

Power (Charm) Daily (Standard Action)

When you sound the horn, make an attack: Close blast 5; targets undead only; +20 vs. Will. On a hit, the target makes its next attack against one of its adjacent allies. If it has no adjacent allies, it charges its nearest ally. If the target can't attack or charge, it moves its speed away from you.
