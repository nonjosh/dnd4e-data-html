Absorbing ShieldLevel 17 Uncommon

This steel shield absorbs the power from attacks, protecting both you and your allies.

Arms Slot: Any shield        65,000 gp

Power (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An area attack misses you and hits an ally.

Effect: The triggering attack instead misses the ally.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 53.
