Circlet of RevelationsLevel 10 Uncommon

This slender mithral circlet grants you the unique abilities of your sibling-race allies.

Head Slot        5,000 gp

Properties

  • While you have an eladrin ally within 10 squares of you, you gain a +5 item bonus to saving throws against charm effects.
  • While you have an elf ally within 10 squares of you, each ally within 5 squares of you gains a +1 item bonus to Perception checks.
  • While you have a drow ally within 10 squares of you, you gain darkvision.


Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 132.
