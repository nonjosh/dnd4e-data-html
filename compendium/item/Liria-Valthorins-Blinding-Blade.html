Liria Valthorin's Blinding BladeLevel 21 Uncommon

Your blade pulses with eldritch power, and your opponent's sight is lost to the aching beauty of the Feywild.

Grandmaster Training        225,000 gp

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You hit with a melee attack.

Effect: The target is blinded (save ends).

Special: If the triggering attack was made with a longsword, the target takes a -2 penalty to its saving throw to end this effect.
