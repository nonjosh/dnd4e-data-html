Syllable of GraceLevel 2 Uncommon

The sigil Uur flares with pale light, lending grace and speed to your movements.

Consumable: Consumable        20 gp

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You roll for initiative.

Effect: You gain a +2 item bonus to your initiative check. This power is lost when you use the word of creation's consumable power.

Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Requirement: You must be able to speak Abyssal or Supernal.

Effect: You speak the syllable and it orbits your head. You gain a +3 bonus to AC against opportunity attacks and a +3 item bonus to speed until the end of the encounter.



Published in Dragon Magazine 392.
