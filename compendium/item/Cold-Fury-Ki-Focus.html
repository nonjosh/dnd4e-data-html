Implement: Ki focus

Property

When you use your second wind, your attacks using this ki focus deal extra cold damage until the end of your next turn. The extra damage equals the ki focus’s enhancement bonus.

Attack Power (Cold) Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You hit an adjacent enemy with an attack using this ki focus.

Effect: The target is immobilized until it takes damage from an attack, until it uses a standard action to end this effect, or until the end of the encounter. If it is damaged before the end of your next turn, it takes extra cold damage equal to 3 + the ki focus’s enhancement bonus.
