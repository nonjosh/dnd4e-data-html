Echo of the Tree of LifeLevel 16 Uncommon

A majestic tree of life instills a healing boon on the worthy.

Echo of Power        45,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Heal checks and to any skill checks related to the performance of a scrying ritual.

Power (Healing) Daily (Standard Action)

An ally adjacent to you can spend a healing surge and regain additional hit points equal to half your healing surge value.



Published in Dark Sun Campaign Setting, page(s) 211.
