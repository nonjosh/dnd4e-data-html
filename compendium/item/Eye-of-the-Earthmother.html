Eye of the EarthmotherLevel 19 Uncommon

A knothole resembling an eye peers out of this fist-sized tangle of roots, which floats near your head.

Head Slot        105,000 gp

Property

You know the origin, type, and keyword(s) of any creature in sight.

Power (Charm) Daily (Standard Action)

Make a ranged attack: Ranged sight; affects beasts only; Intelligence + 4, Wisdom + 4, or Charisma + 4 vs. Will; on a hit, the targeted beast is dominated until the end of your next turn. Sustain minor (repeat the attack roll and hit to continue dominating the target).
