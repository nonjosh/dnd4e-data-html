Gauntlets of Remote ActionLevel 9 Uncommon

You send one of these gauntlets into the air, where it waits for you to control its actions from a safe distance.

Hands Slot        4,200 gp

Utility Power At-Will (Minor Action)

Effect: You cause one of the gauntlets to lift off your hand and float in a square adjacent to you. The gauntlet does not occupy its square. While the gauntlet is floating, you can perform actions with it as if your hand were still in it, such as opening doors, picking up objects, and making Thievery checks. You cannot use the floating gauntlet to make attacks or to perform any action requiring two hands.
    You can take a move action to move the gauntlet up to 10 squares, and it must end the move within 10 squares of you. If the gauntlet is adjacent to you, you can return it to your hand as a minor action.
