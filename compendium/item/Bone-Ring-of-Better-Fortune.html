Bone Ring of Better FortuneLevel 18 Uncommon

Formed from coated bones cleverly entwined, this tiny circlet fortifies your life force.

Ring Slot        85,000 gp

Property

Reduce by half the necrotic damage you take.

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when you are hit by an attack with the necrotic keyword. After applying any resistance, choose either to take no damage or to ignore an effect imposed by that attack, but not both.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you take no damage and ignore any effects imposed by that attack.
