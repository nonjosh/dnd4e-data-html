Fruit of LifeLevel 25 Uncommon

Vibrant green and veined in crimson, this fist-sized fruit subtly vibrates with barely contained life force.

Consumable: Consumable        25,000 gp

Power (Healing) Consumable (Minor Action)

You spend a healing surge and regain 15 additional hit points. Until you take an extended rest, you gain resist 15 necrotic and do not need to eat or drink.
