Cold iron shavings, 1 bag


Category: Gear
Price: 50 gp
Weight: 1 lb
Description: Fey creatures cannot abide contact with cold iron, and they will not touch any item placed in a bag filled with cold iron shavings. Used as part of the Magic Circle ritual, cold iron shavings can prevent fey creatures from crossing the perimeter of the circle. Each bag contains enough cold iron shavings for the creation of a single circle, and if used as a part of that ritual, lessens the penalty to the Arcana check from -10 to -5 to determine the level of creatures that cannot pass through the circle.

Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 133.
