Ring of FlightLevel 20 Uncommon

This delicate silver ring bears a feathery pattern.

Ring Slot        125,000 gp

Property

You take no damage from a fall and always land on your feet.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

Gain a fly speed equal to your speed until the end of your turn.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, this lasts until the end of your next turn instead.
