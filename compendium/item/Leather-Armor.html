Leather Armor


Description: Leather armor is sturdier than cloth armor. It protects vital areas with multiple layers of boiled-leather plates, while covering the limbs with supple leather that provides a small amount of protection.
    Magic appears at higher item levels, and grants higher armor bonuses than its mundane counterparts. Leather armor grants +3 AC at levels 16-25, and +4 AC at levels 26-30.
AC Bonus: +2
Weight: 15 lb.
Cost: 25 gp.
Type: Leather

Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 214, Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 325.
