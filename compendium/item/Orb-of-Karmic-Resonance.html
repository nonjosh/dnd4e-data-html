Orb of Karmic ResonanceLevel 13+ Uncommon

With this orb, you steal good luck from enemies and send them ill fortune.

Implement: Orb

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d6 damage per plus

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when an enemy succeeds on a saving throw. Choose one of the following effects:
    End an effect or condition currently affecting you or one ally within 5 squares of you.
    The enemy's saving throw fails instead of succeeding. Regardless of your choice, your next saving throw made in this encounter also fails.
