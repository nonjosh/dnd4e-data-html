Wand of ThunderLevel 13+ Rare

This brass wand, which resembles a miniaturized horn, will drive your enemies back with a wave of thunder, or drop them with a shock wave.

Lvl 13+317,000 gp Lvl 23+5425,000 gp
Lvl 18+485,000 gp Lvl 28+62,125,000 gp

Implement: Wand

Enhancement Bonus: Attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d8 thunder damage per plus

Property

Once during each of your turns, when you hit with a thunder attack using this wand, you can push one creature adjacent to you 1 square.

Attack Power Encounter (Standard Action)

Attack: Close blast 5 (creatures and objects in the blast); the wand’s level + 3 vs. Fortitude.

Hit: You push the target up to a number of squares equal to the wand’s enhancement bonus and knock it prone.

Attack Power (Thunder) Daily (No Action)

Trigger: You hit a target with a thunder attack using this wand.

Effect: Each creature adjacent to the target takes 10 thunder damage and falls prone.

Level 23 or 28: 20 thunder damage.
