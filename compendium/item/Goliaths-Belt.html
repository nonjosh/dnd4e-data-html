Goliath's BeltLevel 9 Common

This hide belt makes it easier for you to push people around.

Waist Slot        4,200 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus on Strength attacks to bull rush or grab a target. In addition, you can attempt to bull rush or grab a target up to two sizes larger than you.
