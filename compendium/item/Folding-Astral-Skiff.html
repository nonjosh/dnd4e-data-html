Folding Astral SkiffLevel 16 Uncommon

This small metallic cube expands into a full-sized astral skiff.

Wondrous Item        45,000 gp

Power Daily (Standard Action)

The cube unfolds into a full-sized astral skiff. The cube unfolds only if there is enough unoccupied space to contain the skiff.

Power (Standard Action)

The astral skiff collapses into a cube. You cannot use this power while creatures or objects (not including objects normally contained in the skiff ) occupy the astral skiff.



Published in Manual of the Planes, page(s) 158.
