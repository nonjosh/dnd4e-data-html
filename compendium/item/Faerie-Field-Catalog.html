From boggles to brownies, nixies to nymphs, this volume keenly details the fey, giving you access to obscure knowledge.

Wondrous Item        1,000 gp

Property

If you have training in Arcana, you gain a +2 item bonus to monster knowledge checks regarding fey creatures. If you do not have training in Arcana, you can make monster knowledge checks regarding fey creatures as if you had training (with the +5 bonus).



Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 137.
