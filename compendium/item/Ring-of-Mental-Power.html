Ring of Mental PowerLevel 14 Uncommon

This brushed silver band pulses with a catalyst for psionic minds, allowing you to expend energy when your reserves are gone.

Ring Slot        21,000 gp

Power Encounter (Free Action)

Trigger: You have no power points remaining.

Effect: You regain 1 power point.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You miss with an augmented psionic attack power.

Effect: You regain the power points you spent to augment the power. If you have reached at least one milestone today, you can instead reroll the attack roll and use either result.



Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 213.
