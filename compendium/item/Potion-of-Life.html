Potion of LifeLevel 30 Common

This fabled potion not only heals wounds but can even bring back the recently deceased.

Consumable: Potion        125,000 gp

Power (Healing) Consumable (Standard Action)

If this potion is administered to a character who died since the end of your last turn, that character is restored to life at 50 hit points.

If consumed by a living creature, this potion instead functions as a potion of recovery.



Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 255, Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 352, Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms, page(s) 352.
