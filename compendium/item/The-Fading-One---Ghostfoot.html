The Fading One - GhostfootLevel 3 Uncommon

You have begun to fade from this life, little by little. One day you will fade away completely, but the legend of your extraordinary power will endure.

Legendary Boon        680 gp

Property

When you make an Athletics check to jump, you gain a +5 bonus and are considered to have a running start.

Power Encounter (Free Action)

Trigger: You spend an action point.

Effect: You can fly your speed until the end of your turn.



Published in Dragon Magazine 389, page(s) 10.
