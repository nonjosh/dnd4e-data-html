Gauntlets of DiscontinuityLevel 13 Uncommon

Arcane energy crackles over these gauntlets, disrupting teleportation.

Hands Slot        17,000 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An enemy within 2 squares of you teleports.

Effect: You negate the teleport, and the triggering enemy can't teleport until the end of your next turn.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 58.
