Mrtok, Ogre Chief (Gauntlets of Ogre Power)Level 5 Rare

Crafted of thick plates of iron, these gauntlets are clearly very old and more than a little rusted. Designs are painted on them: a sadistic grin on one and beady eyes on the other.
These gauntlets chuckle quietly to themselves.

Hands Slot        0 gp

Property

Gain a +1 item bonus to Athletics checks and Strength ability checks (but not Strength attacks).

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you hit with a melee attack. Add a +5 power bonus to the damage roll.



Published in Dragon Magazine Annual, Dragon Magazine 367, page(s) 27.
