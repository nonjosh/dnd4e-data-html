Muddling MindLevel 15 Uncommon

You pierce an enemy's mind and manipulate its senses so it perceives what you want it to perceive.

Psionic Talent        25,000 gp

Property

While you are not bloodied, you gain a +4 item bonus to Stealth checks.

Power (Psionic) Encounter (Free Action)

Trigger: You hit an enemy with an unaugmented at-will attack power.

Effect: You become invisible to the target until you make an attack or until the start of your next turn.



Published in Dungeon Magazine 177, page(s) 86.
