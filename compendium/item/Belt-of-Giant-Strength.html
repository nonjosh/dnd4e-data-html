Belt of Giant StrengthLevel 15 Uncommon

This rugged leather belt is inset with numerous chunks of polished gray stone. It improves your athletic skills and melee damage.

Waist Slot        25,000 gp

Property

Gain a +2 item bonus to Athletics checks and Strength ability checks (but not Strength attacks).

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you hit with a melee attack. Add a +10 power bonus to the damage roll.
