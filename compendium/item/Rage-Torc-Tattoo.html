Rage Torc TattooLevel 12 Uncommon

Circling the neck like a spiral torc, this tattoo of spirit opens with a notch on one side for the magic to flow through and return.

Wondrous Item        13,000 gp

Property

When you spend an action point to take an extra action, you do not expend the action point if you use a rage attack and the attack misses all its targets.



Published in Dragon Magazine 380, page(s) 33.
