Skaivani's AnkletsLevel 10 Uncommon

These anklets of leather and copper let you surge across the battlefield.

Feet Slot        5,000 gp

Power Encounter (Free Action)

You gain a +2 power bonus to speed until the end of your next turn. While this bonus is in effect, when you make an Acrobatics check or Athletics check and roll 5 or lower, you can reroll the check.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 135.
