Dragondaunt ShieldLevel 16+ Uncommon

This ornate shield provides extra protection against dragon attacks and can be activated to reduce the damage of an area attack.

Arms Slot: Any shield

Property

Gain resist 5 to all attacks of dragons.
    Level 26: Resist 10

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

You can use this power when you are hit by an area or a close attack. Reduce the damage dealt by the attack to you and each adjacent ally by 10.

Level 26: Reduce the damage dealt by 15.
