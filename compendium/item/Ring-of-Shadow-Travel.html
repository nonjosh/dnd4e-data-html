Ring of Shadow TravelLevel 15 Uncommon

This dark iron ring lets you disappear into the shadows.

Ring Slot        25,000 gp

Property

Gain a +2 item bonus to Stealth checks.

Power (Teleportation) Daily (Move Action)

Teleport 4 squares. If the space you teleport from is not brightly lit, you can teleport 8 squares. You cannot teleport to a brightly lit space. If you've reached at least one milestone today, double all teleport distances of this power.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 159.
