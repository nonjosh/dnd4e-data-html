Climbing ClawsLevel 4 Uncommon

Sharp claws magically unfold from the palms of these padded leather gloves.

Hands Slot        840 gp

Property

Gain a +1 item bonus to Athletics checks to climb. Each of these gloves can also be used as a one-handed, off-hand, simple, light blade that applies a +2 proficiency bonus to attack rolls and deals 1d4 damage. The wearer gains proficiency with this weapon.
