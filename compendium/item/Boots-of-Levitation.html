Boots of LevitationLevel 9 Uncommon

You rise into the air thanks to these soft leather boots, able to see and be seen across a broader area.

Feet Slot        4,200 gp

Utility Power Encounter (Move Action)

Effect: You fly up to 4 squares vertically and 1 square horizontally, and you can hover there until the end of your next turn. When the levitation ends, you descend to the ground without taking falling damage.



Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 63.
