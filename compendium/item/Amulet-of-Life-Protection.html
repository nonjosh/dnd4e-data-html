Amulet of Life ProtectionLevel 2+ Common

Each time you take damage to body or mind, this amulet pulses warmly, reminding you of the protection it holds for you, even in death.

Lvl 2+1520 gp Lvl 17+465,000 gp
Lvl 7+22,600 gp Lvl 22+5325,000 gp
Lvl 12+313,000 gp Lvl 27+61,625,000 gp

Neck Slot

Enhancement Bonus: Fortitude, Reflex, and Will

Property

If you die while wearing the amulet and it is left on your body, the time that your corpse can lie dead and still be revived by a power or a ritual is multiplied by 10. In addition, you cannot be raised as an undead creature. If the amulet is removed from your corpse, these benefits end. Placing the amulet on an already dead body has no effect.



Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 71.
