Helm of the FlamewarpedLevel 10 Uncommon

The one who wears this slightly charred copper helm can strike a devastating blow, but at a cost.

Head Slot        5,000 gp

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you make a melee basic attack or use an at-will melee attack power. You are dazed until the end of your next turn. If your attack hits, the power's damage roll deals maximum damage, and you can choose to make it fire damage.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 143.
