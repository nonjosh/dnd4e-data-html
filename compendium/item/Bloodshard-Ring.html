Bloodshard RingLevel 26 Uncommon

The images of eladrin nobles flicker within this circular shard of mirrored glass, which injures you even as it extends your vitality.

Ring Slot        1,125,000 gp

Property

You reduce your maximum hit points by 5. In addition, whenever you spend a healing surge, you regain 2 additional hit points.

Power (Healing) Daily (Minor Action)

Regain hit points as if you had spent a healing surge. If you've reached at least one milestone today, you also regain a spent healing surge.
