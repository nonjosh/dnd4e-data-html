Ring of Many FormsLevel 15 Uncommon

This ring appears as a band of shifting energy, sharpening your combat prowess when you wield the magic of transformation.

Ring Slot        25,000 gp

Property

When you use a polymorph power, you gain a +1 bonus to your next attack roll before the end of your turn.
