Teamstrike TattooLevel 11 Uncommon

These tattoos frequently show wolves acting as a pack.

Wondrous Item        9,000 gp

Property

When you spend an action point to make an attack, each ally you can see who also has a teamstrike tattoo gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls against the first target of your attack until the end of your next turn.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 86.
