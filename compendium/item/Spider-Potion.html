Spider PotionLevel 20 Uncommon

This pungent-smelling, sepia-colored concoction grants a spider's resilience against damage and poison.

Consumable: Potion        5,000 gp

Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Drink this potion and spend a healing surge. You do not regain hit points as normal. Instead, gain 20 temporary hit points and resist 10 poison until the end of the encounter.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 189.
