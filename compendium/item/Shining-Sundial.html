Shining SundialLevel 10 Uncommon

This gem-studded silver sundial reflects light brilliantly, amplifying radiant effects.

Wondrous Item        5,000 gp

Property

This sundial must be placed in a location where it receives natural light while the sun is up. When you use a radiant attack power, until the end of your next turn, creatures within 10 squares of the sundial gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and a +3 bonus to damage rolls.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 82.
