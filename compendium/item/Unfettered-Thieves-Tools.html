Unfettered Thieves' ToolsLevel 11 Uncommon

The picks, keys, and tongs of this set of thieves’ tools appear ordinary—until they rise into the air to move under your mental command.

Wondrous Item        9,000 gp

Property

Like a normal set of thieves’ tools, unfettered thieves’ tools grant a +2 bonus to Thievery checks to open locks or disable traps.

Utility Power Daily (Standard Action)

Effect: You make a Thievery check on an object you can see within 5 squares of you.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 178, Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 89.
