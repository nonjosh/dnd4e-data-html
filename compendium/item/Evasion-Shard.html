Evasion ShardLevel 20 Uncommon

This amber crystal warms quickly before drifting slowly from your grip to float overhead.

Head Slot        125,000 gp

Utility Power (Teleportation) Encounter (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An attack hits you.

Effect: Roll a d20. On a result of 6–19, you teleport up to 5 squares. On a result of 20, you teleport up to 5 squares, and this power is not expended.

Malfunction: If you roll a 1–5, you take 15 damage and fall prone.



Published in Dungeon Magazine 201.
