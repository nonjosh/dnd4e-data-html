Winged BootsLevel 13 Rare

These enchanted boots protect you from falling damage and can be activated to allow you to fly.

Feet Slot        17,000 gp

Property

You take no damage from a fall and always land on your feet.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You gain a fly speed equal to your speed until the end of the encounter.
