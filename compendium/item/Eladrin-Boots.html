Eladrin BootsLevel 16 Uncommon

These enchanted boots, crafted in the eladrin tradition, increase your teleport distance.

Feet Slot        45,000 gp

Property

Add 2 to the maximum range of any teleport you make (other than that provided by these boots).

Power (Teleportation) Daily (Move Action)

Teleport up to 5 squares (or up to 10 if you're an eladrin).



Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 246.
