Know the PathLevel 5 Uncommon

You can sense the way that you must go as clearly as if a tended path lay before you.

Fey Magic Gift        1,000 gp

Utility Power Daily (Minor Action)

Effect: You utter the name of a specific location you have seen before that is on the same plane as you. Until you take an extended rest, you can sense the general direction to that location and the distance to it.



Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 142.
