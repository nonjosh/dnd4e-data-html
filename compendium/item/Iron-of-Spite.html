Iron of SpiteLevel 27 Uncommon

A spiked metal sphere, slightly smaller than your fist, hovers near your shoulder and gives off black sparks. It greatly enhances your arcana and intimidate skills.

Head Slot        1,625,000 gp

Properties

  • Gain a +6 item bonus to Arcana checks and Intimidate checks.
  • Any enemy that hits you with a melee attack takes 1d10 necrotic damage.


Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 249.
