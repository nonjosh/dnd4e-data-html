Lantern, Bull’s-Eye


Category: Gear
Price: 12 gp
Weight: 2 lb
Description: A bull’s-eye lantern is equipped with a lens that enables it to project a beam of bright light 2 squares wide out to 10 squares. This way, you can illuminate long hallways without lighting up the whole area.

Published in Into the Unknown: The Dungeon Survival Handbook, page(s) 115.
