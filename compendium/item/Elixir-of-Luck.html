Elixir of LuckLevel 10 Rare

The red-gold liquid contained within this vial shines with its own dim light. When you drink it, you feel as though nothing could go wrong.

Consumable: Elixir        200 gp

Utility Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Effect: You drink the elixir and lose an action point. Once before the end of the encounter, you can reroll a failed skill check, attack roll, or saving throw. You must use the second result.



Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 93.
