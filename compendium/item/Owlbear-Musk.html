Owlbear Musk


Category: Gear
Price: 500 gp
Weight: 1 lb
Description: Some brave hunters shadow owlbears in heat to track down the males. Centaurs are practiced in luring hormone-crazed owlbears into traps, and they can extract musk glands from a corpse in seconds.
    A single whiff of owlbear musk is strong enough to bring an enchanted creature crashing back to reality, immediately ending any dazing, stunning, or dominating magical effects. Each pouch is good for three doses, after which its potency fades away.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 211.
