Soulfang of RageLevel 10 Uncommon

While this fang bites at your flesh, you rage without end.

Consumable: Soulfang        200 gp

Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Until you remove this soulfang, you are considered to be raging, and you take damage equal to half your healing surge value at the start of your turn. Damage caused by this soulfang cannot be reduced by any means.



Published in Dragon Magazine 378.
