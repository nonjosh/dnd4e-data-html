Song of DestructionLevel 25 Uncommon

The cacophonous power of this song brings destruction to those who hear it. In the wake of its discordant melody, disaster follows.

Grandmaster Training        625,000 gp

Property

You gain a +10 item bonus to damage rolls against objects.

Utility Power Encounter (Minor Action)

Effect: You shatter a nonmagical object made of crystal, glass, ceramic, porcelain, or other fragile material adjacent to you. The object can weigh no more than 10 pounds.

Attack Power (Thunder) Daily (Standard Action)

Attack: Close burst 3 (enemies in the burst); +28 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + 10 thunder damage, and the target falls prone. The target is also weakened (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and the target is weakened until the end of your next turn.



Published in Dragon Magazine 415.
