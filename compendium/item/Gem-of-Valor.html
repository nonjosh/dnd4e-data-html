Gem of ValorLevel 20 Uncommon

This brilliant sapphire promises great rewards to the bold.

Consumable: Consumable        5,000 gp

Power Consumable (Free Action)

Use this power when you spend an action point. Roll 1d20 to determine the result, adding 1 to the result for each milestone you've reached today. Regardless of the result, using the gem consumes it and turns it to dust.

1-9: Gain a +1 power bonus to all defenses until the end of your next turn.

10-19: Gain a +1 power bonus to all attack rolls until the end of your next turn.

20: You gain an extra standard action that you must spend before the end of your next turn.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 191.
