Shroud of RevivalLevel 7 Uncommon

This white gossamer shroud grants protection and bodily strength to a dying ally.

Wondrous Item        2,600 gp

Power Daily (Standard Action)

When you place the shroud of revival over an adjacent dying creature, that creature gains resist 20 to all damage and a +2 power bonus to death saving throws. This effect lasts until the creature regains consciousness or dies, or until the shroud is removed (a standard action), whichever comes first.
