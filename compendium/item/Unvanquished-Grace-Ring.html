Unvanquished Grace RingLevel 14 Uncommon

The distinctive gold and silver weave of this grace ring is carved with feathers.

Ring Slot        21,000 gp

Property

You gain a +1 item bonus to saving throws against dazing or stunning effects.

Power Encounter (No Action)

Trigger: You are conscious and end your turn without having made an attack during it.

Effect: You or an ally within 5 squares of you can stand up as a free action.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 74.
