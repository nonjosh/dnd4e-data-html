Pike


Military two-handed melee weapon
Cost: 15 gp
Damage: 1d10
Proficient: +2
Range: -
Weight: 6 lb.

The pike is similar to a longspear, except that its superior construction and balance make it usable by gnomes, halflings, and other Small folk.
