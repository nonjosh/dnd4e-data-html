Dragon Claw Life-Thief StrikeLevel 6 Uncommon

Your claws cut deep, as you drink life from those around you.

Grandmaster Training        3,400 gp

Utility Power (Healing) Encounter (Move Action)

Effect: You shift up to 2 squares. If your next melee weapon attack this turn hits, you regain 5 hit points. You can instead regain 10 hit points if a willing ally within 5 squares of you takes 5 damage when you hit. (This damage cannot be prevented in any way.)
