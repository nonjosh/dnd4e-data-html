Ring of Perfect GripLevel 14 Uncommon

This rough alloy band gives you a grip of steel when you are about to fall.

Ring Slot        21,000 gp

Property

Gain a +5 item bonus to saving throws to catch yourself from falling.

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when you would be forced over a precipice or into a pit. You automatically succeed on the saving throw to catch yourself from falling.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you do not fall prone when you catch yourself from falling.
