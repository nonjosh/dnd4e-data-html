Winged BracersLevel 12 Uncommon

With these ornate bracers on your arms, you can glide safely down from great heights.

Arms Slot        13,000 gp

Property

When you leap down at least 4 squares (rather than fall accidentally), you can fly 8 squares at any point before you hit the ground. This flight cannot take you upward. If you fall for more than 1 round, you can fly 8 each round as a free action. You do not take damage from this fall.



Published in Dragon Magazine 369, page(s) 14.
