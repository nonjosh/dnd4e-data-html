Waistband of the GrapplerLevel 13 Uncommon

This fur-lined wrap channels the strength and the recklessness of an accomplished wrestler.

Waist Slot        17,000 gp

Power (Minor Action)

You gain a +5 power bonus to damage rolls and to grab attempts, but take a -2 penalty to attack rolls. You also grant combat advantage to all enemies. This effect lasts until you end it as a minor action.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 75.
