GuenhwyvarLevel 21 Uncommon

This onyx figurine is a ferocious panther.

Wondrous Item        225,000 gp

Property

Unique; this item cannot be purchased or created with the Enchant Magic Item ritual.

Power (Conjuration) Daily (Standard Action)

Use this figurine to conjure Guenhwyvar. As a free action, you can spend a healing surge when activating this item to give the creature temporary hit points equal to your healing surge value.
