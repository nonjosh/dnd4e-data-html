Poison GlovesLevel 4 Uncommon

You can store a nasty surprise in these plain-looking gloves to envenom a weapon at a moment's notice.

Hands Slot        840 gp

Power (Standard Action)

You store one dose of poison that can be applied to a weapon in these gloves. The gloves can hold only one dose of poison at a time.

Power (Minor Action)

You apply the stored poison to a weapon you're holding; doing so consumes that dose of poison.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 61.
