Immurement of Baleful GossamerLevel 28 Rare

A tree surrounded by cobwebs is visible within this soft, clear sphere.

Consumable        85,000 gp

Power Consumable (Standard Action)

You expend this immurement and change the terrain in a close blast 8 until the end of the encounter. Replace the terrain in the blast with the following terrain and effects.

*: Squares filled with webs are difficult terrain.

*: The tree in the center of the area is difficult terrain that costs 2 extra squares to enter and provides cover.

*: When a creature starts its turn in a web square, you make an attack against that creature: +27 vs. Reflex; on a hit, the target is immobilized until the start of its next turn. On a miss, the target is slowed until the start of its next turn.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 89.
