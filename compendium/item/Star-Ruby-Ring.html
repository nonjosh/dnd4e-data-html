Star Ruby RingLevel 19 Uncommon

This handsome ruby ring is favored by courtiers who rise and fall by the favor they earn with their honeyed words.

Ring Slot        105,000 gp

Property

Gain a +2 item bonus to Diplomacy checks.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when making a Charisma-based skill check during a skill challenge. You can roll twice and take the better result.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, a successful roll on the skill challenge counts for two victories.
