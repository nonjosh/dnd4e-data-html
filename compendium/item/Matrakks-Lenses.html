Matrakk's LensesLevel 11 Uncommon

Lenses of perfect crystal hone your vision and protect you from attacks that target your sight.

Head Slot        9,000 gp

Properties

  • You gain an item bonus to Perception checks equal to the number of allies who wear or wield one or more items from this set.
  • You gain a +1 bonus to all defenses against gaze attacks and a +5 bonus to saving throws against effects that cause the blinded condition.


Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 134.
