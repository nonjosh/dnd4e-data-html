Vortex MaskLevel 12 Uncommon

This eerie mask has a mouth from which no light escapes, and it draws foes toward that darkness in a great inhalation.

Head Slot        13,000 gp

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You pull an enemy within 5 squares of you 2 squares (no attack roll). If you have the enemy marked, you pull the enemy 4 squares.



Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 97.
