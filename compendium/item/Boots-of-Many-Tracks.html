Boots of Many TracksLevel 9 Uncommon

These rugged boots conceal your steps.

Feet Slot        4,200 gp

Property

The DC of any Perception check to find your tracks is increased by 10. Even if your tracks are found, identifying them takes a Nature check against the same DC. If the Nature check fails, the tracks seem to be of some animal (chosen by you at the time you made the tracks).



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 126.
