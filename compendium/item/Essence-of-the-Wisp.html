Essence of the WispLevel 21 Uncommon

A glowing ball of light buzzes around your head, drawing in foes that attack you from a distance.

Head Slot        225,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Will.

Power (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: An enemy hits you with a ranged attack.

Effect: You pull the triggering enemy 2 squares.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 61.
