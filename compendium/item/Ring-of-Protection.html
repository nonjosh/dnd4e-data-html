Ring of ProtectionLevel 17 Rare

This simple ring of white gold is engraved with a tiny shield.

Ring Slot        65,000 gp

Property

Gain a +1 item bonus to saving throws.

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: You are hit by an enemy's attack.

Effect: You gain a +2 power bonus to a single defense score against the attack.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, this bonus lasts until the start of your next turn instead.



Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 252, Dungeon Master's Kit, page(s) 267.
