Moran's EyeLevel 14 Uncommon

This sphere of translucent orange crystal is 6 inches in diameter and warm to the touch. One side of the orb features a gold-flecked disk split by a vertical black slash, like a cat's eye.

Lvl 14+321,000 gp

Implement: Orb

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d6 damage per plus

Property

You gain a +5 bonus to Arcana checks as long as you have the Eye in your possession.

Power (Arcane, Implement) Daily

Smoldering Eye. Standard Action. Close blast 5; Intelligence vs. Reflex; target takes ongoing 10 fire damage and a -2 penalty to attack rolls (save ends both).

Power (Arcane, Implement) Encounter

Baleful Eye. Minor Action. Ranged 10; Intelligence vs. Will; the target is slowed and cannot teleport (save ends both). The effect ends if the wielder uses baleful eye on another creature. Ritual of Retrieval: The wielder of Moran's Eye can perform a unique ritual.
