Chirurgeon's tools


Category: Gear
Price: 20 gp
Weight: 4 lb
Description: This surgical gear includes brass wedges that can be heated to cauterize wounds and jars of leeches for bleeding patients. When you use these tools to treat an ally who has contracted a disease caused by an attack from a creature, that ally gains a +2 bonus to the next check he or she makes against the disease.

Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 124.
