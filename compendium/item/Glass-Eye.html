Glass EyeLevel 16 Uncommon

Conceal this glass replica of a bloodshot eye so you can scry the location later.

Wondrous Item        45,000 gp

Power (Minor Action)

You prime the glass eye and place it somewhere. Until another creature touches the eye or until you end this effect as a free action, you can choose to create your scrying sensor in the glass eye's square with the Wizard's Sight ritual even when the eye is as far away as 100 squares. You can also scry the eye's location using View Location even if the eye's location is not a location you have visited before or it is not fixed in place.



Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 28.
