Silver Hands of PowerLevel 14+ Uncommon

The pool of Ralyar glows with arcane power. When the wizard Fenstern dips his hands into the pool, he shrieks in pain. In exchange for this power, Fenstern accepts the momentary agony of the pool transforming his hands into living quicksilver.
