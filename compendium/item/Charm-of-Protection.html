Charm of ProtectionLevel 8 Uncommon

Your familiar is protected by the charm almost as if it has a destiny of its own.

Familiar Slot        3,400 gp

Power Encounter (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An attack hits your familiar.

Effect: Your familiar gains a +4 power bonus to AC and Reflex until the end of your next turn.



Published in Dragon Magazine 382, page(s) 28.
