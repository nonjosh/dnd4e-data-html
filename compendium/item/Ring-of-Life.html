Ring of LifeLevel 26 Uncommon

A band of warm, green wood, this bark-covered ring infuses you with vitality.

Ring Slot        1,125,000 gp

Property

You do not need to eat or drink. In addition, you gain a +5 item bonus to saving throws against necrotic effects.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You take necrotic damage.

Effect: You reduce the necrotic damage to 0.



Published in Dark Sun Campaign Setting, page(s) 129.
