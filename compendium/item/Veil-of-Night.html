Veil of NightLevel 18 Rare

This diaphanous cloth seems made of shadows and twinkling stars. With it you can vanish from sight.

Head Slot        85,000 gp

Property

Gain a +4 item bonus to Stealth checks.

Power (Illusion) Daily (Move Action)

You and an adjacent ally become invisible until either of you leaves the squares you occupy or until the end of the encounter.

Power (Illusion) Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An enemy attacks you or an ally adjacent to you.

Effect: The target of the triggering attack becomes invisible until the end of your next turn.



Published in Dragon Magazine 394.
