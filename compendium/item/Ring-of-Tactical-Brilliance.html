Ring of Tactical BrillianceLevel 16 Uncommon

The power of this golden ring helps protect an ally who changes position at your command.

Ring Slot        45,000 gp

Property

When you use a warlord power that lets an ally shift, that ally gains a +1 bonus to all defenses until the end of his or her next turn.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 123.
