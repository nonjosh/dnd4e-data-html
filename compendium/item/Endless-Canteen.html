Endless CanteenLevel 9 Common

This mundane-looking canteen pours forth water in a seemingly limitless stream.

Wondrous Item        4,200 gp

Power (Standard Action)

When you open its stopper, the endless canteen pours out up to 1 pint of cool, clean water. Any water that has not been consumed within 1 hour of its creation disappears.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 171.
