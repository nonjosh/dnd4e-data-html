Ring of CallingLevel 14 Uncommon

This mithral ring brings you and your allies closer together in times of need.

Ring Slot        21,000 gp

Property

When an ally adjacent to you uses a teleport power, he or she can increase the distance teleported by 4 squares.

Power (Teleportation) Daily (Minor Action)

Choose one ally within 20 squares of you and within line of sight. That ally is teleported to any unoccupied square adjacent to you.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you can instead teleport to a square adjacent to an ally within 20 squares of you.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 158.
