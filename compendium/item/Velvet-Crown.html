Velvet CrownLevel 8 Rare

This velvet band might be worn about the leg as a garter, but its powers of stealth and alacrity only come into play when the crown is worn about the head.

Head Slot        3,400 gp

Properties

  • You gain a +2 item bonus to Stealth checks, escape attempts, and saving throws against effects that render you immobilized, restrained, or slowed.
  • When you fall 30 feet or less, you take no damage and land on your feet.

Utility Power (Zone) Daily (Standard Action)

Effect: Close burst 3. The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of your next turn and moves with you. No sound can be made in the zone, and no noise from outside the zone can be heard in it or through it.

Sustain Minor: You sustain the effect until the end of your next turn.



Published in Halls of Undermountain, page(s) 51.
