Belt of Titan StrengthLevel 25 Uncommon

This rugged leather belt is inset with numerous chunks of polished obsidian. It greatly improves your athletic skills and melee damage.

Waist Slot        625,000 gp

Property

Gain a +3 item bonus to Athletics checks and Strength ability checks (but not Strength attacks).

Power Daily (Free Action)

Gain a +10 power bonus to all melee damage rolls until the start of your next turn.
