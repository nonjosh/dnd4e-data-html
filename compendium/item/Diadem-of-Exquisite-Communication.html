Diadem of Exquisite CommunicationLevel 13 Uncommon

This diadem makes you a conduit for unspoken thoughts and lets you take the advantage when an enemy attacks your strength of mind.

Head Slot        17,000 gp

Property

While you have at least 1 power point, you have telepathy 5.

Power (Augmentable) Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: An enemy attack targets your Will.

Effect: The triggering enemy grants you combat advantage until the end of your next turn.

Augment 1: (Psychic): The enemy also takes psychic damage equal to your Constitution, Intelligence, or Charisma modifier (whichever is highest).



Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 158.
