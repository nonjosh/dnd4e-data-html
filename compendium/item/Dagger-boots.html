Dagger boots


Category: Gear
Price: 2 gp
Weight: 4 lb
Description: These spike-toed footwear help you kick notches into a surface you are climbing. While wearing dagger boots, you gain +2 bonus to Athletics checks to climb but take a -2 penalty to Stealth checks to move silently.

Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 124.
