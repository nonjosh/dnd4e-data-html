Hood of the WolfLevel 20 Uncommon

Fashioned from the head of a great gray wolf, this helm frames the wearer's face in the open maw of the beast, and it crowns them with its red eyes.

Head Slot        125,000 gp

Property

Gain darkvision and a +4 item bonus to Insight, Perception, and Intimidate checks.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

Choose an enemy. Until the end of the encounter, that enemy does not benefit from concealment, total concealment, cover, superior cover, or invisibility against you.



Published in Dragon Magazine 364, page(s) 52.
