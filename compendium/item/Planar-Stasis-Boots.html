Planar Stasis BootsLevel 18 Uncommon

These supple boots make you feel confident of your ability to stay in one place when confronting magical attacks that displace you.

Feet Slot        85,000 gp

Property

You cannot be teleported against your will.

Power Encounter (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when you would be pulled, pushed, or slid. You are moved 2 fewer squares.



Published in Manual of the Planes, page(s) 156.
