Jester ShoesLevel 2 Uncommon

These colorful, pointed shoes help you stand out—but not stand up—in a crowd.

Feet Slot        520 gp

Power Encounter (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when you are pushed, pulled, or slid. Reduce the distance you are pulled, pushed, or slided by 1 square and fall prone.
