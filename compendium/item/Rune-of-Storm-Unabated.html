Rune of Storm UnabatedLevel 16 Rare

Sparks fly from the rune when you access its power and thunder growls in your footsteps.

Lost Rune        45,000 gp

Property

Whenever you would enter a new rune state, you can enter the rune state of storm unabated. While in this state, your runepriest at-will attack powers deal 2d6 extra lightning and thunder damage.

Power (Lightning, Thunder) Daily (Standard Action)

Attack: Area burst 1 within 5 (creatures in the burst); +19 vs. Reflex.

Hit: 10 lightning and thunder damage, and the target is dazed until the end of your next turn.
