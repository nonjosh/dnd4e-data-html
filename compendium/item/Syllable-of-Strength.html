Syllable of StrengthLevel 10 Uncommon

Aesh burns brightly, filling you with uncommon power and strength.

Consumable: Consumable        200 gp

Power Consumable (Standard Action)

Requirement: You must be able to speak Abyssal or Supernal.

Effect: You speak the syllable and it orbits your head. You gain a +6 power bonus to damage rolls until the end of the encounter.



Published in Dragon Magazine 392.
