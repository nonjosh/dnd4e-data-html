Cyclops HelmLevel 18 Uncommon

The gemlike eye that stares from the brow of this finely wrought mithral helm reveals what is hidden.

Head Slot        85,000 gp

Property

You gain a +4 item bonus to Perception checks.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You can see invisible creatures until the end of your next turn. Also, choose one invisible creature that you can see. That creature becomes visible and can't become invisible again until the end of your next turn.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 61.
