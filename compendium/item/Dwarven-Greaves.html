Dwarven GreavesLevel 7 Uncommon

This enchanted leg armor, crafted in the dwarven tradition, can be activated to negate a pull, a push, or a slide effect.

Feet Slot        2,600 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

You can use this power when you are hit by a power that has a pull, a push, or a slide effect. You negate the forced movement.
