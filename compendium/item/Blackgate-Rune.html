Blackgate RuneLevel 12 Uncommon

This glossy black stone bears a complex sigil etched on its surface.

Consumable: Consumable        520 gp

Power (Teleportation) Consumable (Standard Action)

You open a portal between corresponding places in the Shadowfell and the world. The portal lasts for 3 rounds, or until you wish it to close (free action), whichever is less time.
