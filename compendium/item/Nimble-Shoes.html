Nimble ShoesLevel 12 Uncommon

With these supple shoes on your feet, you leap and climb with ease.

Feet Slot        13,000 gp

Property

When you would make an Athletics check, you can make an Acrobatics check instead.

Power (Augmentable) Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: An attack knocks you prone.

Effect: You stand up.

Augment 1: You cannot be knocked prone and you can ignore forced movement until the end of your next turn.
