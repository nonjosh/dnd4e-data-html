Steadfast SaddleLevel 8 Uncommon

This saddle keeps you mounted when faced with effects that might normally knock you off your mount.

Mount        3,400 gp

Power Encounter (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when the mount you are riding would be pulled, pushed, or slid. The mount is not pulled, pushed, or slid.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 124.
