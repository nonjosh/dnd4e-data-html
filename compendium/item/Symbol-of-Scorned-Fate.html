Symbol of Scorned FateLevel 5+ Uncommon

This symbol, shaped like a double-headed coin, lets you elude fate for a split second.

Lvl 5+11,000 gp Lvl 20+4125,000 gp
Lvl 10+25,000 gp Lvl 25+5625,000 gp
Lvl 15+325,000 gp Lvl 30+63,125,000 gp

Implement: Holy symbol

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d8 radiant damage per plus

Power Encounter (Free Action)

Trigger: You fail your first saving throw against an effect.

Effect: The effect against which you failed your saving throw does not affect you until the end of your next turn. If the effect changes or has an additional effect after you fail your first saving throw, the triggering saving throw does not count as the first failed saving throw.
