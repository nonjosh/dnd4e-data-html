Vanous's Menacing BeastLevel 9 Uncommon

Seeing you imperiled prompts your beast companion to strike.

Grandmaster Training        4,200 gp

Requirement: You must have the beast mastery or beast mastery (hybrid) class feature.

Property

While you are within 5 squares of your beast companion, you gain a +2 item bonus to Intimidate checks.

Power (Beast) Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Requirement: You must be adjacent to your beast companion.

Trigger: An enemy adjacent to you targets you with a melee attack.

Effect: Slide your beast companion up to 2 squares into a square adjacent to the triggering enemy. Your beast companion then makes a melee basic attack against that enemy.



Published in Dragon Magazine 393.
