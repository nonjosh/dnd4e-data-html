Stewmatic


Category: Gear
Price: 50 gp
Description: The stewmatic is a labor-saving invention intended to prepare savory, hot meals from raw materials with a minimum of effort and without a fire. The user adds raw grains, meats, vegetables, and water to the stewmatic. An hour later, the machine delivers a steaming, tasteless—but nutritious and edible—glop into waiting bowls. The stewmatic feeds the same number of people that the raw materials would have.

Published in Dragon Magazine 422.
