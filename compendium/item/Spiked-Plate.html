Spiked Plate


Description: A dwarven innovation, spiked plate is a suit of plate mail studded with sharp, metal spikes. The dwarves developed this armor to deal with subterranean beasts such as hook horrors and umber hulks that grasp and crush their prey.
    Magic appears at higher item levels, and grants higher armor bonuses than its mundane counterparts. Spiked plate grants +9 AC at levels 6-10, +10 AC at levels 11-15, +11 AC at levels 16-20, +12 AC at levels 21-25, and +14 AC at levels 26-30.
AC Bonus: +8
Check: -3
Speed: -1
Weight: 60 lb.
Cost: 55 gp.
Special: Barbed
Type: Plate

Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 8.
