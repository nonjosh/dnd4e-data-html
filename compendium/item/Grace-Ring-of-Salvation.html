Grace Ring of SalvationLevel 14 Uncommon

When hope has fled, this ring's pulsing blue sapphire might recapture it.

Ring Slot        21,000 gp

Property

You gain a +1 item bonus to saving throws against dazing or stunning effects.

Power Encounter (No Action)

Trigger: You are conscious and end your turn without having made an attack during it.

Effect: You gain a +5 power bonus to your next saving throw before the end of the encounter.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 70.
