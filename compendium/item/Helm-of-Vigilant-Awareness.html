Helm of Vigilant AwarenessLevel 7 Uncommon

The gems mounted above the ears and eyeholes of this steel helmet flash red when a deafening noise or blinding light appears.

Head Slot        2,600 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when an attack would make you blinded or deafened. The blinded or deafened condition from that attack does not affect you
