Sturdy Mount (mount)

The dreadmount can move 1 square fewer than an effect specifies when subjected to a pull, a push, or a slide. When an attack would knock it or its rider prone, the dreadmount can make a saving throw to prevent it or its rider from falling prone.

Attack (Immediate Interrupt): Melee 1 (triggering enemy); +8 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d6 + 6 damage, and the target falls prone.
