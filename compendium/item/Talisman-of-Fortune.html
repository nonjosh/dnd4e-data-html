Talisman of FortuneLevel 15 Uncommon

This small gold talisman renews a magic item or strengthens your health, attacks, and defenses in turn.

Wondrous Item        25,000 gp

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You must spend an action point to use this power. When you do, roll 1d20 and add +1 to the result for each milestone you've reached today. The total result determines the power's effect(s):

1-9: 1-9: Lose 1 healing surge, or take damage equal to onequarter of your maximum hit point total if you have no healing surges remaining. Gain one extra daily use of a magic item in your possession (your choice, not including the talisman of fortune) until the end of the encounter. You also renew the power of the talisman.

10-19: 10-19: Recover one expended healing surge and gain a +1 power bonus to attack rolls and all defenses until the end of your next turn. 20 or higher: As 10-19, and you renew the power of the talisman.
