Rod of Shadow's CaressLevel 15+ Uncommon

This rod summons tentacles of shadowy energy to impede your foes.

Lvl 15+325,000 gp Lvl 25+5625,000 gp
Lvl 20+4125,000 gp Lvl 30+63,125,000 gp

Implement: Rod

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d6 necrotic damage per plus

Power (Conjuration, Zone) Daily (Standard Action)

You evoke the essence of the Shadowfell in an area burst 1 within 10 squares. The burst creates a zone of grasping tentacles. Any enemy that starts its turn within the zone is slowed until the start of its next turn. In the Shadowfell, the power has an area burst 2 within 10 squares. A minor action sustains the zone.
