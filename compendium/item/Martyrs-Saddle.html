Martyr's SaddleLevel 6 Uncommon

Those who seek to protect their mounts at any cost favor this saddle.

Mount        1,800 gp

Property

The mount gains a +1 item bonus to all defenses.

Power (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when an attack would damage the mount you are riding. The mount takes half damage from the attack and you take the remainder. Nothing can reduce or prevent the damage that a rider takes in this way.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 124.
