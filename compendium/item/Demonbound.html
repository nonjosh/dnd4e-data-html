Power (Summoning) Daily (Minor Action)

You summon a Medium demon in an unoccupied square within 10 squares of you. The demon has a fly speed of 6 (hover). It has a +2 bonus to AC and a +2 bonus to Fortitude. You can give the demon the following special commands.

*: Standard Action: Melee 1 (targets one creature); your highest ability +4 vs. AC; 1d8 + your highest ability modifier damage.

*: Instinctive Effect: If you haven't given the demon any commands by the end of your turn, it attacks a bloodied enemy if it can. Otherwise, it moves its speed to a square adjacent to the nearest enemy. If it can do neither of these and a bloodied ally is within its melee reach or charge distance, it attacks that ally instead.
