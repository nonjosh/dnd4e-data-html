Sudden Escape ArmorLevel 28 Uncommon

This shimmering armor allows you to invoke astral energy to spirit yourself away from an attack.

Lvl 28+62,125,000 gp

Enhancement Bonus: AC

Power (Teleportation) Daily (Immediate Interrupt)
