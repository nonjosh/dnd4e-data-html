Skull HelmLevel 12 Uncommon

The face of this iron helm resembles a screaming skull.

Head Slot        13,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Intimidate and Endurance checks.

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

You can use this power when an enemy makes an attack against your Will defense. Gain a +2 item bonus to Will until the end of your next turn. If the attack targeting you has the fear keyword, gain a +4 item bonus instead.



Published in Dragon Magazine Annual.
