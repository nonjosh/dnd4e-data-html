Charm of Abundant ActionLevel 28 Uncommon

This plain metal charm allows you to outrun or outfight your foes.

Wondrous Item        2,125,000 gp

Power Encounter (Free Action)

Use this power on your turn to spend an action point (assuming you have one available). You can spend the action point even if you spent an action point earlier in the encounter. You must be holding the charm when you spend the second action point.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 169.
