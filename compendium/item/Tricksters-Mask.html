Trickster's MaskLevel 20 Uncommon

This velvet masquerade mask is highly prized among the more shadowy worshipers of Avandra.

Head Slot        125,000 gp

Property

When you make a Stealth or Thievery check, roll twice and take the better result.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power before you make a Stealth or Thievery check. Treat that check as though you rolled a natural 20.
