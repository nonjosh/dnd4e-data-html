Ring of GiantsLevel 13 Uncommon

Your limbs are infused with the strength of a creature much larger than yourself.

Ring Slot        17,000 gp

Property

Gain a +2 bonus to critical hit damage per enhancement bonus of the weapon you wield.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you hit with a primal attack power. In addition to the power's normal effect, you also push the target 2 squares and knock it prone.
