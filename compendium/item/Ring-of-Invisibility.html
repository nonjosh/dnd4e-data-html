Ring of InvisibilityLevel 18 Rare

This simple golden ring bears Rellanic script etched lightly across its surface.

Ring Slot        85,000 gp

Property

Gain a +4 item bonus to Stealth checks.

Power (Illusion) Encounter (Standard Action)

You become invisible until the end of your next turn.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, using this power requires only a minor action.



Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 252, Dungeon Master's Kit, page(s) 267.
