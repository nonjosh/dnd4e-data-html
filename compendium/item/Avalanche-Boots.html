Avalanche BootsLevel 10 Uncommon

These heavy leather boots are covered in thick rock dust, and will not clean off no matter how well they are scrubbed.

Feet Slot        5,000 gp

Properties

  • Whenever you hit with an attack that pushes a target, you push the target 1 extra square.
  • After charging, you can shift 1 square before your turn ends.
