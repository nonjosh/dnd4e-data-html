Hide of Plain SightLevel 25 Uncommon

This wall hanging is not woven, but rather consists of sheets of soft, vellum-like skins cunningly stitched together. The scene it depicts is tattooed with many-colored inks.

Wondrous Item        625,000 gp

Utility Power Daily (Standard Action)

Requirement: You must be adjacent to the tapestry.

Effect: You step into the tapestry and become part of the depicted scene. While in the tapestry, you have line of sight but not line of effect to objects and creatures outside the tapestry. Creatures outside the tapestry have line of sight but not line of effect to you.
    Until the effect ends, you cannot move, you do not age, and you do not need to breathe, drink, or eat. You can end this effect as a standard action. The effect automatically ends after one year or if the tapestry is destroyed. When the effect ends, you appear in a square of your choice adjacent to the tapestry. Until the effect ends, no other creature can use this power.

Utility Power Daily (Standard Action)

Effect: You place an object against the surface of the tapestry, and the object appears as part of the depicted scene. You can end this effect as a standard action or by storing a different object in the tapestry, whereupon the first object is expelled. The effect automatically ends after one year or if the tapestry is destroyed. When the effect ends, the stored object appears in a square of your choice adjacent to the tapestry. Until the effect ends, no other creature can use this power.



Published in Dragon Magazine 418.
