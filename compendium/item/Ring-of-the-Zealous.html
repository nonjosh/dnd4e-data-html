Ring of the ZealousLevel 15 Uncommon

This gold ring is inlaid with mother-of-pearl blessed by priests of old.

Ring Slot        25,000 gp

Property

When you use the turn undead power, you can increase the size of the burst by 1.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You use turn undead.

Effect: Each target you hit with that power is restrained instead of immobilized.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you can increase the size of the burst by 1 (in addition to the increase from the ring's property).



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 72.
