Spying Mirrors (pair)Level 18 Uncommon

You look into one mirror and see out of its twin.

Wondrous Item        85,000 gp

Power (Standard Action)

When each of these mirrors is affixed to a solid structure and you're adjacent to one of the mirrors, you can see and hear through the other mirror as if you were in its square until the start of your next turn. If you choose, creatures adjacent to the other mirror can also see and hear through yours.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 82.
