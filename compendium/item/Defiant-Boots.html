Defiant BootsLevel 18 Uncommon

These sturdy iron boots grip the ground when you are moved against your will.

Feet Slot        85,000 gp

Property

When you are pulled, pushed, or slid, reduce the distance you are moved by 2.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you are subject to a pull, push, or slide effect. Instead, shift a number of squares equal to the number of squares you would have been pulled, pushed, or slid.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 128.
