Ritualist's LecternLevel 12 Uncommon

This heavy teak lectern is engraved with runes and patterns of arcane power.

Wondrous Item        13,000 gp

Property

When you use this lectern to master a ritual, reduce the time necessary to 4 hours, and you can master rituals of a level equal to or less than your level + 2.

Power (Standard Action)

Use this power before performing a ritual that you have mastered. Performing the ritual takes half the normal time. This power has no effect on the time it takes to perform a ritual from a scroll.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 81.
