Ring of Dimensional EscapeLevel 20 Uncommon

You might escape death, but only if you have friends nearby.

Ring Slot        125,000 gp

Property

Add 1 square to the maximum distance of any teleport you make.

Power (Teleportation) Daily (No Action)

Use this power when an attack drops you to 0 hit points or fewer. Teleport 20 squares.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you also stabilize. You don't make death saving throws unless you take more damage.



Published in Manual of the Planes, page(s) 157.
