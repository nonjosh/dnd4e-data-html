Power (Polymorph) Daily (Standard Action)

You assume the form of an ordinary bat and gain a fly speed equal to your speed until the end of the encounter or until you end the effect as a minor action. You can't attack, carry anything, or manipulate objects while in this form. If you end this effect while you're still airborne, you float to the ground without taking falling damage.
