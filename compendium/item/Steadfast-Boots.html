Steadfast BootsLevel 8 Uncommon

Rough iron studs nailed to these boots buttress your defenses.

Feet Slot        3,400 gp

Power Encounter (Minor Action)

As long as you stay in the same space that you began the current turn, gain a +2 power bonus to AC and all defenses until the beginning of your next turn. If you move or are moved from your starting square at any time through any means, you lose these bonuses.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 130.
