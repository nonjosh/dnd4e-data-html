Ring of Guarded WillLevel 26 Uncommon

This band is made of sculpted force. It occasionally hums faintly in your mind.

Ring Slot        1,125,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Will.

Power (Psychic) Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: An enemy hits you with an attack that targets Will.

Effect: The triggering enemy takes ongoing 10 psychic damage (save ends).
    If you've reached at least one milestone today, the triggering enemy instead takes ongoing 15 psychic damage (save ends).
