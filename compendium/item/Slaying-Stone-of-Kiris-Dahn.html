Slaying Stone of Kiris DahnLevel 17 Rare

Blood-red runes wind around this twisted mass of glassy black rock.

Wondrous Item        0 gp

Requirement: This power can be used only in the city of kiris dahn or within 5 miles of its borders.

Property

This item cannot be created with the Enchant Magic Item ritual or disenchanted with the Disenchant Magic Item ritual.

Power Daily (Standard Action)

Trigger: Make an attack: Ranged 20; +10 vs. Fortitude; the slaying stone is destroyed, and the target dies at the end of its next turn.

Effect: You are weakened until the end of your next turn.



Published in HS1 The Slaying Stone, page(s) 10.
