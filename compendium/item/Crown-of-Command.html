Crown of CommandLevel 17 Uncommon

This ornate crown greatly enhances your diplomatic and intimidation skills.

Head Slot        65,000 gp

Property

Gain a +4 item bonus to Diplomacy checks and Intimidate checks.

Power Daily (Free Action)

You can use this power when you or an ally within 5 squares of you rolls a poor Diplomacy or Intimidate check. You or the ally rerolls the check and uses the new result.



Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 248.
