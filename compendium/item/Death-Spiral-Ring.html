Death Spiral RingLevel 16 Uncommon

This ring's dizzying black spirals seem to bend space and time.

Ring Slot        45,000 gp

Property

You gain resist 5 necrotic.

Power (Teleportation) Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: A creature within 10 squares of you drops to 0 hit points or fewer.

Effect: You teleport into the creature's space or to a square adjacent to it.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you can use the power a second time during this encounter. This second use doesn't count as a use of a daily magic item power.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 70.
