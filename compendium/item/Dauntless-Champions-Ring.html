Dauntless Champion's RingLevel 30 Uncommon

Supernal, Iokharic, and Barazhad characters entwine about this thick mithral band, rearranging themselves when the ring's magic is activated.

Ring Slot        3,125,000 gp

Property

You gain a +4 item bonus to your healing surge value.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

If you have expended all of your encounter attack powers, you regain the use of one of them, determined randomly.

If you've reached at least one milestone today and have expended all of your daily attack powers, you regain the use of one of them, determined randomly.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 70.
