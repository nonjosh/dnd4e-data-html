Crown of WhispersLevel 10 Uncommon

This gold circlet rises to points above the temples and the center of the brow. Three star rubies decorate the front.

Head Slot        5,000 gp

Property

Gain a +2 item bonus to Diplomacy and Insight checks.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You gain a +2 item bonus to attack rolls with a Charisma-based power you use before the end of your turn. This bonus increases to +3 if the power has the charm keyword.
