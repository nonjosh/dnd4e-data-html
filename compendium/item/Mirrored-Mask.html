Mirrored MaskLevel 27 Uncommon

A jagged-cut mask of silvered glass appears to show images of eladrin scouts even as it protects you from attacks.

Head Slot        1,625,000 gp

Properties

  • You are immune to gaze attacks.
  • You gain a +2 bonus to all defenses against ranged or area attacks.
