Suppression CrystalLevel 4+ Common

The crystal diminishes the lightning bolt and silences the thunder.

Lvl 440 gp Lvl 2421,000 gp
Lvl 14800 gp

Alchemical Item: OtherFormula Cost: 120 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or NatureTime: 1 hour

Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Until it triggers or until the end of the encounter, you are protected by the suppression crystal. As an immediate interrupt that occurs automatically when you are first hit by a thunder or a lightning attack, you gain resist 5 thunder and resist 5 lightning until the end of your next turn. If this power is used but the resistance is not triggered before the end of the encounter, the crystal is still consumed.

Level 14: Gain resist 10 thunder and resist 10 lightning.

Level 24: Gain resist 15 thunder and resist 15 lightning.
