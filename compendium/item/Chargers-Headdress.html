Charger's HeaddressLevel 12 Uncommon

Fashioned from a mountain ram's horns, this headdress lets you charge unhindered through the spirit world.

Head Slot        13,000 gp

Property

You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls with charge attacks.

Power (Teleportation) Daily (Standard Action)

Make a charge attack, but instead of moving your speed before the attack, you teleport the same number of squares.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 111.
