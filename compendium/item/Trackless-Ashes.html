Trackless AshesLevel 6 Common

You dump these ashes into your campfire, removing all signs of the camp and of your passage from it.

Consumable        75 gp

Utility Power Consumable (Standard Action)

Effect: You pour the ashes into a dying campfire. After 5 minutes, the remains of the campfire as well as all tracks within 20 squares of it vanish.



Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 101.
