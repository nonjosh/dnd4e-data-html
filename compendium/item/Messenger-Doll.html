Messenger DollLevel 11 Uncommon

With features resembling your own, this wood and cloth doll acts in your stead when you are not present.

Wondrous Item        9,000 gp

Utility Power Daily (Minor Action)

Effect: The doll animates for 1 hour, adopting your voice and a vague resemblance to your features. It has a speed of 6, and it can move to a location you specify. You can mentally direct the doll to another location as a minor action. The doll is destroyed if it is attacked. You can hear through the doll and speak through it. If the doll moves more than a mile away from you, you can no longer hear through it or control its actions, and the doll stops moving and stands rigid.



Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 137.
