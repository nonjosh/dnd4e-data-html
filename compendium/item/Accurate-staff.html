Accurate staff


Superior
Cost: 20 gp
Weight: 4 lb.

An accurate staff is smooth and straight, balanced for easy handling and made of polished hardwood.

Properties:
Accurate (You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls made with an accurate implement.).

Group:
Staff (In its most basic form, a staff is a long piece of wood or some other substance, roughly the same diameter along its whole length.).

Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 195, Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 40.
