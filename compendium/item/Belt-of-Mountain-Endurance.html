Belt of Mountain EnduranceLevel 18 Uncommon

The steel buckle of this leather belt is engraved with the image of a mountain.

Waist Slot        85,000 gp

Property

When you spend a healing surge, you can add your Strength modifier to the surge value.

Power Daily (Free Action)

You can trigger this power when you spend an action point. You gain a +2 bonus to all defenses until the start of your next turn.
