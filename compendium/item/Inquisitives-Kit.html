Inquisitive’s Kit


Category: Gear
Price: 40 gp
Weight: 4 lb
Description: This gear includes several containers made from different materials, brushes, mundane dusts, tweezers, picks, probes, a magnifying glass, ink and quills, parchment, and a small journal. An inquisitive’s kit grants a +2 bonus to Perception checks to search an area for something specific.

Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 96.
