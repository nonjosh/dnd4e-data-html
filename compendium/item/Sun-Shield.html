Sun ShieldLevel 13 Uncommon

This golden disc flares with radiance as you wield it.

Arms Slot: Any shield        13,000 gp

Property

This shield can be used as a one-handed melee weapon with +3 proficiency, 1d6 damage, heavy blade, off-hand. It grants a +3 enhancement bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls when used as a melee weapon, and it deals 3d12 extra radiant damage on a critical hit.

Power (Radiant) (Minor Action)

The shield sheds bright light within 10 squares, and melee attacks made with the shield deal radiant damage instead of other damage types. You can end this effect as a minor action.
