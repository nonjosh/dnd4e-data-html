Woundstitch PowderLevel 1 Common

This worn leather pouch contains a quantity of life-saving yellow powder.

Wondrous Item        360 gp

Power (Standard Action)

You sprinkle this dust on an adjacent dying creature. That creature stops making death saving throws until it takes damage, and any untyped ongoing damage on the creature ends.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 78.
