Reflective ShieldLevel 27 Uncommon

A nasty surprise awaits those who make attacks against you while you wear this mirrorlike shield.

Arms Slot: Any shield        1,625,000 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Use this power when an attack against your AC or Reflex defense misses. The source of the attack makes a new attack roll against its own appropriate defense. If the attack roll succeeds, it hits the attacker (apply damage and effect where appropriate).



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 119.
