Hypnotic ShieldLevel 14 Uncommon

The spiral pattern on this round shield mesmerizes unwary foes.

Arms Slot: Any shield        21,000 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Use this power when a melee attack misses you. The attacker is dazed (save ends). This effect also ends if you are not adjacent to the attacker at the end of your turn, or if the attacker can no longer see you.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 117.
