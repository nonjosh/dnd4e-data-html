Coif of FocusLevel 21 Uncommon

This plain mail hood hangs close to your eyes and ears, protecting them from assault.

Head Slot        225,000 gp

Property

Gain a +5 item bonus to saving throws against effects that make you dazed and/or stunned.

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when an attack would make you dazed or stunned. Spend a healing surge to not be dazed or stunned by that attack.
