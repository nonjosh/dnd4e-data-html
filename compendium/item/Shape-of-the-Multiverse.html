Shape of the MultiverseLevel 20 Uncommon

This dodecahedron, carved from amethyst, initially appears to have numbered sides. On second glance, stars and spirals swirl within it.

Wondrous Item        125,000 gp

Utility Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You or a creature within 10 squares of you makes an attack roll, skill check, or saving throw and dislikes the result.

Effect: The triggering creature must reroll and take the second result.



Published in Dragon Magazine 417.
