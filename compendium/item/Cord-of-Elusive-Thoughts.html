Cord of Elusive ThoughtsLevel 18 Uncommon

This thin cord of eel's skin lets you focus the power of your mind to escape from physical restraints.

Waist Slot        85,000 gp

Property

When you make an escape check, you can make an Arcana check instead of an Acrobatics or Athletics check.

Power (Psionic) Daily (Minor Action)

Effect: You spend 1 power point and gain a +5 bonus to your next escape attempt made before the end of your next turn. If the attempt succeeds, you can shift 2 squares as a free action.



Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 159.
