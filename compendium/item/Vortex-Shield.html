Vortex ShieldLevel 14 Uncommon

Arcane runes form a spiral pattern on the surface of this shield, which serves to draw large attacks toward it.

Arms Slot: Any shield        21,000 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An enemy targets you with a burst or blast attack.

Effect: The triggering enemy takes a -4 penalty to attack rolls against all other targets of the attack.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 88.
