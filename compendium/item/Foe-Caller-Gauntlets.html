Foe Caller GauntletsLevel 22 Uncommon

No enemy can escape your challenge when you point the foe out while wearing these gauntlets.

Hands Slot        325,000 gp

Power (Teleportation) Encounter (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An enemy marked by you makes an attack that does not include you.

Effect: You teleport the enemy 10 squares to a space adjacent to you and make a melee basic attack against that enemy.
