Amethyst Band of Invisible EyesLevel 19 Uncommon

This band of pristine amethyst is favored by spellcasters seeking indirect targeting capability.

Ring Slot        105,000 gp

Property

Determine line of sight from the square you occupy or any square adjacent to you. Determine cover from the square you occupy as normal.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

Choose a square within 10 squares of you. Determine line of sight from this square until the end of your next turn.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you also gain darkvision until the end of your next turn.
