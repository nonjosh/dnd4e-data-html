Nightspider Silk


Category: Gear
Price: 50 gp
Weight: 1 lb
Description: The spiders of Brokenstone Vale weave a thread that can be seen only by the light of the moon. When the clouds part, these silvered webs glow as though painted with gleaming dewdrops. A spool of nightspider silk is strong as a length of wire but wholly invisible during the day.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 211.
