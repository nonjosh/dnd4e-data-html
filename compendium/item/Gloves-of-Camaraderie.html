Gloves of CamaraderieLevel 21 Uncommon

These cashmere gloves draw an ailing ally's condition onto you.

Hands Slot        225,000 gp

Power Encounter (Immediate Reaction)

Use this power when an ally within 10 squares of you gains a condition or harmful effect. You gain that condition or effect, and the ally loses it. The condition or effect lasts for the duration specified in the description of the power that caused it.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 134.
