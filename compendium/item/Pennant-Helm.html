Pennant HelmLevel 14 Uncommon

This brightly decorated helmet grants you strength of will and helps revitalize your allies.

Head Slot        21,000 gp

Property

You gain a +1 item bonus to Will.

Power (Healing) Daily (Minor Action)

Spend a healing surge. You don't regain hit points as normal. Instead, each ally who can see you can spend a healing surge.
