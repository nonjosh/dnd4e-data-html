Torch


Category: Gear
Price: 1 sp
Weight: 1 lb
Description: A torch is a wooden rod capped with twisted flax soaked in tallow. A torch burns for one hour and provides bright light to a radius of 5 squares.

Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 222, Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 335, Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms, page(s) 336.
