Kruthik PotionLevel 10 Uncommon

This foul concoction bestows a burst of health and coats you in fine, silvery plates that protect against acid.

Consumable: Potion        200 gp

Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Drink this potion and spend a healing surge. You do not regain hit points as normal. Instead, gain 15 temporary hit points and resist 5 acid until the end of the encounter.
