Phantom BridleLevel 10 Common

Placing this bridle on your phantom steed makes it nearly impervious to damage.

Wondrous Item        5,000 gp

Property

After performing the Phantom Steed ritual, you can place this bridle on one of the steeds created. That phantom steed gains resist 20 to all damage until the ritual ends or you remove the bridle.



Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 29.
