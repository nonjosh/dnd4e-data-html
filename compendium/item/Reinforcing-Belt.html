Reinforcing BeltLevel 9 Common

This sturdy belt offers magical protection when you need it most.

Waist Slot        4,200 gp

Properties

  • Only the wearer of this belt can remove it. If the wearer is dead, anyone else can remove the belt as a standard action.
  • Enemies cannot perform a coup de grace against you while you are helpless.


Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 166.
