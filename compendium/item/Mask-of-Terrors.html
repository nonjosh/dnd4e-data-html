Mask of TerrorsLevel 14 Uncommon

This green metal mask has a strip of studs that run down the center and end just above the nose.

Head Slot        21,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Intimidate checks.

Power (Fear) Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you damage an enemy with a melee attack. You push the target 3 squares.
