Ring of WizardryLevel 21 Uncommon

This silver band is engraved with powerful arcane symbols.

Ring Slot        225,000 gp

Property

Gain a +3 item bonus to Arcana checks.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

Regain the use of an arcane encounter utility power that you've already used (as if you hadn't used it this encounter).

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you can instead regain the use of an arcane encounter attack power.
