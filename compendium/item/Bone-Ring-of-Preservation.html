Bone Ring of PreservationLevel 19 Uncommon

This plain, bone ring protects its wearer from life-draining effects.

Ring Slot        105,000 gp

Property

You gain resist 15 necrotic.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when an effect would make you lose a healing surge. You do not lose the healing surge.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, the source of the effect takes 3d10 damage.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 156.
