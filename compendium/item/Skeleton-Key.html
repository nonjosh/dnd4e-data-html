Skeleton KeyLevel 10 Uncommon

This ornate ivory key opens locks with ease.

Wondrous Item        5,000 gp

Power Daily (Standard Action)

When you touch the key to a locked door, chest, gate, or other object, make a Thievery check with a +20 bonus against the DC required to open the lock.
