Standard of TaraskirLevel 13 Uncommon

The banner unfurls with a roar to reveal a stylized lion’s head in red against a gold background.

Wondrous Item        17,000 gp

Utility Power (Zone) Daily (Standard Action)

Effect: When you plant the standard in your space or an adjacent square, it creates a zone in a close burst 10. While in the zone, you and your allies gain a +3 item bonus to damage rolls, and can use second wind as a minor action.
    This effect lasts until the end of the encounter or until the standard is removed from the ground. Any character in or adjacent to the battle standard’s square can remove it from the ground as a standard action.



Published in Dungeon Magazine 202.
