Talisman of RepulsionLevel 30 Uncommon

This platinum talisman is inset with astral diamonds that flash brightly and unleash a burst of force when you are threatened.

Lvl 30+63,125,000 gp

Neck Slot

Enhancement Bonus: Fortitude, Reflex, and Will

Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Use this power when you are hit by a melee attack. Make an attack against the enemy that attacked you: Melee 1; +35 vs. Fortitude; on a hit, the target slides 5 squares and is immobilized until the end of your next turn.
