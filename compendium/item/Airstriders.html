These light coverings lift your feet and your spirit.

Feet Slot        625,000 gp

Property

You take no damage from a fall and always land on your feet. You have a fly speed equal to your speed +2, but you must end each turn on a solid surface or you fall.

Power Encounter (Free Action)

Use this power when you would fall. You do no fall until the end of your next turn.
