Oceanstrider BootsLevel 14 Uncommon

Water is no obstacle for you in these thigh-high oilskin boots.

Feet Slot        21,000 gp

Property

Gain a +1 item bonus to speed. You can move across and stand on horizontal liquid surfaces as though they were solid ground. You still take damage from hazardous liquid surfaces upon which you stand (such as acid and magma).



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 129.
