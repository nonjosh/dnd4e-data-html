Silt SandalsLevel 5 Uncommon

Dust and stains fall away from the surface and the tread of these simple clogs.

Feet Slot        1,000 gp

Property

You ignore difficult terrain resulting from dirt, sand, or silt. You leave no tracks in such terrain. If you start your turn standing on a solid surface, you can move across a nonsolid horizontal surface (such as silt or water) as if it were solid ground. If you are on a nonsolid surface at the end of your turn, you sink.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You can move across non-solid horizontal surfaces (such as silt or water) as if they were normal terrain until the end of the encounter.



Published in Dark Sun Campaign Setting, page(s) 128.
