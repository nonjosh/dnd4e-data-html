Rain Stick


Category: Gear
Price: 150 gp
Weight: 1 lb
Description: Rain sticks are crafted from tree branches by dryads, hags, and other fey in the wilderness. When a creature breaks a rain stick, the act creates a sudden downpour of rain in a half-mile radius that lasts up to one hour. If a stick is broken indoors or underground, the downpour occurs outside, centered above the location where the stick was broken.

Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 134.
