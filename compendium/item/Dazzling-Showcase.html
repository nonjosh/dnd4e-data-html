Dazzling ShowcaseLevel 14 Uncommon

A finely crafted cabinet of polished rare wood with crystal shelves, this fixture displays your collection and awes those who come before you.

Wondrous Item        21,000 gp

Property

While five different art objects each worth at least 1,000 gp are displayed in the cabinet and you are within 5 squares of the cabinet, you gain a +2 item bonus to attack rolls when using fear powers.



Published in Dragon Magazine 383, page(s) 47.
