Sleep GrenadeLevel 11 Rare

This device is about the size of a large apple, with indentations all around that are suitable for grasping.

Consumable        350 gp

Attack Power (Charm) Consumable (Standard Action)

Attack: Area burst 2d3 - 1 within 10 (creatures in the burst); +14 vs. Will.

Malfunction: If you roll a 1 on an attack roll with this grenade, you fall unconscious (save ends).

Hit: The target is slowed (save ends).

First Failed Saving Throw: The target is instead unconscious (save ends).



Published in Dragon Magazine 410.
