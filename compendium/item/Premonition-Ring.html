Premonition RingLevel 15 Uncommon

With this dark obsidian ring on your finger, you act quickly when faced with danger.

Ring Slot        25,000 gp

Property

Gain a +2 item bonus to initiative and passive Perception checks.

Power Daily (No Action)

Use this power when you are surprised. You are not surprised.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you also move 3 squares and take a minor action.
