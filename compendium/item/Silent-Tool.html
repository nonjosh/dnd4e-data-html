Silent ToolLevel 2 Common

This mundane-looking tool muffles the noise you make while you work.

Wondrous Item        520 gp

Property

When used to do the work it was designed for, a silent tool grants the user a +5 power bonus to Stealth checks made while using the tool. Any individual mundane tool—a woodcutter’s axe, a crowbar, a grappling hook, a hammer, a shovel, and so on—can be made silent. For example, a silent crowbar could aid attempts to open a locked door without alerting the sleeping monster on the other side, while a silent grappling hook has less chance of being heard by nearby guards.
This property can be applied only to mundane tools. It cannot be placed on weapons, clothing, jewelry, or other mundane objects.
