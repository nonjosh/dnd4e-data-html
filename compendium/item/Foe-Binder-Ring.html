Foe Binder RingLevel 19 Uncommon

This hefty iron ring earns you greater enmity from all whom you challenge. Such a burden falls heavy on all but the stoutest heart.

Ring Slot        105,000 gp

Property

When you mark an enemy, the enemy takes the -2 penalty to attack rolls against other creatures even when you are included in the attack.
