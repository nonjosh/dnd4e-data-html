Purple Dragon Commander's RingLevel 13 Uncommon

This silver ring is engraved with the Purple Dragon signet of the Obarskyr royal family.

Ring Slot        17,000 gp

Power (Arcane) Daily (Standard Action)

Detect Magic and Poison: Activated by command word (usually inscribed on the inside of the ring, and typically "Bonthar". When the power is activated and the ring touched to food or drink, it glows with an eerie gold-green if the substance is poisonous and bright blue if it is enchanted (including potions), although the ring cannot identify what sort of poison or magic

is present.

Power (Arcane) Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

You can use this power when you fall. You take no damage from the fall, regardless of its distance, and you do not fall prone at the end of the fall.



Published in Dragon Magazine 365, page(s) 20.
