Shield of AversionLevel 15 Uncommon

Even as your foe closes in to attack, you see its eyes cloud with uncertainty.

Arms Slot: Any shield        25,000 gp

Property

You gain a +4 item bonus to all defenses against charge attacks.

Utility Power (Charm) Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An enemy hits you with a melee attack while you are adjacent to another creature.

Effect: Choose a creature adjacent to you, other than the triggering enemy. The attack hits that creature instead of you.
