Blood JewelLevel 8 Rare

This blood red glowing crystal hovers about your head, bathing you in candle-bright red light.

Head Slot        3,400 gp

Properties

  • The item sheds bright, blood-red light.
  • You make saving throws against ongoing untyped damage at the start of your turn instead of at the end of your turn.
  • You gain a +2 bonus to all Charisma-based skill checks when dealing with servants and cultists of Ul-Athra.


Published in Dungeon Magazine 183.
