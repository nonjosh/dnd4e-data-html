Feystride BootsLevel 11 Uncommon

These supple gray boots help you close the distance across the battlefield.

Feet Slot        9,000 gp

Property

While you have a drow or elf ally within 10 squares of you, the range of your fey step racial power increases by 2 squares.

Power (Teleportation) Encounter (Move Action)

While you have an eladrin ally within 10 squares of you, you can expend your elven accuracy racial power or your use of the Lolthtouched racial trait to teleport 5 squares.
