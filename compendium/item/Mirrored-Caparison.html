Mirrored CaparisonLevel 2 Uncommon

This coat contains rows of faceted crystals that protect against wide-ranging attacks.

Mount        520 gp

Property

The mount gains a +1 item bonus to Fortitude, Reflex, and Will defenses.

Power (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when an area attack would target the mount you are riding. The mount is not targeted by the attack.
