Symbol of RevivificationLevel 30 Uncommon

This symbol holds the power to revive a dead or dying ally.

Implement: Holy symbol        3,125,000 gp

Critical: +6d6 damage

Power (Healing) Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you hit with an attack using this holy symbol. Spend two healing surges, do not regain any hit points, and choose a dying or dead ally within 10 squares of you. That ally is returned to life at his bloodied hit point total. This power does not revive an ally who's been dead for longer than 1 day.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 90.
