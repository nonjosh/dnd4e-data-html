Tattoo of the UnluckyLevel 7 Uncommon

Broken coins, three-leaf clovers, mirror shards, or all three do nicely for this tattoo.

Wondrous Item        2,600 gp

Property

When you miss with a daily attack power that has an effect on a miss, that effect deals extra damage equal to the number of healing surges you have spent since your last extended rest.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 86.
