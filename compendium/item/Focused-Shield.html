Focused ShieldLevel 13 Uncommon

When a foe misses you at range, the complex engravings on this shield's surface hinder the accuracy of that foe's subsequent attacks.

Arms Slot: Any shield        17,000 gp

Property

You gain resist 5 to all damage from ranged attacks.

Power (Augmentable) Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: A ranged attack misses you.

Effect: The triggering enemy takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls for attacks that include you as a target until the end of your next turn.

Augment 1: The triggering enemy takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls (save ends).
