Rune of Fiery MightLevel 19 Rare

Summoning the rune's magic causes liquid fire to fill its etchings until they blaze with brilliant light.

Lost Rune        105,000 gp

Property

Whenever you would enter a new rune state, you can enter the rune state of fiery might instead. While in this state, when you hit with an attack, enemies adjacent to you take 10 fire damage.

Power (Fire, Teleportation) Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: You are hit by an attack.

Effect: Each enemy adjacent to you takes 10 fire damage. You then teleport up to 5 squares.



Published in Dragon Magazine 394.
