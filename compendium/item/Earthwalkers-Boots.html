Earthwalker's BootsLevel 23 Uncommon

These heavy boots look to be fashioned from stone, but are as flexible as ordinary leather.

Feet Slot        425,000 gp

Power Daily (Move Action)

You can burrow 5 squares. If you burrow, you gain combat advantage until the end of your current turn against any enemies that you end your move adjacent to.
