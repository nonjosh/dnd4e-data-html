Ride the WindsLevel 1 Uncommon

You whisper quiet words to a group of mounts, giving them the ability to run like the wind.

Fey Magic Gift        360 gp

Utility Power Daily (Minor Action)

Effect: Choose up to eight creatures within 10 squares of you that have the mount keyword. For the next 8 hours, each of those creatures gains a +2 power bonus to speed outside combat.
