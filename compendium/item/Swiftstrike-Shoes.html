Swiftstrike ShoesLevel 20 Uncommon

More supple shoes never touched the foot of a hero, and in these shoes a hero's feet sometimes barely touch the ground.

Feet Slot        125,000 gp

Property

You gain a +5 bonus to speed until the end of your first turn each encounter.

Power (Teleportation) Daily (Move Action)

You teleport 20 squares to a space adjacent to any enemy within your line of sight.
