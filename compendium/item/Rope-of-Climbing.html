Rope of ClimbingLevel 10 Common

A coil of golden rope.

Wondrous Item        5,000 gp

Property

This 100-foot-long rope has 100 hit points and can hold up to 2,500 pounds (roughly 10 Medium creatures and their gear).

Power (Minor Action)

The rope moves up to 10 squares along a horizontal or a vertical surface. As part of the same action, it can tie itself around an object to create a secure point for climbing. It can't tie itself to or otherwise affect a creature.

Anyone holding the rope can activate its power. On command, the rope unties itself as a minor action.
