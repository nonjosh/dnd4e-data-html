Phoenix HelmLevel 23 Uncommon

This helm, carved to resemble a phoenix, increases your perception and can be activated to improve a ranged attack.

Head Slot        425,000 gp

Property

Gain a +5 item bonus to Perception checks.

Power (Fire) Daily (Minor Action)

Gain a +2 power bonus to your next ranged attack roll this turn. If that attack hits, it deals an extra 2d8 fire damage.
