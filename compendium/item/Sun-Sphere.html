Sun SphereLevel 20 Uncommon

This tiny sphere in the shape of the sun circles your head, burning foes who move too close.

Head Slot        125,000 gp

Property

You gain a +1 item bonus to Will.

Power (Fire, Radiant) Encounter (Minor Action)

Each enemy adjacent to you takes fire and radiant damage equal to your Strength modifier.

Special: If you have the Cosmic Magic class feature and are in the phase of the sun, this power affects each enemy within 2 squares of you.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 118.
