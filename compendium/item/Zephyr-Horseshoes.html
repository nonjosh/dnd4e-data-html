Zephyr HorseshoesLevel 9 Uncommon

These black iron horseshoes allow a mount to race across all kinds of terrain.

Mount        4,200 gp

Property

The mount ignores the effect of difficult terrain and can cross liquid surfaces as if they were solid ground. Any adverse effect of that terrain, such as the damage from acid or magma, still affects the mount normally.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 124.
