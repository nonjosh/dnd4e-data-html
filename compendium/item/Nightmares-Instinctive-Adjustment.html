Nightmare's Instinctive AdjustmentLevel 13 Uncommon

You let your heightened instincts guide you out of danger.

Grandmaster Training        17,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Perception checks.

Power (Augmentable, Psionic) Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: An enemy ends its turn in a square adjacent to you.

Effect: You shift 1 square and gain combat advantage against the triggering enemy until the end of your next turn.

Augment 1: As above, but you shift up to 3 squares.



Published in Dragon Magazine 393.
