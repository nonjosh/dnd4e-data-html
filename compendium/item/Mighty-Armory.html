Mighty ArmoryLevel 28 Uncommon

Gorgeous armor and brilliant weapons are mounted on the walls of this sumptuous chamber.

Wondrous Item        2,125,000 gp

Property

While five different magic weapons or armors each worth at least 100,000 gp are displayed in this armory and you are within 10 squares of the armory, you gain a +2 item bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls with melee attacks and a +2 item bonus to AC.



Published in Dragon Magazine 383, page(s) 47.
