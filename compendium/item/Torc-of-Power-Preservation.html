Torc of Power PreservationLevel 15+ Uncommon

This platinum and gold neckband contains a reservoir of energy that you can tap.

Lvl 15+325,000 gp Lvl 25+5625,000 gp
Lvl 20+4125,000 gp Lvl 30+63,125,000 gp

Neck Slot

Enhancement Bonus: Fortitude, Reflex, and Will

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power after you use an encounter power of the torc's level or lower. Roll 1d20 + the torc's enhancement bonus. If the result is 10 or higher, that power renews as if you had taken a short rest. If the result is lower than 10, you don't expend the use of this power and no daily use of a magic item power occurs.
