Sun's SliverLevel

This sliver of pure sunlight is the only object in the universe that can truly destroy the Winter Witch.

Wondrous Item

Special: This item cannot be crafted.

Properties

  • The creature that possesses the Sun’s Sliver takes no ill effect from cold weather and gains ice walk.
  • The creature that possesses the Sun’s Sliver gains resist cold 10.

Power Daily (Standard Action)

Effect: You and allies within 5 squares of you regain the use of your second wind and all spent encounter powers.

Power Daily (Standard Action)

Effect: If the artifact is used against Koliada the Winter Witch when she is unconscious and at 0 hit points or fewer, this use destroys the Winter Witch and the Sun’s Sliver.



Published in Dungeon Magazine 162, page(s) 61, Dungeon Magazine Annual, page(s) 59.
