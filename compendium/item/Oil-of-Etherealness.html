Oil of EtherealnessLevel 20 Common

When you apply this oil to yourself or an object, it is momentarily removed from the physical world.

Lvl 205,000 gp

Alchemical Item: OilFormula Cost: 6,750 gp
Key Skill: ArcanaTime: 1 hour

Utility Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Effect: You apply the oil to yourself or to an adjacent creature or object. That creature or object becomes insubstantial and gains phasing until the end of your next turn. As a minor action, a creature benefiting from this power can lose a healing surge to sustain this power until the end of its next turn.



Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 133.
