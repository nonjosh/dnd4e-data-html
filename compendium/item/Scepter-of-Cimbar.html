Scepter of CimbarLevel 15 Uncommon

This 2-foot silver rod is inset with pieces of jet, which have been carved and positioned to resemble a stylized dragon rampant. The scepter's ball-shaped hilt has a hole through its center, so that the implement can be hung on a sash cord around the waist.

Lvl 15+325,000 gp

Implement: Rod

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d8 damage per plus

Properties

  • You gain telepathy 10.
  • You gain a +3 item bonus to Insight and Perception checks.

Power (Illusion) Daily (Standard Action)

You gain the appearance of a faceless, androgynous humanoid of average build. The illusion lasts for 24 hours, or until you end it as a minor action.
