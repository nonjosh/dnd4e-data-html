Distillation Kit


Category: Gear
Price: 50 gp
Weight: 20 lb
Description: Desert travelers use distillation kits to extract water from wastes or toxic fluids. The device “cooks” moisture from the materials and collects it in a reservoir. Using a distillation kit for 6 hours produces 1 day’s worth of water.

Published in Dark Sun Campaign Setting, page(s) 123.
