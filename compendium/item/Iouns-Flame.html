Ioun's FlameLevel 24 Uncommon

A flickering violet flame darts about your head, granting you knowledge and flaring to life at your command.

Head Slot        525,000 gp

Properties

  • You gain a +2 item bonus to Will.
  • You gain a +8 item bonus to knowledge and monster knowledge checks.

Power (Minor Action)

You set Ioun's flame to shed bright light 10 squares in all directions, dim light 5 squares in all directions, or no light.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 127.
