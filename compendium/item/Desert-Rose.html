Desert RoseLevel 7+ Uncommon

Growing only in remote desert oases, this yellow flower helps to maintain arcane and divine powers.

Consumable: Reagent

Power Consumable (Free Action)

Expend this reagent when you use a arcane or divine power of up to 5th level. You do not need to use an action on the following round to sustain that power (sustaining it for subsequent rounds does require the appropriate action).

Level 17: Power up to 15th level.

Level 27: Power up to 25th level.
