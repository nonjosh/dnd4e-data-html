Watcher's SignetLevel 11 Uncommon

This white gold ring takes the shape of a dagger that curls around the wearer's finger.

Ring Slot        9,000 gp

Property

You can use illusion powers within the radius of a revelation sphere, and your illusion powers do not end when you enter the radius of a revelation sphere.

Power (Illusion) Encounter (Minor Action)

Gain a +5 power bonus to Stealth checks until the end of your next turn.



Published in Dragon Magazine Annual, Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 16.
