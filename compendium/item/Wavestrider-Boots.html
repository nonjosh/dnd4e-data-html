Wavestrider BootsLevel 4 Uncommon

These enchanted boots allow you to walk across liquid as if it were solid land.

Feet Slot        840 gp

Property

If you begin your turn standing on a solid surface, you can move across liquid as if it were normal terrain. If you are still on liquid at the end of your turn, you fall in.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You can move across liquid surfaces as if they were normal terrain until the end of the encounter.



Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 246.
