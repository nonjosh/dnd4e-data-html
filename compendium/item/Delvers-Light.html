Delver's LightLevel 2 Common

Often attached to the forehead or chest, this magic gem gives off light powered by your life force.

Wondrous Item (embedded component)        520 gp

Requirement: You must have the living construct racial trait to use this item.

Property

Power (Free Action)

The delver's light sheds dim light to a radius of 20 squares.

Power (Free Action)

The delver's light sheds bright light to a radius of 20 squares.

Power (Free Action)

The delver's light sheds no light.
