Speak with AnimalsLevel 4 Uncommon

You speak to a creature using the language of animals, learning information from an unlikely source.

Fey Magic Gift        840 gp

Utility Power Daily (Minor Action)

Effect: For the next 10 minutes, you can understand the barks, chirps, roars, and other animal sounds of natural beasts, and they can understand your speech. This power does not make the beasts friendly or hostile toward you.



Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 142.
