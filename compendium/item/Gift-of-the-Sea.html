Gift of the SeaLevel 8 Uncommon

The harder your enemy tries to move you, the harder you move the enemy.

Elemental gift        3,400 gp

Attack Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: An enemy pulls, pushes, or slides you.

Effect: Close burst 10 (the triggering enemy in the burst). You push the target a number of squares equal to the distance you were pulled, pushed, or slid.



Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 158.
