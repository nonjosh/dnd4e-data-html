Syllable of SpiritLevel 19 Uncommon

When you manifest Vaul's brilliant light, your spirit shines with uncommon brightness, making it hard for other enemies to strike you.

Consumable: Consumable        4,200 gp

Power Consumable (Standard Action)

Requirement: You must be able to speak Abyssal or Supernal.

Effect: You speak the syllable and it orbits your head. Until the end of the encounter you are phasing and gain insubstantial while bloodied.



Published in Dragon Magazine 392.
