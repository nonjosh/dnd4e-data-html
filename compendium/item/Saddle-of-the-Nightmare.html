Saddle of the NightmareLevel 15 Uncommon

This black, twisted leather bridle lets you take advantage of your mount's ability to teleport.

Mount        25,000 gp

Property

When a mount teleports, the rider on the mount can remain mounted and teleport along with the mount even if the mount’s movement doesn’t normally allow it.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 124.
