Ricochet ShieldLevel 12 Uncommon

The strange, seemingly arbitrary angles of this shield take on a sinister purpose when you redirect a foe's ranged attack.

Arms Slot: Any shield        13,000 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Use this power when a ranged attack against AC misses you. The source of the attack repeats the attack roll against a different target of your choice within 10 squares of you. If the attack roll succeeds, it hits that target (apply damage and effect where appropriate).



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 119.
