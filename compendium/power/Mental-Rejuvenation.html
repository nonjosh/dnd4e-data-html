Ardent Utility 16Mental Rejuvenation

Your psionic magic allows one ally to ignore many wounds or several allies to ignore a few.

Daily        Healing, Psionic
Standard Action      Close burst 5

Target: You or one ally in the burst or you and each ally in the burst

Effect: If you target only one creature with this power, the target regains hit points as if he or she had spent two healing surges. If you target more than one creature, each target regains hit points as if he or she had spent a healing surge.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 32.
