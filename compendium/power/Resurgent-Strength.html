Cleric Utility 2Resurgent Strength

This healing prayer staunches wounds while also providing your ally with energy to redouble an attack.

Daily        Divine, Healing
Minor Action      Close burst 3

Target: You or one ally in the burst

Effect: The target can spend a healing surge. In addition, the target gains a +4 power bonus to damage rolls until the end of your next turn.


Published in Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 91.
