Druid Attack 17Scavenger's Prize

Your foe is trapped in your grasp as you drag it off.

Encounter        Beast Form, Implement, Primal
Standard Action      Melee touch

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d10 + Wisdom modifier damage, and you grab the target.

  Primal Predator: You can also shift a number of squares equal to your Dexterity modifier and pull the target with you.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 91.
