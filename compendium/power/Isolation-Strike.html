Ranger Attack 1Isolation Strike

Your flurry of attacks knocks your opponents back, allowing you to focus on a single foe.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One or two creatures

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage. If the target is not your quarry, you can push it a number of squares equal to your Wisdom modifier.

Miss: Half damage.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 35.
