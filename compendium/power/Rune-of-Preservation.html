Runepriest Utility 16Rune of Preservation

The rune of preservation grants its targets the ability to withstand attacks that would cripple a normal creature.

Encounter        Divine, Healing
Minor Action      Close burst 5

Target: Two allies in the burst or you and one ally in the burst

Effect: Each target gains resist 5 to all damage until the end of your next turn. In addition, each target can spend a healing surge.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 108.
