Sivis Truenamer Attack 11Utterance of Mockery

You speak words of Supernal so potent that your enemy loses its sense of identity and mistakenly attacks itself.

Encounter        Charm, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence or Charisma vs. Will

Hit: As a free action, the target makes a basic attack against itself and hits. If the target has multiple basic attacks, you choose which attack it uses.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 76.
