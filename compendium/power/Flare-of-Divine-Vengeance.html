Paladin Utility 6Flare of Divine Vengeance

The energy of your divine challenge erupts with light that heals you as the enemy attacks and empowers your next strike against it.

Daily        Divine, Healing
Immediate Interrupt      Personal

Trigger: The target of your divine challenge hits you

Effect: You can spend a healing surge. You gain a +2 power bonus to attack rolls against the triggering enemy until the end of your next turn.


Published in PH Heroes: Series 2.
