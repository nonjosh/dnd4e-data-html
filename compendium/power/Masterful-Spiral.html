Monk Attack 1Masterful Spiral

With a sudden burst of motion, you deliver crushing kicks and punches of psionic force to nearby enemies.

Daily        Force, Implement, Psionic, Stance
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d8 + Dexterity modifier force damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You assume the spiral stance. Until the stance ends, your reach with melee touch attacks increases by 1.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 66.
