Soul Igniter Utility 12Soul Furnace

The air around your allies erupts in licking fire, scorching any enemy that dares to come too close.

Daily        Fire, Primal
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, any enemy that starts its turn or enters a square adjacent to a creature affected by your soulfire power takes fire damage equal to your Wisdom modifier. A creature can take this damage only once per turn.
