Warlock Utility Shadow Warp

When a creature falls, you draw strength from its death to carry another creature across the battlefield.

At-Will        Arcane, Shadow
Free Action      Close burst 5

Trigger: You reduce a creature to 0 hit points, or an enemy adjacent to you drops to 0 hit points.

Target: You or one creature in the burst

Effect: You slide the target up to 3 squares.

Special: You can use this power only once per round.
