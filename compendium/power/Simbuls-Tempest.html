Simbarch of Aglarond Attack 20Simbul's Tempest

You create a powerful telekinetic storm to pummel your foes. Your arcane wrath transports you in a blaze of silver fire, burning away our mortal frailty.

Daily        Arcane, Force, Implement
Standard Action      Area burst 3 within 20 squares

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Intelligence vs. Fortitude

Hit: 5d10 force damage. After you finish all attack rolls for this power, for every enemy you hit, you can immediately roll one saving throw against an effect that a save can end. You can roll multiple saving throws against a single effect that a save can end if your first attempts fail.

Miss: Half damage, and you gain no extra saving throws.


Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 62.
