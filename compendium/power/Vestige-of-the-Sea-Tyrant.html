Sea Tyrant Oracle Attack 20Vestige of the Sea Tyrant

Your magic evokes the wrath of the Sea Tyrant, sweeping your foes away.

Daily        Arcane, Implement
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Constitution vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d6 + Constitution modifier damage, and you push the target a number of squares equal to your Constitution modifier and knock it prone.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You gain access to the vestige of the Sea Tyrant.

  Sea Tyrant Pact Boon: You slide one creature cursed by you that you can see a number of squares equal to your Constitution modifier.

  Eyes of the Vestige Augment: You also slide your eyes of the vestige target a number of squares equal to your Intelligence modifier.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 94.
