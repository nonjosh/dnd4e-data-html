Sorcerer Attack 17Rolls of Thunder

Spheres of thunder manifest and roll through the air to bowl over your enemies.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement, Thunder
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One or two creatures

Attack: Charisma vs. Will

Hit: 3d10 + Charisma modifier damage, and the target falls prone.

  Active Familiar: You can also push the target 3 squares.


Published in Dragon Magazine 386, page(s) 23.
