Shaman Attack 13Howling Gust

A howling spirit of wind appears next to your foe and batters it with a gale, then channels its essence through your spirit companion to whisk a nearby ally across the battlefield.

Encounter        Implement, Primal, Teleportation
Standard Action      Ranged 5

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d8 + Wisdom modifier damage, and you slide the target 2 squares. You teleport one ally adjacent to your spirit companion 5 squares.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 126.
