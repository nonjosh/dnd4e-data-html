Battlemind Utility 6Winged Weapon

You channel psionic energy into your weapon, adjusting the rules of nature so that the weapon will slide through the air as if on wings when you hurl it.

Encounter        Psionic
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Choose a weapon you are holding. The next melee attack you make with that weapon before the end of your next turn becomes a ranged attack with a range of 10. The weapon returns to your hand after you make that attack.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 48.
