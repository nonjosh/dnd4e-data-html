Life-Stealer Attack 11Soul Scorch

As black fire immolates your adversary, you release one of your life sparks. Your foe cries out in pain as he feels the life ebb from his body.

Encounter        Arcane, Fire, Implement, Necrotic
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Constitution vs. Will

Hit: 3d8 + Constitution modifier fire and necrotic damage. If the target creature has the same origin as a life spark you possess, you can expend a single life spark matching that origin to deal an extra 10 damage to the target.
