Favored of Mielikki Attack 11Protective Vines

Mielikki causes a growth of plants to constrict your foes and protect your friends.

Encounter        Charm
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each enemy in the blast

Attack: Your highest ability score modifier + 6 vs. Reflex
Level 21: Your highest ability score modifier + 9 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d6 + your highest ability score modifier damage. The target is slowed and cannot make opportunity attacks until the end of your next turn.
Level 21: 3d6 + your highest ability score modifier damage, and the target is slowed until the end of your next turn.
