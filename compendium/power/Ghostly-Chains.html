Avenger Attack 15Ghostly Chains

At the touch of your weapon, phantasmal fetters appear around your enemy, hindering its steps.

Daily        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Wisdom modifier damage, and the target is slowed (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and the target is slowed until the end of your next turn.

Special: If two or more of your allies are adjacent to the target, it is immobilized instead of slowed.


Published in Divine Power, page(s) 13.
