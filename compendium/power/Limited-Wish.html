Prince of Genies Utility 12Limited Wish

Name your desire, and the genie provides it.

Daily        Elemental
Minor Action      Close burst 1

Target: You or one ally in the burst

Effect: Choose one of the following.
         The target regains the use of an expended encounter utility power.
         The target treats its next miss before the end of the encounter as a hit.
         The target gains a +2 power bonus to all defenses until the end of the encounter.
         The target gains training in one skill of his or her choice until the end of the encounter.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 136.
