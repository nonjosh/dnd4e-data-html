Monk Attack 5Supreme Avalanche Combination

A cascade of punches creates deep wounds that your future attacks can exploit.

Daily        Implement, Psionic
Standard Action      Melee touch

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d10 + Dexterity modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, you gain a +2 power bonus to damage rolls against the target, and you slide it 1 square whenever you hit it with an attack. If that attack includes forced movement, you can increase the distance of that movement by 1 square instead of sliding the target 1 square.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 68.
