Druid Attack 29Blizzard’s Embrace

Your strike sets the spirits of elemental cold against your enemies in a storm of snow, wind, and ice.

Daily        Beast Form, Cold, Elemental, Implement, Primal, Zone
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 4d8 + Wisdom modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of the encounter. For enemies, the zone is heavily obscured and difficult terrain. In addition, any enemy that ends its turn in the zone takes 10 cold damage and is restrained until the start of its next turn.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 67.
