Psion Attack 29Consuming Psychosis

A burst of psychic energy inflicts a pain so potent that your foes lash out blindly in their madness.

Daily        Implement, Psionic, Psychic
Standard Action      Area burst 1 within 10 squares

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Intelligence vs. Will

Hit: 3d8 + Intelligence modifier psychic damage, and ongoing 10 psychic damage (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and ongoing 5 psychic damage (save ends).

Effect: When the target takes ongoing psychic damage from this attack, it makes a melee basic attack against a random creature as a free action. If it cannot make a melee attack against any creature, it targets itself with a melee basic attack.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 99.
