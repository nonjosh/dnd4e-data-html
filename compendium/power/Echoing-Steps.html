Bard Utility 6Echoing Steps

Your incantation pulls its subject into the Feywild long enough to move freely. Arcane reverberations enable shorter return trips.

Daily        Arcane, Teleportation
Minor Action      Ranged 10

Target: You or one ally

Effect: The target can teleport 6 squares as a free action. Until the end of the encounter, the target can teleport 1 square as a minor action.


Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 26.
