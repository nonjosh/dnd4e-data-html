Warlock Attack 7Maggot Conduit

Holes devoured through space-time crawl with the green-white maggots of the Worm. You invoke a conduit, which manifests as a writhing pit of maggots beneath your foes.

Encounter        Acid, Arcane, Implement, Zone
Standard Action      Area burst 1 within 10 squares

Target: Each creature in the burst

Attack: Constitution vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + Constitution modifier acid damage.

Star Pact: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of your next turn. Any creature that enters the zone or ends its turn there takes acid damage equal to 1 + your Intelligence modifier (a creature can take this damage only once per turn).


Published in Dragon Magazine 403.
