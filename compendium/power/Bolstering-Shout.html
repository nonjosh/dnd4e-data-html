Warlord Utility 10Bolstering Shout

Your war cry quickly rallies your allies.

Encounter        Healing, Martial
Minor Action      Close burst 10

Target: Each ally in the burst

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, each target regains additional hit points equal to your Charisma modifier when he or she uses second wind. Each target can also choose to use second wind as a minor action but gain no bonus to defenses from it.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 109.
