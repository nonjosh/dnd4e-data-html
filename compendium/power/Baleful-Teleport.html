Battlemind Attack 9Baleful Teleport

A crippling strike shunts your foe across the battlefield, then leaves it dazed.

Daily        Psionic, Teleportation, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Constitution vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2[W] + Constitution modifier damage, and you teleport the target 5 squares. The target is then dazed (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and the target is dazed until the end of your next turn.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 40.
