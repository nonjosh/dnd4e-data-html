Artificer Attack 27Thunder-Shock Weapon

Your weapon screams like a hurricane as you swing it toward your foe. It strikes with a thunderous blow, knocking your foe down.

Encounter        Arcane, Thunder, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Intelligence modifier thunder damage, and you knock the target prone. The target is dazed until the end of your next turn.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 57.
