Barbarian Attack 25Blood Hunger Rage

Your fierce attack leaves your target reeling. As you rage, the Primal Beast surges in your blood, drawing on the carnage around you to empower your attacks.

Daily        Primal, Rage, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 7[W] + Strength modifier, and the target is dazed (save ends).

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You enter the blood hunger rage. Until the rage ends, you gain a bonus to attack rolls equal to the number of bloodied creatures within 3 squares of you.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 60.
