Warlock Utility 2Charm of Hearts

Your enemies might not love you, but thanks to this charm crafted by the Prince of Hearts, they do not attack you when you give them the opportunity.

Daily        Arcane, Charm
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, you do not provoke opportunity attacks and you gain a +2 power bonus to all defenses.

Sustain Minor: The effect persists.


Published in Dragon Magazine 382, page(s) 60.
