Runepriest Attack 15Rune of the Flanking Wind

You imprint the rune of the flanking wind on your foe. Thus summoned, the wind carries your allies into position against the foe and protects them.

Daily        Divine, Teleportation, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. Reflex

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Choose one ally within 10 squares of you. You teleport that ally to a square where he or she flanks the target. Until the end of the encounter, you and your allies gain a +2 power bonus to all defenses while flanking the target.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 107.
