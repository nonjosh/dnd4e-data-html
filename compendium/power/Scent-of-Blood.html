Barbarian Utility 16Scent of Blood

As your lifeblood drains from you, the scent of it awakens primal power, strengthening your blows and aiding your recuperation.

Encounter        Primal
Immediate Reaction      Personal

Trigger: An enemy bloodies you

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, you gain a bonus equal to your Constitution modifier to damage rolls and your healing surge value.


Published in Primal Power, page(s) 18.
