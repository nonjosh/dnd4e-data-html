Bladeling Racial Utility 22Bred for Battle

You cause the blades all over your body to lengthen and tear your foes, while your awareness of nearby danger heightens.

Daily      Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You assume the bred for battle stance. Until the stance ends, you gain the following benefits.
         You gain a +2 power bonus to AC.
         You cannot be marked.
         Your melee weapon attacks that hit deal 2d6 extra damage.
         At the end of each of your turns, you can spend a healing surge and regain no hit points. If you do so, you regain the use of your razor storm power.


Published in Dragon Magazine 419.
