Psion Utility 16Kinetic Grasp

You quickly create a psychic connection to another creature that allows it to hold fast where it stands.

Encounter        Psionic
Immediate Interrupt      Ranged 10

Trigger: A creature within 10 squares of you is pulled, pushed, slid, or knocked prone

Target: The triggering creature

Effect: The target is unaffected by the forced movement or is not knocked prone.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 94.
