Chainbinder Attack 20Grab and Smash

You lash out with your weapon, drawing a foe closer. Then you snare a second enemy and slam it into the first.

Daily        Martial, Reliable, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a flail.

Target: One or two creatures

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage, and you slide the target 3 squares to a square adjacent to you. If you hit two targets, each of them takes extra damage equal to your Strength modifier.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 100.
