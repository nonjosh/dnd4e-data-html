Monk Utility 10Flame’s Blessing

The pale flames dancing across your body do not harm you, but instead turn you into a faster and more accurate attacker.

Daily        Elemental, Psionic, Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You assume the flame’s blessing stance. Until the stance ends, you gain a +2 power bonus to speed and a +1 power bonus to fire attack rolls.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 77.
