Fighter Attack 19Devastation’s Wake

You thrash your foes with an array of strikes, and then unleash your fury a second time against any that are left standing.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Close burst 1

Primary Target: Each enemy you can see in the burst

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Until the start of your next turn, you can use the secondary power at will.
