Ranger Utility 6Healing Herbs

You aid a wounded companion by quickly applying a poultice of healing herbs.

Daily        Healing, Martial
Minor Action      Melee touch

Target: One ally

Effect: You make a Heal check. The target regains hit points equal to half the result of your check. The target can make a saving throw against one poison effect on him or her that a save can end.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 37.
