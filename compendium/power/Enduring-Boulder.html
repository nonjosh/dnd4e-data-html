Monk Attack 25Enduring Boulder

You become a creature not unlike stone, capable of bringing great physical force to bear and proving almost invulnerable to attacks for a time.

Daily        Elemental, Implement, Polymorph, Psionic
Standard Action      Melee touch

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 5d10 + Dexterity modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You assume the form of the enduring boulder. While in this form, you gain resist 30 to all damage and a +3 power bonus to melee damage rolls. Each time you are hit by an attack, reduce the resistance by 5. When the resistance drops to 0, the form ends.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 82.
