Goblin Racial Utility 10Down and Through

All of a sudden, you’re beneath your clumsy foe and then out the other side.

Encounter
Move Action      Personal

Effect: Choose a Medium or larger enemy adjacent to you, and shift up to 5 squares to a different square adjacent to that creature. You can move through that creature’s space during this shift.


Published in Into the Unknown: The Dungeon Survival Handbook, page(s) 39.
