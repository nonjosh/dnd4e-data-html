Battlemind Utility 22Perfect Eye

You cast your mind out, ignoring all barriers to find your foes.

Daily        Psionic
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, you ignore all barriers to line of effect and line of sight that are within 20 squares of you. In addition, you ignore the -2 penalty for attacking enemies that have concealment or cover within that range.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 54.
