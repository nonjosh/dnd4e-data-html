Sorcerer Attack 17Azure Talons

Blue claws of lightning materialize around you and slash at foes that remain too close.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement, Lightning
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Charisma vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d8 + Charisma modifier lightning damage. If the target ends its next turn within 2 squares of you, it takes 5 lightning damage.

  Dragon Magic: If the target ends its next turn within 2 squares of you, it takes lightning damage equal to 5 + your Strength modifier.


Revision (10/13/2009)
On the Dragon Magic line, replace “Dexterity” with “Strength”.

Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 37.
