Shaman Utility 2Condensation

Moisture gathers in the air around your spirit companion as you fuse its form with one of your allies to provide healing and a protective barrier against the elements.

Daily        Healing, Primal, Spirit
Minor Action      Melee spirit 5

Target: One ally

Effect: Your spirit companion disappears. The target gains resist 10 fire and regeneration equal to your Intelligence modifier until the end of the encounter or until you use call spirit companion.


Published in Dark Sun Campaign Setting, page(s) 91.
