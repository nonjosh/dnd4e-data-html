Shadow Assassin Attack 20Final Blow

Your enemy is seriously wounded. This shot will finish it off.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a crossbow, a light blade, or a sling.

Target: One bloodied creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 5[W] + Dexterity modifier damage, and you shift a number of squares up to your Charisma modifier. You must end this shift adjacent to an enemy.

Miss: Half damage.
