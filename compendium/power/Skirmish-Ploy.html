Combat Veteran Attack 11Skirmish Ploy

You score a glancing blow, which you turn into an opportunity to thrust your enemy out of position and into the path of a nearby ally.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. Reflex

Hit: 1[W] + Strength modifier damage, and the target is dazed until the end of your next turn. In addition, you slide the target up to 2 squares, and an ally within 2 squares of you can shift 1 square closer to the target as a free action.


Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 154, Class Compendium.
