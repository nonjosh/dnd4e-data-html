Exemplar of Evil Utility 30Victory at Any Price

You sacrifice an ally to ensure a swift and certain victory.

Daily  
No Action      Close burst 5

Trigger: You hit an enemy with an at-will or an encounter attack power.

Target: One ally in the burst who is not bloodied

Effect: The target takes damage equal to his or her bloodied value, and your hit becomes a critical hit.


Published in The Book of Vile Darkness, page(s) 1-29.
