Swordmage Utility 16Elemental Protection

You wrap layers of arcane protection into your warding, keeping elemental energy at bay.

Daily        Arcane
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Choose acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder. Until the end of the encounter, you gain resist 10 + your Constitution modifier to that damage type.
