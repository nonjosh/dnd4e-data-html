Rogue Attack 15Whirlwind of Blades

You leap and spin past your foe, nicking it with dozens of cuts.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a light blade.

Target: One creature

Effect: Before and after the attack, you move your speed. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks from the target.

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Dexterity modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 66.
