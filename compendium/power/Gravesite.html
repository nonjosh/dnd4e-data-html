Cleric Utility 6Gravesite

You invoke the power of your god to claim the ground around you. Enemies that attempt to contest that ground pay for their temerity.

Daily        Divine, Healing, Shadow, Zone
Minor Action      Close burst 3

Effect: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of the encounter. You and your allies gain a +2 power bonus to damage rolls against enemies in the zone. Whenever an enemy drops to 0 hit points in the zone, you and each ally in the zone regain 5 hit points.


Published in Heroes of Shadow, page(s) 83.
