Cannith Mastermaker Attack 11Power Siphon

You blast your foe with a golden ray, and streaming wisps of power flow from your enemy into your equipment and into the equipment of nearby allies.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement, Radiant
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. Reflex

Hit: 4d6 + Intelligence modifier radiant damage, and you and each ally within 10 squares of you gain a +2 power bonus to attack rolls or a +2 power bonus to all defenses until the end of your next turn.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 67.
