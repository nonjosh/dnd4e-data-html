Ranger Attack 25Ambusher's Reaping

You leap from the shadows, cut down two foes, and then disappear from view.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Targets: One or two creatures that are surprised or that you are hidden from

Attack: Strength vs. AC (melee) or Dexterity vs. AC (ranged), one attack per target

Hit: 5[W] + Strength modifier + Wisdom modifier damage (melee) or 5[W] + Dexterity modifier + Wisdom modifier damage (ranged), and you can shift your speed. If you are hidden from the target when you make the attack, you remain hidden if you have any cover or concealment.

Miss: Half damage, and you can shift 1 square. You can make a Stealth check with a +2 bonus to remain hidden if you have any cover or concealment.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 55.
