Cleric Attack 25Nimbus of Doom

Your attack illuminates your foe with a radiant glow, guiding future attacks against it.

Daily        Divine, Radiant, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 6[W] + Strength modifier radiant damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The target takes a -2 penalty to all defenses (save ends).
