Monk Attack 19Hare's Panicked Retreat

The power of your forceful strike fills your foe with dread, causing it to knock down other enemies as you control its feeble attempts to escape from you.

Daily        Fear, Implement, Psionic
Standard Action      Melee touch

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d10 + Dexterity modifier damage, and the target is slowed (save ends). Until this effect ends, whenever the target starts its turn, you can push it 2 squares as a free action. Each enemy adjacent to the target at the end of the push falls prone.

Miss: Half damage, and you push the target its speed.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 70.
