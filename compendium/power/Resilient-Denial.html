Dragonfoe Ragespell Utility 12Resilient Denial

After you are hit by a particularly devastating attack, you channel your fury and attune your arcane energies to resist another such strike.

Daily        Arcane
Immediate Reaction      Personal

Trigger: An attack bloodies you

Effect: Choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder damage. You gain resist 15 against that damage type until the end of the encounter.
