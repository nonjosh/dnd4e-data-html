Shaman Attack 7Call to the Howling Storm

Your spirit companion takes on an aspect of howling winds and lightning to strike at your enemy while whirling a nearby ally into a better position.

Encounter        Implement, Lightning, Primal, Spirit, Thunder
Standard Action      Melee spirit 1

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d8 + Wisdom modifier lightning and thunder damage, and one ally adjacent to your spirit companion can shift 5 squares as a free action.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 125.
