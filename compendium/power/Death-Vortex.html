Spellscarred Attack 9Death Vortex

Your flesh bursts into ebony-tipped blue flame, burning away your outer layer of skin as the flame expands to encompass nearby creatures in a miniature sapphire vortex.

Encounter        Arcane, Necrotic
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: Each creature in the burst

Attack: Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d8 + Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma modifier necrotic damage.

Miss: Half damage

Sustain Minor: You can spend a healing surge to repeat the attack against all foes you previously hit (even if no longer in range, although still within line of sight) when you sustain the power. On a hit, the attack instead deals 1d8 necrotic damage and you can slide the target 2 squares.


Published in Dragon Magazine 375, page(s) 83.
