Contract Killer Utility 12Next to Die

You single out a target, pursuing it until it is no more.

Daily        Martial
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Choose one enemy that you can see within 5 squares of you. Until the end of the encounter, you gain combat advantage against that enemy, and you take a -2 penalty to attack rolls against creatures other than it until it drops to 0 hit points. In addition, whenever the enemy moves more than 2 squares on its turn, you can shift up to 2 squares as a free action.


Published in The Book of Vile Darkness, page(s) 1-23.
