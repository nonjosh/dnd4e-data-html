Master of Moments Utility 26Freeze Time

Your attack freezes a foe in a timeless void.

Daily        Psionic
Free Action      Personal

Trigger: You hit an enemy

Target: The enemy you hit

Effect: You freeze the target in time until the end of your next turn. While frozen in time, the target cannot take actions, does not have a start or an end of turn, is not aware of its surroundings, and is immune to all damage and effects.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 149.
