Warlock Attack 3Pipes of Winter

With a gesture, a set of glacial pipes appears at your lips. Blowing on it, you produce a horrid keening that chills your foes to the bone.

Encounter        Arcane, Cold, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 5

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d6 + Charisma modifier cold damage, and the target is immobilized until the end of your next turn.

  Fey Pact: Each enemy adjacent to the target is slowed until the end of your next turn.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 76.
