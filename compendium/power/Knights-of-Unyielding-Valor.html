Cleric Utility 10Knights of Unyielding Valor

You conjure four ghostly knights, which carry shields emblazoned with the symbol of your deity.

Daily        Conjuration, Divine
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Effect: You conjure four ghostly knights, each occupying a different square within range. The knights last until the end of the encounter. They grant partial cover to you and your allies. Enemies can’t enter the knights’ spaces, but you and your allies can move through them. The knights can’t attack or be attacked or damaged.

  Move Action: You move each knight up to 2 squares.


Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 67, Class Compendium.
