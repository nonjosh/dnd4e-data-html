Cleric Attack 5Halo of Consequence

A ring of faint light surrounds your enemy and punishes it for attacking you or your allies.

Daily        Divine, Implement, Reliable
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: The target is affected by your halo of consequence (save ends). Until the halo ends, the target takes a -4 penalty to attack rolls, and after the target attacks you or any ally, it is dazed until the end of its next turn. The target takes a -2 penalty to saving throws against the halo.


Published in Divine Power, page(s) 34.
