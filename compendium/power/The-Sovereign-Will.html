God-Emperor Utility 26The Sovereign Will

Your commanding presence radiates from you to grant authority to your companions.

Encounter        Aura
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You activate an aura 2 that lasts until the end of your next turn. When you or any ally in the aura makes a Diplomacy check, instead of rolling a d20, resolve the check as if that character had rolled a 20.
