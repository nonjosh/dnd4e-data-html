Bard Attack 29Horrifying Truth

Your cruel pronouncement becomes truer with every strike.

Daily        Arcane, Implement
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each enemy in the blast

Attack: Charisma vs. Will

Effect: The target is dazed (save ends).

Hit: 4d8 + Charisma modifier damage. If the target is hit by an attack while it is dazed by this power, it becomes stunned instead (save ends).

Miss: Half damage.
