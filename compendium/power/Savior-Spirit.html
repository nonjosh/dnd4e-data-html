Sorcerer Utility 22Savior Spirit

Your familiar latches onto an ally, then both teleport away.

Encounter        Arcane, Teleportation
Move Action      Close burst 10

Requirement: Your familiar must be in its active mode.

Target: One ally adjacent to your familiar

Effect: Teleport your familiar and the target 10 squares to squares adjacent to one another. Then, you can teleport your familiar 10 squares.


Published in Dragon Magazine 386, page(s) 23.
