Heir of Siberys Utility 26Siberys Mark of Handling

You grant a beast or summoned creature its own mind and will.

Daily  
Standard Action      Personal

Effect: You gain an extra standard action each turn until the end of the encounter. You can use this action only to use an at-will beast power or to command a summoned creature. Alternatively, your mount can use this extra action to attack.
