Warden Utility 10Warding Vines

Spectral vines bloom around you to shield you and your allies.

Daily        Primal, Zone
Minor Action      Close burst 2

Effect: The burst creates a zone of protective vines that lasts until the end of the encounter. While within the zone, you and your allies gain resistance to all damage equal to your Constitution modifier.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 160.
