Wizard Attack 19Mass Transformation

When you utter the powerful command, your foes disappear in plumes of smoke, to be replaced by frogs, mice, hermit crabs, or some other tiny creatures.

Daily        Arcane, Implement, Polymorph, Transmutation
Standard Action      Area burst 2 within 20 squares

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Intelligence vs. Fortitude

Hit: The target turns into a Tiny beast of your choice (save ends).

  Aftereffect: The target takes 3d8 + your Intelligence modifier damage from the transformation.

Miss: The target turns into a Tiny beast of your choice until the end of its next turn.

Effect: As a Tiny beast, the target is dazed, and the only actions it can take are to move its speed or shift. All of the targetâ€™s equipment transforms with it. If it takes damage from any source, this effect ends.
