Warlord Attack 15Warlord’s Gambit

You provoke your adversary with a bold stroke. Each time it lunges at you, it recklessly sets up your forces for victory.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, the target gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls against you, and whenever it attacks you, choose an ally within a number of squares of you equal to your Intelligence modifier. That ally can make a basic attack against the target as an immediate interrupt.


Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 150, Class Compendium.
