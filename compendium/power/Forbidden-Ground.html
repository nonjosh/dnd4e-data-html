Warlord Utility 16Forbidden Ground

You declare the space in front of you to be forbidden, and any enemy that dares to enter it will suffer the consequences.

Daily        Martial
Standard Action      Close blast 3

Effect: The blast creates an area of forbidden ground. Until the end of the encounter, whenever an enemy enters the area, you or an ally can charge that enemy or make a melee basic attack against it as an immediate reaction.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 91.
