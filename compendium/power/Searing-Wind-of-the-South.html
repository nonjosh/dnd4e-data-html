Shaman Attack 15Searing Wind of the South

The first wind of the south blew through a trackless desert, scattering sand and gusting with bone-charring heat. Its spirit still races across the world, and you call it forth to aid you in battle.

Daily        Fire, Implement, Primal
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each enemy in the blast

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 4d6 + Wisdom modifier fire damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You slide each ally in the blast to another space in or adjacent to the blast.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 127.
