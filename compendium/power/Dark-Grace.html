You shine with a soft golden light, inspiring allies at your side and drawing their life force to you.

Daily        Aura, Shadow
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You activate an aura 1 that lasts until the end of the encounter. Whenever you or an ally in the aura hits an enemy, he or she gains 5 temporary hit points. Whenever an ally in the aura spends a healing surge, he or she regains only half the hit points, and you regain the rest.
