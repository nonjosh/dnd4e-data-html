Demonslayer Attack 11Demonslayer’s Smite

You intone a prayer over your weapon, empowering it to overwhelm demonic defenses.

Encounter        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC. If the target is a demon, you gain a +2 bonus to the attack roll.

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and the target loses its resistances until the end of your next turn.

Special: Whenever a demon within 10 squares of you scores a critical hit or spends an action point, you regain the use of this power.


Published in Divine Power, page(s) 96.
