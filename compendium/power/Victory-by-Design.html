Warlord Attack 29Victory by Design

Leading with an attack announces the start of your cunning stratagem.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage

Effect: Choose up to four allies within 10 squares of you that can see and hear you. Each ally can either make a charge attack, make a basic attack, or shift up to his or her speed as a free action. The allies that choose to charge or make basic attacks deal 1d10 extra damage on a hit and cannot attack the same target or the target of this attack.


Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 56.
