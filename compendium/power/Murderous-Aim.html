Ranger Attack 9Murderous Aim

A moment’s careful study reveals to you a vast array of weak points to attack.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Ranged weapon

Target: One creature designated as your quarry

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Dexterity modifier damage.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, when you deal Hunter’s Quarry damage to this target, you can reroll any Hunter’s Quarry damage die result of 1 or 2 until it shows a value of 3 or higher.
