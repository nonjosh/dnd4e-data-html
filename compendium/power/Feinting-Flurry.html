Rogue Attack 19Feinting Flurry

A series of clever feints throws your foe off his game and makes it an easy target.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a crossbow, a light blade, or a sling.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Will

Hit: 5[W] + Dexterity modifier damage.

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, the target takes a penalty to all defenses against your attacks. The penalty equals your Charisma modifier.

Sustain Minor: The penalty persists until the end of your next turn.
