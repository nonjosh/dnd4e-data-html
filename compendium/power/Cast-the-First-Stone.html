Invoker Attack 9Cast the First Stone

A single stone splits into many shards that pelt your enemies and drive them to their knees.

Daily        Divine, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d10 + Wisdom modifier damage, and the target is knocked prone.

Effect: Each enemy adjacent to the target takes 5 damage and slides 1 square to a square not adjacent to the target.


Published in Dragon Magazine 383, page(s) 34.
