Paladin Utility 6Wrath of the Gods

You extend the power of your virtue to nearby allies, enabling you and your friends to easily strike down your enemies.

Daily        Divine
Minor Action      Close burst 1

Targets: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, each target gains a power bonus to damage rolls equal to your Charisma modifier.
