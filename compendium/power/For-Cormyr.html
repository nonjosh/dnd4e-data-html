Purple Dragon Knight Attack 20For Cormyr!

Your battle cry becomes a chant as one after another of your allies join in. The enemy recoils in fear.

Daily        Fear, Martial
Standard Action      Close burst 5

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Charisma +5 vs. Will

Hit: You push the target 2 squares. The target takes a –2 penalty to attack rolls, all defenses, and saving throws (save ends).

Effect: Each ally in the burst gains 25 temporary hit points.

Miss: The target takes a –2 penalty to attack rolls, all defenses, and saving throws (save ends).


Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 60.
