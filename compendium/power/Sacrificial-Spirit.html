Shaman Utility 10Sacrificial Spirit

You temporarily sap your spirit companion of its strength, transferring its power to bolster the life force of an ally.

Daily        Primal, Spirit
Free Action      Personal

Effect: Each ally adjacent to your spirit companion regains a healing surge, and your spirit companion is immediately dismissed and cannot be conjured again until the start of your next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 372, page(s) 22.
