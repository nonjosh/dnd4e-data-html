Rogue Utility 10Acrobat's Escape

As an enemy tries to move close, you slip away and leave the approaching foe off balance.

Encounter        Martial
Immediate Reaction      Melee 1

Prerequisite: You must have training in Acrobatics.

Trigger: An enemy enters a square adjacent to you.

Target: The triggering enemy

Effect: The target is immobilized until the start of its next turn. You then shift a number of squares up to your Dexterity modifier to a square that is not adjacent to the target.


Published in Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 184.
