Warlock Attack 5Lure of Gibbeth

A fleeting thought of Gibbeth the Endless, an unknowable entity slain at the birth of the world but poised to return at the world’s ending, seizes your enemy’s mind and draws your foe toward you like a moth to flame.

Daily        Arcane, Charm, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Will

Hit: 2d10 + Charisma modifier psychic damage, and you pull the target 4 squares.

Miss: Half damage, and you pull the target 2 squares.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, you can pull the target 2 squares as a minor action once during your turn.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 76.
