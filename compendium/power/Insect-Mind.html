Thri-Kreen Racial Utility 10Insect Mind

Your instincts take over as your higher mental functions fail you.

Daily      Psionic
No Action      Personal

Trigger: You start your turn dazed, dominated, stunned, or unconscious, and you have at least 1 hit point.

Effect: You ignore the triggering effect until the end of this turn.

Special: If you have the Thri-Kreen Wild Talent feat, you can sustain this effect until the end of your next turn by taking a minor action and spending 1 power point.


Published in Dragon Magazine 411.
