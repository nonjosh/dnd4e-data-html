Runepriest Utility 6Rune of Meritorious Alacrity

As battle begins, you invoke the rune of meritorious alacrity, bringing your friends instantly to readiness.

Daily        Divine
Free Action      Ranged sight

Trigger: You roll initiative

Target: You and each ally

Effect: Each target gains a +10 bonus to his or her initiative check.
