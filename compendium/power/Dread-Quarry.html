Reaving Axe Savant Utility 12Dread Quarry

You spy a new enemy and swear an oath to bring it down.

Daily        Martial
Minor Action      Ranged sight

Target: The nearest enemy to you that you can see

Effect: Using your Hunter’s Quarry, you designate the target as your quarry. Until the target is no longer your quarry, your Hunter’s Quarry deals one extra die of Hunter’s Quarry damage to the target.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 51.
