Ardent Attack 19Corridor of Pain

The power of your devastating strike resonates within a foe, letting you continually fling it into optimal position for your allies to attack.

Daily        Psionic, Teleportation, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Charisma modifier damage. You slide the target 5 squares, and it is restrained (save ends).

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, whenever the target takes damage from an attack, you teleport the target 5 squares to a destination adjacent to at least one of your allies.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 19.
