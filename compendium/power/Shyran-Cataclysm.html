Anarch of Shyr Attack 20Shyran Cataclysm

You slice a rift between the planes. An elemental maelstrom explodes from the rift, searing and burning all in its path.

Daily        Acid, Arcane, Cold, Fire, Implement, Lightning, Thunder
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Intelligence vs. AC

Hit: 5d8 + Intelligence modifier acid, cold, fire, lightning, and thunder damage.

Miss: Half damage. Choose one damage type for the half damage: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder.
