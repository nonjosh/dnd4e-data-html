Rogue Attack 23Killer's Retreat

You jab your weapon into tender flesh, forcing the opponent to let you slip away.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a light blade.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Dexterity modifier damage, and if you were marked, you are no longer marked. You shift 2 squares.

  Cunning Sneak: You instead shift a number of squares equal to 1 + your Intelligence modifier, and you can make a Stealth check to become hidden after the shift.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 69.
