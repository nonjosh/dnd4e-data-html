Heir of Siberys Utility 26Siberys Mark of Storm

Howling winds swirl around you and thunder rumbles in the sky.

Daily        Zone
Standard Action      Close burst 3

Effect: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of the encounter. The zone is difficult terrain for creatures on the ground. When a creature flies to a square within the zone, you can slide that creature a number of squares equal to its fly speed as an opportunity action, and then its movement ends. You can fly your speed and hover while within the zone. In addition, thunder and lightning attacks deal 1d10 extra damage against targets within the zone.


Published in Dragon Magazine 388.
