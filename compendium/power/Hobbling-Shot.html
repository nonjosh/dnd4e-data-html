Ranger Attack 23Hobbling Shot

The enemy wobbles from your shot and can move only poorly for a moment.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Ranged weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Dexterity modifier damage, and the target is slowed until the start of your next turn. If the target is your quarry, it is instead immobilized until the start of your next turn.
