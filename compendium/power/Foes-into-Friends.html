Satyr Racial Utility 16Foes into Friends

You draw an enemy back from the brink of death, holding it under your spell.

Daily      Charm
Immediate Interrupt      Ranged 10

Trigger: An enemy within 10 squares of you drops below 1 hit point.

Target: The triggering enemy

Effect: The target instead has 1 hit point, and it is dominated by you until the end of the encounter. The target cannot regain hit points, and a missed attack cannot damage it. If the target still has 1 hit point at the end of the encounter, it drops to 0 hit points. You choose whether it dies or is unconscious.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 39.
