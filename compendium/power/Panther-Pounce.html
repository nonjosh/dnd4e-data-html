Wildcat Stalker Attack 20Panther Pounce

At your signal, your cat launches at your enemy, tearing with tooth and claw.

Daily        Beast, Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee beast 1

Target: One creature designated as your quarry

Attack: Beast’s attack bonus vs. AC

Hit: 2[B] + beast’s Dexterity modifier damage. If your beast companion was hidden from the target at the start of your turn, the attack deals 3[B] extra damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Before and after the attack, your beast companion can shift its speed, ignoring difficult terrain.
