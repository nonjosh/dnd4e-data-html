Rogue Attack 25Gory Slash

Your bladed savagery opens a gushing wound that cripples your foe with pain.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a light blade.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Dexterity modifier damage, and the target takes ongoing 10 damage and is immobilized (save ends both).

Miss: Half damage, and ongoing 5 damage (save ends). The target is immobilized until the start of your next turn.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 85.
