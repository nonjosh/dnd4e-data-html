Steadfast Sentinel Utility 12Bounty of Spring

The primal spirits grant you and your allies a healing boon.

Daily        Healing, Primal
Minor Action      Close burst 10

Target: You and one ally in the burst

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, each target has regeneration 5 while bloodied. As a minor action, a target can end this regeneration on himself or herself to spend a healing surge and regain additional hit points equal to your Wisdom modifier.


Published in Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms.
