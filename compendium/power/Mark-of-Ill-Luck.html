Runepriest Attack 19Mark of Ill Luck

You place the mark of ill luck on your foe. The mark ensures that failed attacks ricochet onto that foe.

Daily        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, the target takes 5 damage both when a ranged attack against a creature within 5 squares of it misses and when a melee attack against a creature adjacent to it misses.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 109.
