Bard Attack 17Greater Echoing Weapon

Your touch causes vibrations to shoot from one end of the weapon to the other, filling the air with a low rumble.

Encounter        Arcane, Thunder
Minor Action      Melee 1

Target: One weapon

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, the next weapon attack made with the target weapon deals 3d6 thunder damage to one target of the attack, whether the attack hits or misses.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 65.
