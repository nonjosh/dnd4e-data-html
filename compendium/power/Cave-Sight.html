Arcana Utility 2Cave Sight

You close your eyes, projecting arcane awareness to give you a mental picture of the cavern ahead.

Daily
Minor Action      Close burst 20

Effect: You learn the general layout of terrain features in the burst. You cannot sense through solid objects, such as cave walls, but you do sense around corners and into narrow gaps. Additionally, you can make a Perception check to detect hidden creatures, objects, and traps as though you were within 10 squares of them.


Published in Into the Unknown: The Dungeon Survival Handbook, page(s) 57.
