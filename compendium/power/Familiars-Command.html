Familiar Keeper Attack 20Familiar's Command

Your familiar turns into a mist of arcane energy and flows into your foe, taking over its mind.

Daily        Arcane, Charm, Implement
Standard Action      Melee 1

Special: Your familiar must be in active mode, and the attack comes from the familiar’s square instead of yours.

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma vs. Will

Hit: The target is dominated (save ends). Your familiar returns to passive mode, and it can’t return to active mode while the target is dominated.

Miss: You can choose to return your familiar to passive mode. If you do, this power is not expended.

Special: When you gain this power, choose Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma as the ability score you use when making attack rolls with this power.


Published in Dragon Magazine 374, page(s) 32.
