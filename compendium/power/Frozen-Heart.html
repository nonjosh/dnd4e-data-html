Long Night Scion Attack 20Frozen Heart

Your deadly chill freezes the very blood of your foe.

Daily        Arcane, Cold, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d8 + Charisma modifier cold damage, and the target is dazed and slowed (save ends both). First Failed Saving Throw: The target is stunned instead of dazed and slowed (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and the target is slowed (save ends).
