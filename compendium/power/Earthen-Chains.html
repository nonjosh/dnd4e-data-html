Cleric Attack 23Earthen Chains

As you strike your foe, you intone a prayer to the gods which causes chains of stone to wrap around your enemy.

Encounter        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3[W] + Wisdom modifier damage.

Effect: The target is knocked prone and cannot stand until the end of your next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 392.
