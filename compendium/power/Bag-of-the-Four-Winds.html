Artificer Utility 22Bag of the Four Winds

You mix reagents to create a windstorm within a small cloth bag. You then unleash the winds to move yourself and your allies.

Encounter        Arcane
Minor Action      Close burst 5

Targets: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: You slide each target a number of squares equal to your Wisdom modifier.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 55.
