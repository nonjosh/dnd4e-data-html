Druid Attack 15Devouring Flies

You conjure a horde of ravenous horseflies, which bite your foes and transfer vitality to you.

Daily        Healing, Implement, Primal, Zone
Standard Action      Area burst 2 within 10 squares

Target: Each creature in the burst

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d6 + Wisdom modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The burst creates a zone of horseflies that lasts until the end of the encounter. When any enemy starts its turn within the zone, you or an ally of your choice within 5 squares of you regains 6 hit points.


Published in Primal Power, page(s) 46.
