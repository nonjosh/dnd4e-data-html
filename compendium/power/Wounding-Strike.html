Rogue Attack 15Wounding Strike

You strike, intending to inflict grave injury.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a crossbow, a light blade, or a sling.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Dexterity modifier damage. If the target is not bloodied before you make this attack, the attack also deals ongoing 10 damage (save ends).

Miss: Half damage.
