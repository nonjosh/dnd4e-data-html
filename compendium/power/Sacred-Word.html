Cleric Attack 25Sacred Word

You utter a single word of divine power that damages and stuns the enemies around you.

Daily        Divine, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 4d10 + Wisdom modifier psychic damage, and the target is stunned until the end of your next turn.

Miss: Half damage.
