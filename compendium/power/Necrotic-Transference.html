Cleric Attack 19Necrotic Transference

You swathe your weapon in necromantic energy, draining the life from an enemy and transferring it to your ally.

Daily        Divine, Necrotic, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. Will

Hit: 4[W] + Charisma modifier necrotic damage and one ally within 10 squares of you regains one healing surge.

Miss: Half damage.
