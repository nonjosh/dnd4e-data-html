Swordmage Utility 6Unicorn's Touch

Your blade briefly adopts the appearance of a unicorn horn, sending out a focused pulse of curative energy.

Daily        Arcane, Healing
Minor Action      Close burst 1

Target: You or one ally in the burst

Effect: The target either rolls a saving throw or regains hit points equal to 5 + your Constitution modifier.
