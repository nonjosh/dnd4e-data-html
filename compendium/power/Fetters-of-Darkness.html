Invoker Attack 23Fetters of Darkness

You tether your enemies with shadowy manacles.

Encounter        Divine, Implement, Necrotic, Teleportation
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One or two creatures

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d8 + Wisdom modifier necrotic damage. You teleport the target 5 squares, and it is immobilized until the end of your next turn. If you target only one creature, the attack deals 1d8 extra necrotic damage, and you teleport the target 10 squares instead of 5.


Published in Divine Power, page(s) 66.
