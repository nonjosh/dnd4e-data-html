Sharpshooter Utility 12Perfect Aim

You take the time to line up a deadly shot.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Minor Action      Personal

Requirement: You must be wielding a ranged weapon.

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, your next ranged attack gains a power bonus to the attack and damage rolls equal to your Wisdom modifier. If you move or take damage before making a ranged attack, the bonus is lost.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 67.
