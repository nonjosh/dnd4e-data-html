Druid Utility 16Wall of Stone

A granite wall emerges from the ground as you direct.

Daily        Conjuration, Primal
Standard Action      Area wall 12 within 10 squares

Effect: You conjure a wall of rough stone. The wall can be up to 6 squares high and must be on a solid surface. The wall is a solid obstacle. Each square of the wall has 100 hit points and crumbles into difficult terrain if it is destroyed. The whole wall crumbles into difficult terrain at the end of the encounter.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 91.
