Rogue Utility 10Counter-Step

As your foe bears down on you, you use its momentum to maneuver it into position for your counterattack.

Encounter        Martial
Immediate Reaction      Melee 1

Prerequisite: You must have training in Athletics.

Trigger: An enemy enters a square adjacent to you.

Target: The triggering enemy

Effect: You can slide the target 1 square. You then shift up to 2 squares to a square adjacent to the target. You gain combat advantage and a +2 power bonus to all defenses against the target until the end of your next turn.


Published in Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 184.
