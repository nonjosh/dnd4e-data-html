Rogue Utility 6Fortuitous Dodge

You duck out of the way of an incoming attack, and it strikes another target.

Daily        Martial
Immediate Reaction      Melee 1

Trigger: A melee or a ranged attack misses you

Target: One creature other than the attacker

Effect: The target is also targeted by the triggering attack. You can then shift 1 square.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 77.
