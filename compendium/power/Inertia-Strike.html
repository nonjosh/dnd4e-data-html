Fighter Attack 7Inertia Strike

Your tremendous swing carries your weapon through one enemy and into anyone else in your reach.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a two-handed weapon.

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength -2 vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage, and any enemy adjacent to you other than the target takes damage equal to your Constitution modifier.
