Huntmaster Attack 20Transfixing Spear

Your spear skewers your quarry, pinning it in place.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a spear.

Target: One creature designated as your quarry

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and the target is immobilized until the end of your next turn. During your next turn, you can use this power against the target again.

Miss: Half damage.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 49.
