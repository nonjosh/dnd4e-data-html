Warlord Attack 13Pincer Maneuver

At your command, two of your allies move to flank a foe.

Encounter        Martial
Standard Action      Close burst 5

Targets: One or two allies in the burst

Effect: Each target can shift 3 squares and make a melee basic attack against an adjacent enemy as a free action. If the target is flanking the enemy, he or she gains a bonus to the damage roll equal to your Charisma modifier.

  Bravura Presence: The target gains a power bonus to the attack roll equal to your Charisma modifier.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 110.
