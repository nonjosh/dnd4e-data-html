Entrancing Mystic Attack 20Ultimatum of the Third Order

You speak in a secret tongue, assaulting the minds of your foes and presenting them with a mental dilemma.

Daily        Arcane, Charm, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Close burst 5

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Charisma vs. Will

Hit: The target chooses either to be stunned until the end of your next turn, or to take ongoing 20 psychic damage (save ends).

Miss: The target chooses either to be dazed until the end of your next turn, or to take ongoing 10 psychic damage (save ends).
