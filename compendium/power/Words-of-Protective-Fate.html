Bard Utility 2Words of Protective Fate

Your voice shields your allies against misfortune, turning otherwise deadly blows into less damaging strikes.

Daily        Arcane
Minor Action      Close burst 10

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, whenever a critical hit is scored on the target, you roll a d20. If you roll a 10 or higher, the attack hits but is not a critical hit.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 8.
