Fighter Utility 22Surprising Finish

As you release your grip on a foe, you smash your hand into its face, sending the creature reeling.

Daily        Martial
Free Action      Melee 1

Trigger: You stop grabbing an enemy

Target: The enemy you released

Effect: You knock the target prone, and the target is dazed (save ends).


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 18.
