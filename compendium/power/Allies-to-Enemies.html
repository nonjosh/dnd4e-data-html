Battlemind Attack 1Allies to Enemies

Your attack rattles your foe with a vision of its allies betraying it. The foe lashes out at a companion before realizing its mistake.

Daily        Charm, Psionic, Psychic, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Constitution vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Constitution modifier psychic damage, and the target makes a melee basic attack as a free action against a creature of your choice.

Miss: Half damage.
