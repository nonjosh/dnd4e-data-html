Entrancing Mystic Utility 12Shroud of Adeptus

Your enemy moves to strike you, but you stare into its eyes and rob the creature of its will.

Daily        Arcane, Charm
Immediate Reaction      Ranged 5

Trigger: An enemy within 5 squares of you misses you with an attack

Target: The triggering enemy

Effect: The target is dazed until the end of your next turn.
