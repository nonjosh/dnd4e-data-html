Monk Attack 19Rising Dragon Fire

You exhale an inferno to burn your enemies.

Daily        Fire, Implement, Psionic, Zone
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: Each creature in the burst

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 5d6 + Dexterity modifier fire damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of your next turn. When you hit any creature that is within the zone, that creature takes 2d6 extra fire damage.

Sustain Minor: The zone persists.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 73.
