Warlord Attack 15Comrade in Arms

As you and your ally work together to defeat a foe, you both develop a bond that provides an advantage throughout combat.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Immediate Reaction      Melee or Ranged weapon

Trigger: An enemy is hit by your ally

Target: The triggering enemy

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, whenever you hit an enemy, the ally gains a power bonus to his or her next attack roll against that enemy. The bonus equals your Intelligence modifier or Wisdom modifier and lasts until the end of your next turn.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 91.
