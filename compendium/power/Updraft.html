Druid Utility 10Updraft

A gust of wind swirls around you, lifting you into the air.

Daily        Primal
Move Action      Ranged 10

Target: You or one ally

Effect: The target flies up to 5 squares.

Sustain Move: The target flies again, or you use the power on a different target.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 81.
