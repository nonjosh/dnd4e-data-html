Monk Attack 5Overwhelming Mountain Strike

Your strike against a pressure point robs your enemy of its ability to maneuver effectively. Until it recovers, you keep it pinned down.

Daily        Implement, Psionic
Standard Action      Melee touch

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + Dexterity modifier damage, and the target is dazed (save ends).

  Aftereffect: The target is slowed until the end of its next turn.

Miss: Half damage, and the target is slowed until the end of your next turn.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 73.
