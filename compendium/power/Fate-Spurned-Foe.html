Swordmage Utility 6Fate-Spurned Foe

Forces beyond your enemy’s comprehension work against it at your command.

Daily        Arcane
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, any effect that a save can end that you place on an enemy causes a –2 penalty to saving throws against the effect.


Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 29.
