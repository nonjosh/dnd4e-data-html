Warlock Utility 2Spider Scuttle

Shadowy spider legs sprout from your body, allowing you to scuttle across the ground and easily scale a nearby surface.

Encounter        Arcane
Free Action      Personal

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, you gain a climb speed equal to your speed, and you can move at that speed when you crawl.
