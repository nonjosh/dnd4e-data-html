Perfect Guardian Utility 26Perfect Guardian's Pounce

You leap forward to absorb an attack meant for your friend.

Encounter        Primal
Immediate Interrupt      Personal

Trigger: A melee or a ranged attack hits an ally within 5 squares of you

Effect: You shift a number of squares equal to twice your speed to a square adjacent to the creature that made the triggering attack. The attack hits you instead, and you mark the creature that made it until the end of your next turn.


Update (4/13/2010)
“Perfect Guardian’s Pounce” (Utility 26) should have “until the end of your next turn” at the end of the last sentence. In the Compendium, “Guardian’s Pounce” (Attack 7), should have “until the end of your next turn” deleted from the Effect entry.
