Fighter Attack 1Takedown Attack

You bash your foe with a vicious attack and then drag the creature down to the ground.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee 1

Requirement: You must have a hand free.

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. Fortitude

Hit: 1[W] + Strength modifier damage, and you knock the target prone.

Special: When charging, you can use this power in place of a melee basic attack.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 8.
