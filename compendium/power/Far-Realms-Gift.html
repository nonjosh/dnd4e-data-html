Alienist Attack 11Far Realm's Gift

You bestow the horror of the Far Realm on your foe, transforming it into a creature of nightmare.

Encounter        Augmentable, Implement, Polymorph, Psionic, Psychic
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. Will

Hit: 1d12 + Intelligence modifier psychic damage, and the target assumes the form of a Far Realm horror until the end of your next turn. While in this form, the target is slowed, takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls, and cannot make opportunity attacks. Any enemy that enters a square adjacent to the horror or ends its turn there takes 5 psychic damage. An enemy can take this damage only once per turn.

Augment 2

Range: Area burst 1 within 10 squares

Target: Each creature in the burst
