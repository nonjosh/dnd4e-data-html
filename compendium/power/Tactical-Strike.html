With a deft step, you flank your foe and give your ally heart.

Encounter        Divine, Healing, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Effect: You can shift 1 square before the attack.

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Wisdom modifier damage.

Effect: One ally flanking the target with you can spend a healing surge. Until the end of your next turn, you and that ally gain a +2 power bonus to attack rolls against enemies that you both flank.
