Verdant Stalker Utility 12Forest Specter

Moving among foliage or around a cavern wall, you blend into your surroundings like a spirit of nature.

Encounter        Martial
Minor Action      Personal

Requirement: You must have total concealment or superior cover.

Effect: You become invisible until the end of your next turn, until you attack, or until you no longer have any cover or any concealment.
