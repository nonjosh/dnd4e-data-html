Warden Utility 22Eagle's Wings

Black wings with white tips extend from your back, letting you catch the wind and fly.

Daily        Polymorph, Primal
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You sprout wings that last until the end of the encounter. While you have the wings, you have a fly speed equal to your speed, and you can hover.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 162.
