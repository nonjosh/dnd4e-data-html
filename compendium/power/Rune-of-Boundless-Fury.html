Runepriest Attack 9Rune of Boundless Fury

Kord first carved this rune, cutting it into the flesh of a primordial beast. His sacred berserkers, their wrath fired by the bloody symbol, overwhelmed the foe.

Daily        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, whenever any ally starts his or her turn adjacent to the target, that ally can make a melee basic attack against the target as a free action.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 105.
