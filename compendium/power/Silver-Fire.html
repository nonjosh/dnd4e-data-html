You summon a jet of pure magical power that burns through all defenses and empowers your other spells.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 20

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence +8 vs. Reflex

Hit: 1d6 + Intelligence modifier damage, and until the end of the encounter, each subsequent time you hit the target with an attack, it takes additional damage equal to onehalf your level.
