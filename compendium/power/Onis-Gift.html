Swordmage Utility 22Oni's Gift

Like the oni of children’s fairy tales, you can disappear from view until you are ready to strike.

Daily        Arcane, Illusion
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You are invisible until the end of your next turn or until you attack.

Sustain Minor: You stay invisible or, if you are no longer invisible because you attacked, you become invisible again.


Revision (10/13/2009)
On the Effect line, replace “start” with “end”.

Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 61.
