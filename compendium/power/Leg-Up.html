Goblin Racial Utility 2Leg Up

By bouncing off or scrabbling up the body of a nearby creature, you fling yourself through the air.

Encounter
Move Action      Personal

Requirement: You must be adjacent to a creature.

Effect: You jump up to your speed horizontally or up to 10 feet vertically.


Published in Into the Unknown: The Dungeon Survival Handbook, page(s) 39.
