Blood-Crazed Berserker Attack 20Maniacal Fury

Your towering wrath transforms you into a whirlwind of destruction. Relentless and uncaring, you strike out again and again.

Daily        Martial, Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Requirement: You must be bloodied.

Effect: You assume the maniacal fury stance. Until the stance ends, your at-will attacks using a melee weapon deal 1[W] extra damage, and when any creature ends its turn adjacent to you, you must make a melee basic attack against that creature as a free action.


Published in The Book of Vile Darkness, page(s) 1-22.
