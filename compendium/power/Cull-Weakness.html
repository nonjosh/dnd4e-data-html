Barbarian Utility 6Cull Weakness

Only the strong survive, and you let your enemies know that you are a survivor as you strike down the weakest among them with ease. The others quail before you as their wounds mount.

Daily        Primal, Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You assume the cull weakness stance. Until the stance ends, bloodied enemies grant combat advantage to you, and you gain a +4 power bonus to damage rolls against them.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 48.
