Stealth Utility 16Deepening Gloom

Using the surroundings, you obscure yourself from your attacker.

Encounter
Immediate Interrupt      Personal

Trigger: An enemy hits you with a melee or a ranged attack and you have cover or concealment against it

Effect: You become invisible to the triggering enemy until the start of your next turn.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 175.
