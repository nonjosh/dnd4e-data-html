Swordmage Utility 22Boundless

You wreath yourself in magic and unleash a burst of energy that lets you overcome even the most paralyzing afflictions.

Daily        Arcane
No Action      Personal

Effect: End any effects on you that impose the following conditions: grabbed, immobilized, restrained, slowed, and petrified. Until the end of the encounter, you gain a +5 power bonus to saving throws against any effects that include those conditions.
