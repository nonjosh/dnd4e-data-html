Monk Attack 3Springing Drake Assault [Attack Technique]

You can leap over a foe with no fear of reprisal, then deliver an attack whose momentum drives an enemy wherever you want it to go.

Encounter        Full Discipline, Implement, Psionic
Standard Action      Melee touch

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d10 + Dexterity modifier damage, and you slide the target 2 squares.

  Iron Soul: If you make the attack using a light blade or a spear, you slide the target a number of squares equal to your Constitution modifier.


Springing Drake Assault [Movement Technique]

Encounter      Full Discipline, Psionic
Move Action      Personal

Target: One creature adjacent to you

Effect: You jump to any square adjacent to the target. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 63.
