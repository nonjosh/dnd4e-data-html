Warlord Attack 17We Will Not Fail

Your wild strike distracts your foe, giving an ally the chance to strike the creature.

Encounter        Martial
Free Action      Close burst 10

Trigger: You miss an enemy with an attack

Target: One ally in the burst

Effect: The target can make a basic attack against the enemy as a free action. The attack deals extra damage equal to 5 + your Intelligence modifier.
