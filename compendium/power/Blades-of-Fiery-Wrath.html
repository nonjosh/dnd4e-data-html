Swordmage Attack 3Blades of Fiery Wrath

The ambient magic of your unsuccessful attack manifests as countless blades of fire that lash out at nearby enemies.

Encounter        Arcane, Fire, Implement
Free Action      Close burst 1

Trigger: You used a swordmage at-will attack power this turn and did not hit any target with it

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Intelligence vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d6 + Intelligence modifier fire damage.
