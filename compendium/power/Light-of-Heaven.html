Redeemed Drow Utility 26Light of Heaven

The pain of your injuries unleashes the ire of your patron. You will see this battle to its end.

Daily        Healing
Free Action      Personal

Trigger: You are bloodied by an attack.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, you gain regeneration 15, shed bright light in a 10 square radius, and grant all allies within the light resist 10 necrotic.


Published in Dragon Magazine 367, page(s) 41.
