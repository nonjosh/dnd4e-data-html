Satyr Racial Utility 2Satyr’s Leap

You crouch and spring to catch your foes off guard, putting your powerful legs to good use.

At-Will
Move Action      Personal

Effect: You jump with a +10 power bonus to the Athletics check. When you make this jump, you are always considered to have a running start. The distance you jump cannot exceed your speed.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 38.
