Ranger Attack 29Culling Out

You hook your target with your weapon after you hit, and quickly drag it away from its allies.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 5[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You shift a number of squares equal to your Wisdom modifier and pull the target adjacent to you.

Special: When charging, you can use this power in place of a melee basic attack.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 45.
