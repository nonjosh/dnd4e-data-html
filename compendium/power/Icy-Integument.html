Sorcerer Utility 16Icy Integument

You armor yourself with ice covered in sharp icicles. You radiate bitter cold, slowing your attackers.

Encounter        Arcane, Aura, Cold, Elemental
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You activate an aura 1 that lasts until the end of your next turn. Any creature in the aura that makes a melee attack against you takes cold damage equal to 10 + your Charisma modifier, and it is slowed until the end of its next turn. While the aura is active, you have partial cover.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 95.
