Shaman Attack 5War Chieftain's Blessing

The spirit of a regal warrior clad in a chieftain’s robes utters a great battle cry and hews at your foe with an axe. Your allies, heartened by the chieftain’s efforts, redouble their attacks.

Daily        Implement, Primal
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Will

Hit: 2d10 + Wisdom modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, you and your allies gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls against the target.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 124.
