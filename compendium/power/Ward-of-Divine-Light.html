Invoker Utility 22Ward of Divine Light

You shroud yourself in a column of blinding light that sustains your life and shields you from harm.

Daily        Divine, Healing
Standard Action      Personal

Effect: You can spend two healing surges, and you are immune to all damage until the start of your next turn. If you willingly attack before the end of your next turn, you take damage equal to your bloodied value.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 111.
