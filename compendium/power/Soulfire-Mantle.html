Arbiter of Forgotten Justice Attack 20Soulfire Mantle

Divine wrath pours from your soul and wraps itself around your foe in a searing shroud of justice.

Daily        Divine, Implement, Radiant
Standard Action      Ranged 20

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d6 + Wisdom modifier radiant damage.

Effect: When the target fails a saving throw, it takes 10 radiant damage and you can teleport it 3 squares (save ends).

Aftereffect: Ongoing 10 radiant damage (save ends).
