Artificer Utility 2Arcane Anchor

You trigger latent enchantments placed on yourself or an ally to resist being pushed around the area.

Encounter        Arcane
Immediate Interrupt      Ranged 10

Trigger: You or an ally are affected by a push, pull, or slide effect

Target: The triggering creature

Effect: The forced movement is negated.


Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 57.
