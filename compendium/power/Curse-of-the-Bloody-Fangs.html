Warlock Attack 5Curse of the Bloody Fangs

You call up a pack of ferocious, phantasmal beasts from the darkest and most savage depths of the Feywild. Only their slavering fangs appear in this world, snapping and rending in a mad frenzy at the foe you have cursed.

Daily        Arcane, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d10 + Charisma modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The target is subjected to bloody fangs (save ends). Until the effect ends, you can use a minor action once per round, starting on your next turn, to deal 1d10 damage to the target and each of your enemies adjacent to it.
