Hobgoblin Racial Utility 10Plug the Gap

When a foe tries to escape, you move to intercept it or block its path.

Encounter
Immediate Interrupt      Personal

Trigger: An enemy within 3 squares of you moves without shifting.

Effect: You shift up to 3 squares to a square adjacent to the triggering enemy. You then mark that enemy until the end of your next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 419.
