Sorcerer Attack 25Fury of Dragotha

A vision of Dragotha, an undead dragon, fills your sight. You borrow a fragment of its breath weapon to unleash a rotting blast.

Daily        Arcane, Implement, Necrotic
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Charisma vs. Reflex

Hit: 5d8 + Charisma modifier necrotic damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Dragon Magic: Until the end of the encounter, each creature that starts its turn adjacent to you takes necrotic damage equal to your Strength modifier.
