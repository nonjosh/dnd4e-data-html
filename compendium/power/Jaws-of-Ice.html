Druid Attack 15Jaws of Ice

Spirits of cold and winter swirl around you and trap enemies in ice that promises a slow demise.

Daily        Aura, Beast Form, Cold, Elemental, Implement, Primal
Standard Action      Close burst 1

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d10 + Wisdom modifier cold damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You activate an aura 1 that lasts until the end of the encounter or until you dismiss it as a minor action. Enemies are slowed and cannot shift while in the aura. In addition, any enemy that ends its turn in the aura takes 10 cold damage and is grabbed.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 64.
