Rogue Utility 16Raise the Stakes

You focus on bringing your foe down quickly at the expense of your own safety.

Daily        Martial
Minor Action      Personal

Prerequisite: You must have training in Bluff.

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, you can score a critical hit on a roll of 17–20, and any attack against you can score a critical hit on a roll of 19–20.
