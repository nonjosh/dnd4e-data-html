Paladin Attack 23Censuring Radiance

Your weapon sears a sigil into your foe that hinders its attacks against your companions.

Encounter        Divine, Radiant, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3[W] + Charisma modifier radiant damage. Until the end of your next turn, whenever the target makes an attack that doesn’t include you as a target, the target is weakened for that attack.


Update (11/23/2009)
On the Hit line, replace “Strength” with “Charisma.”

Published in Divine Power, page(s) 92.
