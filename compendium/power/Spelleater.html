Encounter        Arcane
Immediate Interrupt      Personal

Trigger: An attack targets you

Effect: Add 4 to your Fortitude, Reflex, and Will defenses against this attack. If the attack misses, you regain hit points equal to one-half the level of the attacker or effect.
