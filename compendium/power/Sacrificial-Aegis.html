Cleric Utility 2Sacrificial Aegis

The power of your faith siphons life out of you and bolsters your ally with divine protection.

Daily        Divine
Minor Action      Melee touch

Target: One ally

Effect: You take damage equal to your surge value. This damage cannot be reduced by any means. The target gains temporary hit points equal to twice that value.


Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 48.
