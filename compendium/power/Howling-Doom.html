Encounter        Beast Form, Fear, Implement, Primal, Psychic
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Wisdom vs. Will

Hit: 3d6 + Wisdom modifier psychic damage, and the target is immobilized and grants combat advantage to you until the end of your next turn.
