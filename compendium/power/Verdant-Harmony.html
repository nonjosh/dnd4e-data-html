Steadfast Sentinel Attack 20Verdant Harmony

You and those around you take advantage of your harmony with nature, even as the fury of the primal spirits scours your foes.

Daily        Aura, Necrotic, Primal
Standard Action      Personal

Effect: You activate an aura 2 that lasts until the end of the encounter. While in the aura, you and your allies ignore difficult terrain, and each of you gains a +5 power bonus to his or her healing surge value. Any enemy in the aura that hits or misses with an attack takes 10 necrotic damage. An enemy can take this damage only once per turn.


Published in Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms.
