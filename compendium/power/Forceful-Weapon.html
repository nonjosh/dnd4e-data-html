Artificer Attack 13Forceful Weapon

You infuse a weapon with magic, making it deliver a greater impact than its size suggests.

Encounter        Arcane, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Intelligence modifier damage, and you push the target a number of squares equal to your Constitution modifier and knock it prone.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 52.
