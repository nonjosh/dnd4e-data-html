Spellscarred Attack 19Gravity of Moment

You bend the laws of the universe before letting them snap back into shape.

Daily        Arcane, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 20

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. Will, Wisdom vs. Will, or Charisma vs. Will

Hit: Slide the target 15 squares.

Miss: Slide the target 10 squares.

Sustain Minor: If the target is in range of the power, slide the target 10 squares.


Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 45.
