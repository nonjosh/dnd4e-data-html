Wizard Attack 1Leaden Transformation

You send a bolt of arcane energy at your foe, which deals damage and makes your foe's feet feel like blocks of lead.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement, Transmutation
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + Intelligence modifier damage.

Effect: The target is slowed and can't shift until the end of its next turn.
