Shade Racial Utility 22Five Darknesses

The essence of the Shadowfell flows freely within you, transforming you into living shadow that strikes fear into the hearts of nearby creatures.

Encounter      Fear, Shadow
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You gain 10 temporary hit points. Until the end of your next turn, you gain a fly speed equal to your speed, you are insubstantial and phasing, and creatures take a -2 penalty to attack rolls while adjacent to you.
