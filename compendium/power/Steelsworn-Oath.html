Avenger Attack 1Steelsworn Oath

As you swing your weapon at your enemy, you infuse the attack with the power of divine retribution, and the power lingers on your foe.

Daily        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Effect: Before the attack, the target becomes the target of your oath of enmity, replacing your current oath of enmity target if you have one.

Attack: Wisdom vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Wisdom modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.
