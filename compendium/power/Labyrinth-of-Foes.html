Champion of the Labyrinth Attack 20Labyrinth of Foes

You navigate the battlefield as you would a labyrinth, striking and dodging amid the maze of your bewildered foes.

Daily        Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Effect: You shift your speed. Each time you enter a square during this move, you can make the following attack.

  Target: One creature you have not attacked with this power this turn

  Attack: Your highest ability vs. AC

  Hit: 1[W] + your highest ability modifier damage, you slide the target 3 squares, and the target is dazed until the end of your next turn.

  Miss: Half damage, and you slide the target 3 squares.
