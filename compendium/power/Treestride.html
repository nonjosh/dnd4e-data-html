Favored of Mielikki Utility 12Treestride

Your faith in Mielikki opens pathways that only creatures as close to nature as dryads can see.

Daily        Teleportation
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, you can use a minor action to teleport up to 20 squares if you begin and end the teleport adjacent to a plant or plant creature of Large size or larger.
