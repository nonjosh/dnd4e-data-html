The genie’s vessel pops open, releasing a powerful elemental spirit that catches your enemies by surprise.

Daily        Charm, Elemental
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Highest ability modifier + 5 vs. Will

Hit: The target is dazed (save ends).

Miss: The target is dazed until the end of your next turn.

Effect: You or one ally in the burst gains an action point that must be used before the end of your next turn or it is lost.
