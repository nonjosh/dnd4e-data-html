Cleric Attack 25Divine Reaping

The power of your deity works its way through each foe you strike, causing it to slacken and falter.

Daily        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Close burst 1

Target: Each enemy you can see in the burst

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and the target is weakened (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and the target is weakened until the end of your next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 49.
