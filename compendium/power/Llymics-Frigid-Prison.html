Warlock Attack 17Llymic’s Frigid Prison

A puff of icy air escapes from Father Llymic’s icy prison, foreshadowing when glaciers will cover all the worlds and remaining life will serve to feed the Father’s brood.

Encounter        Arcane, Cold, Healing, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Constitution vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d10 + Constitution modifier cold damage, and the target is immobilized until the end of your next turn.

  Star Pact: You also regain hit points equal to twice your Intelligence modifier.


Published in Dragon Magazine 403.
