Fighter Utility 6Rock Steady

You sink your center of gravity into the ground below you and grit your teeth. You are going nowhere.

Daily        Martial, Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the stance ends, you cannot be knocked prone, and if you are subject to a pull, a push, or a slide, you can reduce the forced movement by 1 square.
