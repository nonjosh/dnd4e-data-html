Wizard Utility 2Herbal Healing

Using a mix of herbal lore and witchcraft, you work a spell that binds a wound.

Encounter        Arcane
Minor Action      Melee 1

Prerequisite: You must have training in Heal.

Target: One creature

Effect: The target can make a saving throw and use his or her second wind if it is available.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 93.
