Druid Utility 16Darting Viper

You transform into a viper and slip away from your foes. You then return to your normal form but retain some aspects of the viper for a moment.

Encounter        Primal
Move Action      Personal

Prerequisite: You must have the wild shape power.

Effect: You shift your speed. During this movement, you ignore difficult terrain and can shift through enemies’ spaces. You then gain a +4 power bonus to speed and can move through enemies’ spaces until the end of your next turn.


Published in Primal Power, page(s) 47.
