Warlord Attack 17Thunderous Fury

You strike your enemy with a furious blow that creates a momentary lull in the fighting, giving you a chance to direct your allies to victory.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and the target is dazed until the end of your next turn.

  Tactical Presence: Until the end of your next turn, each of your allies gains a power bonus to attack rolls against the target equal to your Intelligence modifier.


Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 150, Class Compendium.
