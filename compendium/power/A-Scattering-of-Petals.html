Grandmaster of Flowers Utility 26A Scattering of Petals

You brush aside a ranged attack with practiced ease, redirecting its energy toward other targets.

Encounter        Psionic
Free Action      Personal

Trigger: A ranged attack hits you

Effect: You take half damage from the triggering attack, and the attack also hits one or two creatures you choose within 10 squares of you.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 148.
