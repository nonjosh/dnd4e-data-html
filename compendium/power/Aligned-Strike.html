Ranger Attack 15Aligned Strike

You and your companion coordinate your strikes to land on two different foes.

Daily        Beast, Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Primary Target: One creature

Primary Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Make a secondary attack.

  Melee: beast 1

  Secondary Target: One creature other than the primary target

  Secondary Attack: Beast’s attack bonus vs. AC

  Hit: 3[B] + beast’s Strength modifier damage.

  Miss: Half damage.
