Invoker Attack 17Curse of Haemnathuun

You invoke a faint echo of the curse that transformed the fearsome primordial Haemnathuun into a statue, partially encasing your foes in stone for a moment.

Encounter        Divine, Implement
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + Wisdom modifier damage, and the target is dazed and immobilized until the end of your next turn.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 110.
