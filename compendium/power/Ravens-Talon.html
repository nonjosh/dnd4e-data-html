Cleric Attack 7Raven's Talon

Your hand becomes a spectral talon that tears through an enemy, its death shriek invigorating a nearby ally.

Encounter        Divine, Healing, Necrotic, Shadow
Standard Action      Melee 1

Target: One bloodied enemy

Effect: The target takes 5 + your Wisdom modifier necrotic damage. If this damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, you or one ally within 5 squares of you can spend a healing surge.


Published in Heroes of Shadow, page(s) 88.
