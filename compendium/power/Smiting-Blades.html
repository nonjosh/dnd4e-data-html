Arbiter of Forgotten Justice Attack 11Smiting Blades

A burst of radiance not only chars your foe, but causes its steps to become unsteady.

Encounter        Divine, Implement, Radiant
Standard Action      Area burst 2 within 12

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 1d10 + Wisdom modifier radiant damage. If the target hits or misses you or an ally before the end of your next turn, it falls prone and is slowed until the end of your next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 77.
