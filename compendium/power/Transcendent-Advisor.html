Cleric Utility 10Transcendent Advisor

A servant of your deity appears before you.

Daily        Conjuration, Divine
Standard Action      Ranged 5

Effect: You conjure an advisor from your deity that appears in an unoccupied square within range. The advisor occupies 1 square and lasts until the end of your next turn. Allies within 5 squares of the counsel can roll twice on all knowledge checks and keep either result.

Sustain Minor: The effect persists.


Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 49.
