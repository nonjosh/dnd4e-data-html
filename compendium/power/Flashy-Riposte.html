Rogue Attack 5Flashy Riposte

Under attack, you elegantly whirl and let your blade give a sharp reply, leaving your foe off balance.

Daily        Martial, Rattling, Weapon
Immediate Reaction      Melee weapon

Trigger: An enemy makes a melee attack against you

Requirement: You must be wielding a light blade.

Target: The triggering enemy

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Dexterity modifier damage.

Effect: The target grants combat advantage to you and your allies until the end of your next turn.
