Great Bear Shaman Attack 20Call to the Great Bear

Your spirit companion mauls your enemy with its claws and teeth. The spirit of the Great Bear lingers around your foe, punishing it for harming your allies.

Daily        Implement, Primal, Spirit
Standard Action      Melee spirit 1

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 4d10 + Wisdom modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The target takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls, and whenever it hits with an attack, the target takes 1d10 + your Wisdom modifier damage (save ends both).


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 134.
