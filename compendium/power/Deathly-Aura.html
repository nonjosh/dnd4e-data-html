Paladin Utility 10Deathly Aura

The spirit of death surrounds you, nipping at your foes as they are struck.

Daily        Aura, Necrotic, Shadow
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You activate an aura 1 that lasts until the end of the encounter. While you are bloodied, enemies in the aura take necrotic damage after they are hit by any attack. The damage equals your Charisma modifier.
