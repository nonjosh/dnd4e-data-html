Hamadryad Racial Utility 2Body of Solid Oak

Your body hardens to take on the strength and resilience of the mighty oak.

Daily
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You gain temporary hit points equal to 3 + one-half your level. Until the end of your next turn, you gain a +2 power bonus to AC and Fortitude, but you also gain vulnerable 5 fire.

Sustain Minor: The bonus and the vulnerability persist until the end of your next turn.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 26.
