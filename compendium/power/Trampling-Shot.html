Seeker Attack 29Trampling Shot

At your call, spectral rhinos slam into your foes, and then you fire, taking advantage of the chaos unleashed by the stampede.

Daily        Primal, Weapon
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each enemy in the blast

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 2[W] + Wisdom modifier damage, and you knock the target prone.

Effect: You make a ranged basic attack with a +2 bonus to attack rolls for each target you hit with the close blast and a +5 bonus to damage rolls for each target you missed with it.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 129.
