Encounter        Cold, Primal, Radiant, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Attack: Wisdom vs. AC

Hit: 1[W] + 1d8 + Wisdom modifier cold and radiant damage, and the target is restrained (save ends).
