Chosen Utility 26Renewing Dawn

A glimpse of the true sun flashes above the battlefield, sending flares that lance down into your foes.

Daily        Healing, Radiant
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: When you or one of your allies spends a healing surge before the end of your next turn, this power deals radiant damage equal to one-half your level + your Charisma modifier to an enemy within 5 squares of the creature that spent the healing surge.

Sustain Minor: The effect persists.


Addition (2/10/2009)
In the Effect line, add “before the end of your next turn” after “When you or one of your allies spends a healing surge”.

Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 73.
