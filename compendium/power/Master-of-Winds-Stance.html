Monk Utility 16Master of Winds Stance

Wind whips around you, taking you where you need to go, not where anyone else would send you.

Daily        Psionic, Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the stance ends, when you are pulled, pushed, or slid by any enemy, you can use a free action at the end of the forced movement to shift the same number of squares that you were moved.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 71.
