Druid Attack 17Bloodthirsty Vines

Rope-like vines spring from the ground, sinking their thorns into your foes.

Encounter        Implement, Primal
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d10 + Wisdom modifier damage.

Effect: You pull the target up to a number of squares equal to 2 + your Constitution or Dexterity modifier.
