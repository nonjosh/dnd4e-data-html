Fighter Attack 17Warrior’s Challenge

You land a mighty blow that staggers your foe and sends it backward. With a wicked grin, you hoist your weapon and challenge the other enemies nearby.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and you push the target up to 2 squares.

Effect: You mark each enemy within 2 squares of the target until the end of your next turn.
