Rogue Attack 1Twilight Menace

Your assault menaces your foe, causing it to flinch and look away as you continue your relentless attack.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a light blade.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Dexterity modifier damage, and the target treats you as having concealment (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and the target treats you as having concealment until the end of your next turn.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 60.
