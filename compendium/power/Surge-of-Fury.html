Barbarian Utility 10Surge of Fury

You release a howl that blocks out the whispers of your enemies and fills you with a fury that gives you a clarity of purpose.

Daily        Primal
No Action      Personal

Trigger: You start your turn subject to a dazing or dominating effect.

Effect: You end the triggering effect. Until the end of your next turn you are immune to effects that daze or dominate you.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 49.
