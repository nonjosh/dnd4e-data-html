Shaman Attack 15Guardian of the Primal Copse

A spirit of the forest smashes your enemy with its mighty branches. For the moment, your foe’s defenses are only as strong as the weakest.

Daily        Implement, Primal
Standard Action      Ranged 5

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 4d8 + Wisdom modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: After the attack, the target’s defenses all equal the lowest of its defenses (save ends).


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 127.
