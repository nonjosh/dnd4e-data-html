Warlock Attack 7Confounding Laughter

You create haunting, maddening laughter about an enemy. The noise drives it mad with frustration, causing it to lash at anyone nearby.

Encounter        Arcane, Illusion, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One enemy

Effect: You can slide the target up to 2 squares.

Attack: Charisma vs. Will

Hit: 1d10 + Charisma modifier psychic damage. The target then makes a melee basic attack as a free action against an enemy you choose. If this attack misses, the target takes 1d6 extra psychic damage.


Published in Dragon Magazine 390.
