Cleric Attack 1Divine Glow

With a quick prayer, you invoke a blast of white radiance from your holy symbol. Foes burn in its stern light, but your allies are heartened and guided by it.

Encounter        Divine, Implement, Radiant
Standard Action      Close blast 3

Target: Each enemy in the blast

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 1d8 + Wisdom modifier radiant damage.

Effect: Each ally in the blast gains a +2 power bonus to attack rolls until the end of your next turn.


Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 63, Class Compendium.
