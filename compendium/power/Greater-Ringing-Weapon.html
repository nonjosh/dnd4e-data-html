Bard Attack 27Greater Ringing Weapon

A great clap of thunder accompanies your strike, which seems to linger on your enemy, calling your allies’ attention to your foe’s position.

Encounter        Arcane, Thunder
No Action      Special

Trigger: You hit an enemy with a basic attack using a weapon.

Effect: The enemy you hit takes 15 thunder damage. Until the end of your next turn, that enemy grants combat advantage and cannot benefit from partial concealment.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 68.
