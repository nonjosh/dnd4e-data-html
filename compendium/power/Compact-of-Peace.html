Runepriest Utility 6Compact of Peace

You invoke the runes of peace, making a pledge to set aside your weapons and giving you and your allies the chance to negotiate.

Encounter        Divine
Minor Action      Close burst 5

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: Each target gains a +5 power bonus to Diplomacy checks and Insight checks until the end of your next turn. The effect ends if any target makes an attack.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 104.
