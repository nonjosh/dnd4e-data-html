Warden Utility 16Vine Poultice

You summon healing vines from the ground and pack the wound with them, creating an efficacious bandage.

Daily        Healing, Primal
Minor Action      Melee touch

Target: You or one creature

Effect: The target can spend a healing surge and make a saving throw against each effect it is suffering from that a save can end.


Published in Dragon Magazine 379, page(s) 47.
