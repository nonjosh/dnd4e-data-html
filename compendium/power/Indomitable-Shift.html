Barbarian Utility 6Indomitable Shift

You pound across the battlefield, leaving no opening as you move and drawing strength from the numbers arrayed against you.

Daily        Primal
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You shift a number of squares equal to your Constitution modifier. You gain 1d10 temporary hit points plus 1 additional temporary hit point for each enemy within 2 squares of you.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 54.
