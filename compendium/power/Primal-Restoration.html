Druid Utility 16Primal Restoration

Energy swirls around your allies to heal their wounds and help them shake off harmful afflictions.

Daily        Healing, Primal
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: Each target can spend a healing surge either to regain hit points or to remove one effect from himself or herself that a save can end.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 91, Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms, page(s) 109.
