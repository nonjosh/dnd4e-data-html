Barbarian Attack 1Whirling Frenzy

Someone will bleed after every strike of this frenzy. You prefer to see your enemy’s blood but are prepared to see your own.

Encounter        Primal, Weapon
Standard Action      Close burst 1

Requirement: You must be wielding two melee weapons.

Target: Each creature in the burst

Attack: Strength vs. AC (main weapon)

  Whirling Slayer: The attack can target Reflex instead of AC.

Hit: 1[W] + 1[W] (off-hand weapon) + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: You take 1d6 damage, with no modifiers.


Update (4/13/2010)
In the Miss entry, replace “1d6” with “1d6, with no modifiers.”
