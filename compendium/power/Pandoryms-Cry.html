Warlock Attack 1Pandorym’s Cry

From a distance too far to measure, you evoke an echo of Pandorym’s birthing cry: a sound of madness incarnate.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Ranged 5

Target: One creature

Attack: Constitution vs. Will

Hit: 2d6 + Constitution modifier psychic damage.
