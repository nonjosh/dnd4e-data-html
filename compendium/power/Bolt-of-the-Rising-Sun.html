Invoker Attack 7Bolt of the Rising Sun

A soft ray of light dances across your foe. The light increases in radiance until it chars the foe’s flesh and impairs its vision.

Encounter        Divine, Implement, Radiant
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 1d10 + Wisdom modifier radiant damage. Until the end of your next turn, the target doesn’t have line of sight to any creature more than 3 squares away from it.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 107.
