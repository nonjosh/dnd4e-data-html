Warlord Attack 27Devastating Charge

You rush forward and furiously assault your enemy, encouraging all who see your courage to do the same.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Effect: You charge and make the following attack in place of a melee basic attack.

  Target: One creature

  Attack: Strength vs. AC

  Hit: 4[W] + Strength modifier damage. Until the end of your next turn, if any ally can see you when he or she charges, the ally gains a bonus to the damage roll of the charge attack. The bonus equals your Charisma modifier.


Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 152, Class Compendium.
