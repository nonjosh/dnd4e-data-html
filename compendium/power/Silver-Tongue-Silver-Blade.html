Silver-Tongued Scoundrel Attack 20Silver Tongue, Silver Blade

Through a combination of careful strikes and witty barbs, you keep your enemy reeling.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Highest ability vs. Reflex

Hit: 3[W] + highest ability modifier damage. In addition, you can choose one: the target takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls or grants combat advantage until the end of your next turn.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, when you hit the target with an attack, you can choose one: the target takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls or grants combat advantage until the end of your next turn.
