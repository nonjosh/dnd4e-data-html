Warlord Attack 27Combined Arms Assault

You fire at an enemy, prompting a pair of nearby allies to charge or shoot the foe.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Ranged weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage. One or two allies you can see can choose either to charge the target or to make a ranged basic attack against the target as a free action.

  Skirmishing Presence: The allies gain a power bonus to the damage rolls equal to your Intelligence modifier or Wisdom modifier.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 95.
