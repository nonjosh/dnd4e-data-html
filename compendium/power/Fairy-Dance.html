Pixie Racial Utility 16Fairy Dance

You strike up the enchanted music of the fey, granting your companions grace even as your foes trip and fumble around you.

Daily      Aura, Charm
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You activate an aura 2 that lasts until the end of your next turn. Any ally who starts his or her turn in the aura can shift 1 square during that turn as a free action. Any enemy that starts its turn in the aura is slowed until the start of its next turn.

Sustain Minor: The aura persists until the end of your next turn.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 33.
