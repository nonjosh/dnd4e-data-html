Streetwise Utility 2City Rat

Like a rat, you are particularly skilled at using the environment, as well as your companions, to stay out of sight.

Encounter
Free Action      Personal

Trigger: You end a move action and have cover against at least one creature

Effect: You make a Streetwise check in place of a Stealth check to become hidden from any creature against which you have cover.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 175.
