Shaman Attack 27Call to the Great Hunter

Your spirit companion is filled with the battle wisdom of the great hunter spirit as it rends your foe and then lends that wisdom to a nearby ally.

Encounter        Implement, Primal, Spirit
Standard Action      Melee spirit 1

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 4d8 + Wisdom modifier damage. One ally adjacent to your spirit companion gains a +5 bonus to attack rolls until the end of your next turn.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 130.
