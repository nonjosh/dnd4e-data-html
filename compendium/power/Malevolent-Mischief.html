Bard Attack 1Malevolent Mischief

You plague your foe with a spell of ill luck, causing knots to unravel and straps to part so that it stumbles like an oaf.

Daily        Arcane, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One Creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d8 + Charisma modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The target is affected by ill luck (save ends). While affected by ill luck, the target is slowed until the end of its next turn whenever you or an ally hits it with an attack.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 7.
