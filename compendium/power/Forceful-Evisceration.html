Barbarian Attack 27Forceful Evisceration

You channel the primal power around you into your weapon and strike. The attack not only causes immense damage, it leaves your enemy devoid of its natural protection for a time.

Encounter        Force, Primal, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + 3d8 + Strength modifier force damage, and the target loses all resistances until the end of your next turn.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 52.
