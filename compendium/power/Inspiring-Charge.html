Warlord Attack 19Inspiring Charge

Giving your companions a quick signal, you charge forward to glory, inspiring them.

Daily        Healing, Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Effect: As a free action, each ally within 10 squares of you can shift a number of squares equal to your Charisma modifier and then spend a healing surge.

Special: When charging, you can use this power in place of a melee basic attack.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 113.
