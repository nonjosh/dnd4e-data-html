Barbarian Utility 16Battle Lust

Your bloodlust pulls you from the brink of death.

Daily        Healing, Primal
No Action      Personal

Trigger: You are dying at the start of your turn

Effect: You spend a healing surge, and you can stand up as a free action. Until the end of your next turn, you gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and a +4 bonus to damage rolls.


Published in Primal Power, page(s) 18.
