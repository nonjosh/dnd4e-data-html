Invoker Attack 25Invoke the Fallen

In their final battles, the gods used their fallen as terrible weapons. The ashes of dead gods choked, blinded, and killed their enemies. You invoke these remains and turn them against your foes.

Daily        Divine, Implement, Zone
Standard Action      Area burst 2 within 10 squares

Target: Each creature in the burst

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + Wisdom modifier damage, and the target takes ongoing 10 damage and is blinded (save ends both).

Miss: Half damage, and the target is blinded until the end of your next turn.

Effect: The burst creates a zone of lightly obscured terrain until the end of the encounter. While within the zone, you and your allies gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls with divine powers.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 112.
