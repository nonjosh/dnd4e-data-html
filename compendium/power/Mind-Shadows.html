Warlock Attack Mind Shadows

Reality unravels into darkness around your enemy, breaking its grip on sanity.

At-Will        Arcane, Implement, Psychic, Shadow
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Will

Hit: 1d10 + Charisma modifier psychic damage. Until the end of your next turn, the target cannot see any of your allies who are 3 or more squares away from it.
    Level 21: 2d10 + Charisma modifier psychic damage.
