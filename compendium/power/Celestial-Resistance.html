Celestial Scholar Utility 12Celestial Resistance

You transform your resistance to ward against a different kind of threat.

Encounter        Arcane
Free Action      Personal

Effect: You can change the resistance granted by your current Soul of the Cosmic Cycle phase to the type of a different phase (cold, psychic, or radiant), or you can increase your resistance against the current type by 5. This benefit lasts until you change your phase.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 43.
