Battlemind Utility 22Wings of Elevated Thought

Your power of thought lifts you free of the battlefield.

Daily        Psionic, Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You assume the wings of elevated thought stance. Until the stance ends, you gain a fly speed of 6, but you descend safely to the ground at the end of each of your turns.
