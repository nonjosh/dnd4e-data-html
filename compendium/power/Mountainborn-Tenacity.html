Dwarf Racial Utility 16Mountainborn Tenacity

Just as no mountain fell from a single strike, no dwarf has fallen from a single blow.

Daily      Healing
Immediate Interrupt      Personal

Trigger: An attack drops you to 0 hit points or fewer.

Effect: You can spend a healing surge. Until the end of your next turn, you gain resistance to all damage equal to twice your Constitution modifier.


Published in Dragon Magazine 402.
