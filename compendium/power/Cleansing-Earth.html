Warden Utility 16Cleansing Earth

Even as harm befalls you, you draw on the earth for the strength to shrug it off.

Encounter        Primal
Immediate Reaction      Personal

Trigger: You are subjected to an effect that a save can end

Effect: You make a saving throw against the triggering effect, with a power bonus to the saving throw equal to your Strength modifier.
