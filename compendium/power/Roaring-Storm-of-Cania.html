Warlock Attack 5Roaring Storm of Cania

A gale of frigid wind rips through your foes, hurling them to the ground and pelting them with razor-sharp blades of ice.

Daily        Arcane, Cold, Implement
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Charisma vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + Charisma modifier cold damage, and the target falls prone.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You slide each target up to 3 squares.


Published in Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms, page(s) 211.
