Daring Acrobat Attack 20Dramatic Finish

You drive your blade deep into your wounded foe, knowing you have something in reserve as a follow-up.

Daily        Healing, Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a light blade.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Dexterity modifier damage, or 6[W] + Dexterity modifier damage if either you or the target is bloodied.

Effect: If this attack fails to reduce the target to 0 hit points, you can either make a melee basic attack against the target with a +5 bonus to the attack roll or spend a healing surge.

Special: When charging, you can use this power in place of a melee basic attack.
