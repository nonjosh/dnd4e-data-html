Spellscarred Attack 13Spellmother's Embrace

Your arm becomes gelatinous and stretches to engulf a nearby foe.

Encounter        Acid, Arcane, Polymorph
Standard Action      Melee 1

Requirement: You must have an empty hand.

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength + 2 vs. Fortitude, Dexterity + 2 vs. Fortitude, or Constitution + 2 vs. Fortitude Increase the bonus to the attack roll to +4 at 11th level and +6 at 21st level.

Hit: You grab the target. It takes 5 acid damage at the end of each of its turns until it is no longer grabbed by you.


Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 44.
