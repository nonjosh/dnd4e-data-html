Warlord Attack 23Shutdown Smite

A well-placed attack throws your foe out of prime fighting form for a moment.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. Will

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and the target cannot recharge any of its powers or use action points until the end of your next turn.

  Tactical Presence: The target also takes a penalty to saving throws equal to your Intelligence modifier until the end of your next turn.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 115.
