Ranger Attack 1Precise Assault

You see an opening in an enemy’s defense, and you break off your current attack to take advantage of the weakness.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Effect: Before the attack, you move your speed. You don’t provoke opportunity attacks for leaving a square at the start of this movement.

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength + 2 vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage.
