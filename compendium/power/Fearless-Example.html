Bard Utility 16Fearless Example

You project a surge of courage into the minds of your allies, allowing them to push through and keep fighting.

Daily        Arcane
Minor Action      Close burst 5

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: All fear effects end on each target. In addition, each target can make a saving throw against each effect that a save can end.
