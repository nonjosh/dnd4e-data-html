Cleric Utility 1Create Water

A cool breeze washes over you as a thick, magical mist fills a vessel with water.

Encounter        Divine
Minor Action      Melee 1

Target: One empty cup, flask, or similar container that can hold up to 1 gallon of liquid

Effect: You cause the container to fill with fresh, potable water.


Published in Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 110.
