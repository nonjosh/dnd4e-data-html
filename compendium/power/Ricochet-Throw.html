Ranger Attack 23Ricochet Throw

As you unleash your weapon, it strikes one foe and then ricochets toward another, hitting that creature as well.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Ranged weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a thrown weapon.

Primary Target: One creature

Primary Attack: Strength vs. AC (thrown weapon)

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage. Make a secondary attack that uses the same thrown weapon you used for the primary attack.

  Secondary Target: One creature within 5 squares of the primary target

  Secondary Attack: Strength vs. AC

  Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 43.
