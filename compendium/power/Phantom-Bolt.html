Wizard Attack 1Phantom Bolt

You wave your hand, and your foe sees a bolt of fire streaking toward it. The enemy dives away from the imagined threat.

At-Will        Arcane, Illusion, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. Will

Hit: 1d8 + Intelligence modifier psychic damage, and you slide the target 1 square.
Level 21: 2d8 + Intelligence modifier psychic damage.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 101, Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 51.
