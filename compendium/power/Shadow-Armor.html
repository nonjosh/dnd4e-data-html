Warlock Utility 10Shadow Armor

Shadow coalesces around you to take the form of a macabre suit of plate armor.

Daily        Arcane, Shadow, Teleportation
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You gain a +2 power bonus to Stealth checks and a +2 power bonus to all defenses until the end of the encounter. As a minor action, you can end this effect and then teleport up to 5 squares. You then become invisible until the end of your next turn, or until you hit or miss with an attack roll.


Published in Heroes of Shadow, page(s) 70.
