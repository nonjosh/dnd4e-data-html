Rogue Utility 10Brisk Stride

Leaping across minor obstacles, you dart across the battlefield.

Encounter        Martial
Move Action      Personal

Prerequisite: You must be trained in Athletics.

Effect: You can move your speed + 4. During this movement and until the end of your next turn, you can move across difficult terrain at your normal speed.


Revision (2/26/2009)
The power is an encounter power and should have a red stripe, not a black stripe.

Published in Martial Power, page(s) 79.
