Drow Racial Utility 16Dark Doorway

You see your moment and seize a pathway of magic that pulls you through shadow to arrive elsewhere.

Daily      Teleportation
Minor Action      Personal

Requirement: You must be hidden from each enemy within 10 squares of you.

Effect: You teleport up to 10 squares to a square providing any degree of cover or concealment. You can make a Stealth check to remain hidden when you arrive, ignoring the normal -5 penalty to the check for movement.


Published in Dragon Magazine 405.
