Cleric Attack 9Pariah's Curse

Your divine power curses a foe, causing the creature to lash out at its allies when they come too close.

Daily        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One enemy

Attack: Wisdom vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Wisdom modifier damage.

Effect: Any enemy that ends its turn adjacent to the target takes 10 damage. The effect lasts until the end of the encounter.


Published in Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 96.
