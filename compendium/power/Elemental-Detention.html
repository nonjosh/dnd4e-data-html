Swordmage Attack 17Elemental Detention

Encounter        Arcane, Lightning, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Intelligence modifier lightning damage, and the target is restrained until the end of your next turn.
