Battlemind Attack 15Mind Blade

You lash out with your weapon and channel a burst of pain into your enemy’s mind. Though the blow doesn’t connect, your foe collapses as it feels the weapon strike a vital area.

Daily        Psionic, Psychic, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Constitution vs. Will

Hit: The target falls unconscious (save ends).

  Aftereffect: 10 + Constitution modifier psychic damage.

Miss: The target is dazed (save ends).


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 51.
