Barbarian Attack 29Rage of the Primal Beast

The Primal Beast awakens within you, and your attack leaves your foe bleeding. As you rage, you feed on the presence of your foes; overwhelming numbers only make you stronger.

Daily        Primal, Rage, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 5[W] + Strength modifier damage, and ongoing 10 damage (save ends).

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You enter the rage of the Primal Beast. Until the rage ends, you gain a bonus to attack rolls equal to the number of enemies you can see.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 61.
