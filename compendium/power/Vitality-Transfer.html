Ardent Attack 19Vitality Transfer

The pain your attack inflicts on your foe gives you strength to heal your comrades.

Daily        Healing, Psionic, Reliable, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Fortitude

Hit: 4[W] + Charisma modifier damage. Once before the end of the encounter, when the target starts its turn bloodied or drops to 0 hit points, each ally you can see regains hit points as if he or she had spent a healing surge.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 34.
