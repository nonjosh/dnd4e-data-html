Avenger Attack 23Sacred Arena

You banish your enemies from your presence, keeping behind only the target of your sacred enmity.

Encounter        Divine, Teleportation, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Effect: Before making this attack, you can teleport each enemy adjacent to you other than your oath of enmity target 5 squares to a square not adjacent to you, and not to a square that causes the creature to make a saving throw or take damage or another negative effect.

Target: Your oath of enmity target

Attack: Wisdom vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Wisdom modifier damage.


Addition (2/18/2010)
Add Teleportation keyword.
