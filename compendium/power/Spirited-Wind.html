Druid Utility 10Spirited Wind

A powerful gust circles around you and lifts you and an ally into the air.

Encounter        Elemental, Primal
Move Action      Close burst 1

Target: You and one ally in the burst

Effect: Each target can fly up to 6 squares as a free action, and gains a +4 power bonus to all defenses against opportunity attacks provoked by this movement.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 64.
