Theurge of the Compact Utility 12Infernal Guardian

A sinister figure formed from hellfire appears beside you to protect you from harm.

Encounter        Conjuration, Divine
Minor Action      Melee 1

Effect: You conjure an infernal guardian in an unoccupied square adjacent to you. The guardian lasts until the end of your next turn. While you are adjacent to the guardian, you are immune to charm effects and fear effects, and you gain resist 10 fire.

Sustain Minor: The guardian persists.


Published in Divine Power, page(s) 78.
