Warden Attack 23Desert Winds

With a sweep of your weapon, you summon a fiery wind that clouds your enemies’ sight and sears all who try to escape it.

Encounter        Fire, Primal, Weapon, Zone
Standard Action      Close blast 3

Target: Each enemy in the blast

Attack: Strength vs. Reflex

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier fire damage. The blast creates a zone of fiery haze until the end of your next turn. The zone blocks enemies’ line of sight, and any enemy entirely within the zone is blinded. Any enemy that starts its turn within the zone takes 2d10 fire damage when it leaves the zone.


Published in Primal Power, page(s) 103.
