Rogue Utility 10Certain Freedom

You are as slippery as an eel.

Daily        Martial
Move Action      Personal

Prerequisite: You must have training in Acrobatics.

Effect: You automatically succeed on an Acrobatics check to escape from a grab or from restraints.
