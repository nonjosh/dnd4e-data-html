Druid Utility Zephyr Carry

Your companion is a concentrated gust that you can direct to bring its target through the air and into your clutches.

Encounter        Primal
Free Action      Special

Trigger: Your living zephyr animal companion hits an enemy within 5 squares of you with animal attack.

Effect: You slide the living zephyr up to 3 squares, and then slide the enemy up to 5 squares to a square adjacent to the zephyr.


Published in Heroes of the Elemental Chaos, page(s) 62.
