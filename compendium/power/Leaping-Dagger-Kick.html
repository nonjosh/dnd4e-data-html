Rogue Attack 17Leaping Dagger Kick

You leap through the air, crashing into your foe and slashing at it.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a light blade.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Effect: You knock the target prone.

Hit: 3[W] + Dexterity modifier damage.

  Artful Dodger: If you charged the target, it is dazed until the end of your next turn.

Special: When charging, you can use this power in place of a melee basic attack.
