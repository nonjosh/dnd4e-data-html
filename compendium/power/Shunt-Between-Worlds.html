Wizard Attack 15Shunt Between Worlds

You blast your foes with the same arcane energy you use to pass between worlds, shunting them into the Feywild and back again.

Daily        Arcane, Implement, Psychic, Teleportation
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Target: All enemies within the area of the burst

Attack: Intelligence vs. Will

Hit: 4d6 + Intelligence modifier psychic damage, and you teleport the target 5 squares. The destination must be on solid ground.

Miss: Half damage.


Published in Dragon Magazine Annual, Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 15.
