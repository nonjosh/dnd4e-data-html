Barbarian Attack 25Ash Hammer Rage

You pull the unyielding might of the forest into your weapon and drive your foe back. As you rage, you draw vitality through your rooted connection to the ground.

Daily        Primal, Rage, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 7[W] + Strength modifier damage, and you push the
target a number of squares equal to your Constitution
modifier.

Miss: Half damage, and you push the target 1 square.

Effect: You enter the rage of the ash hammer. Until the rage ends, whenever you hit with an attack, you gain temporary hit points equal to 10 + your Charisma modifier. If that attack already grants temporary hit points to you, add your Charisma modifier to the number of temporary hit points you gain.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 59.
