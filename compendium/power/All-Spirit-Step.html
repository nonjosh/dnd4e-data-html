Scion of the All-Spirit Utility 12All-Spirit Step

The All-Spirit beckons to your enemies as you slip out of trouble.

Encounter        Primal, Teleportation
Move Action      Personal

Effect: You mark each enemy adjacent to you and then teleport your speed. You must end this teleport adjacent to at least one enemy you marked with this power.
