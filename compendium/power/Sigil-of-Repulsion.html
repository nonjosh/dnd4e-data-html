Sivis Truenamer Utility 12Sigil of Repulsion

You utter ancient words of Supernal, conjuring a silvery glyph that repels your foes.

Daily        Conjuration
Minor Action      Ranged 20

Effect: You conjure a glowing sigil in an unoccupied square within range. Any enemy within 5 squares of the sigil must spend 2 extra squares of movement for each space it moves closer to the sigil.

Sustain Minor: The sigil persists.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 76.
