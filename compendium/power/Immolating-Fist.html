Monk Attack 9Immolating Fist

Fire blazes within your clenched fists to burn those you strike, culminating in an explosive attack that wraps your foes in a shroud of flames.

Daily        Fire, Implement, Psionic, Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the stance ends, you gain resist 5 fire. In addition, whenever you hit an enemy with a melee attack, it takes 5 extra fire damage. You can also use the Immolating Fist Attack power.


Immolating Fist Attack

Daily      Fire, Implement, Psionic, Stance
Standard Action      Melee touch

Requirement: The power Immolating Fist must be active in order to use this power.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 1d8 + Dexterity modifier damage plus 1d12 fire damage, and each enemy adjacent to the target takes 5 fire damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The stance ends.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 66.
