Spellscarred Attack 7Cursed Grasping Air

Bands of twisting force grasp your enemies and pull them toward one another.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement
Standard Action      Ranged 15

Target: Two creatures

Attack: Intelligence vs. Reflex, Wisdom vs. Reflex, or Charisma vs. Reflex, one attack per target

Hit: 2d6 + Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma modifier damage. If you hit both targets, you slide one target to be adjacent to the other.
