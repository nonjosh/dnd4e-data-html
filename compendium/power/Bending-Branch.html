Ranger Utility 2Bending Branch

You resist a foe's attempt to move you, and like a great tree swaying in a storm, you snap back, ready to attack

        Martial
Immediate Interrupt      Personal

Trigger: You are pulled, pushed, or slid

Effect: You negate the forced movement. In addition, you gain a +2 bonus to your next melee attack roll before the end of your next turn


Published in Dragon Magazine 377, page(s) 71.
