Anathema Utility 12Magical Disruption

Your will to destroy means that even magic crumbles away to nothing at your command.

Daily        Implement, Psionic
Minor Action      Ranged 10

Target: One conjuration or zone

Attack: Intelligence + 4 vs. the Will of the target’s creator

Hit: The target is destroyed. All its effects end, including those that a save can end. Until the end of your next turn, whenever the target’s creator attacks, it can use only basic attacks.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 101.
