Master of the Starry Night Attack 11Stellar Debris

Brilliant stellar debris explodes outward from you, burning your enemies.

Encounter        Arcane, Fire, Implement, Radiant
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Constitution or Charisma vs. Reflex

Hit: 1d8 + Constitution modifier or Charisma modifier radiant and fire damage. Until the end of your next turn, the target treats all creatures as having concealment.
