Rogue Utility 10Dangerous Theft

You boldly snatch an item from an enemy.

Encounter        Martial
Free Action      Personal

Prerequisite: You must have training in Thievery.

Effect: On your next action, ignore the -10 penalty when you make a Thievery check to pick a pocket during combat.
