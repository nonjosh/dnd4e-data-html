Shadow Assassin Attack 11Killer's Eye

You strike with a killer’s eye, seeking to take down your enemy as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a crossbow, a light blade, or a sling.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 2[W] + Dexterity modifier damage, or 3[W] + Dexterity modifier damage if the target hasn’t taken any actions during the encounter.
