Sorcerer Attack 29Necrotic Storm

Black tendrils attack your enemy seemingly out of nowhere, pulling your enemy apart.

Daily        Arcane, Implement, Necrotic
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Reflex

Hit: 6d10 + Charisma modifier necrotic damage, and the target is stunned (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and the target is dazed (save ends).

  Active Familiar: When the target saves against this power, you can destroy your familiar as a free action to make the target reroll the saving throw and take the lower result.


Published in Dragon Magazine 386, page(s) 24.
