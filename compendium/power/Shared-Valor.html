Paladin Utility 22Shared Valor

A golden, emboldening light flashes around you and your companions, transporting you to places where you can confront the enemy directly.

Daily        Divine, Teleportation
Free Action      Close burst 5

Trigger: You and your allies roll initiative.

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: Each target gains temporary hit points equal to 15 + your Charisma modifier. In addition, you can teleport each target up to 10 squares to a square adjacent to an enemy.


Published in Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms, page(s) 147.
