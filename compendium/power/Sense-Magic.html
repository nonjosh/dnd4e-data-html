Eladrin Racial Utility 2Sense Magic

You attune your senses to the magical world, and in a flash, nearby magic becomes as obvious as birdsong on the wind.

Encounter
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You sense the presence of magic within 10 squares of you as if you succeeded on an Arcana check to do so. You can then make an Arcana check as a free action to identify any revealed magical phenomena, conjurations, or zones.


Published in Dragon Magazine 405.
