Jorasco Jadehand Utility 12Jade Rejuvenation

Your dragon mark glows a faint green. Sigils identical to the mark then appear over the heads of your friends.

Daily        Healing
Minor Action      Close burst 5

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: You lose one healing surge, and each target gains regeneration 5 until the end of your next turn.

Sustain Minor: The effect persists.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 70.
