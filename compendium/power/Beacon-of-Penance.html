Paladin Utility 10Beacon of Penance

You sacrifice some of your own vitality to help your allies overcome their wounds.

Daily        Divine, Healing
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Once per round until the end of the encounter, you can take a minor action to restore 10 hit points to one ally within 2 squares of you. If you do so, you take 10 damage. This damage ignores your immunities and resistances.


Published in Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms.
