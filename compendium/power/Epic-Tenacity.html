Indomitable Champion Utility 26Epic Tenacity

You shake off a potential killing blow, resolute in your determination to defeat your enemies.

Daily        Healing
No Action      Personal

Trigger: You drop to 0 hit points or fewer.

Effect: You regain hit points as if you had spent a healing surge, and you gain a +5 power bonus to all defenses until the end of your next turn.
