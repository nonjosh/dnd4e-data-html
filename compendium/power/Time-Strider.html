Time Bender Utility 12Time Strider

You slow down time slightly, moving like a hummingbird as you dart among your slow-moving friends and foes.

Daily        Psionic
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, you gain a +2 power bonus to attack rolls, saving throws, and all defenses, and you gain a power bonus to speed equal to your Wisdom modifier.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 96.
