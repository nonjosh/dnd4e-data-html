Rogue Attack 29Steel Nettle Rain

You dance deftly around your foe and deliver an attack that is like a thousand metal nettles against its flesh.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a light blade.

Effect: Before and after the attack, you move your speed. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks from the target.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 6[W] + Dexterity modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 71.
