Chosen Utility 26True Death

Your gesture strips the undead of their tricks. Only death is left to them.

Daily        Healing
Minor Action      Close burst 10

Target: Each undead creature in the burst

Effect: You regain 2d20 hit points for each target. Each target loses all its resistances, the insubstantial quality, and the ability to recharge powers (save ends all).

Aftereffect: Target loses all its resistances, insubstantial abilities, and ability to recharge powers (save ends all).


Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 73.
