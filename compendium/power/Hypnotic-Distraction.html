Artificer Attack 3Hypnotic Distraction

The runes carved on your armor and implement flow hypnotically, drawing the attention of one of your enemies.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. Will

Hit: 2d10 + Intelligence modifier psychic damage, and the target takes a penalty to attack rolls equal to your Wisdom modifier until the end of your next turn.
