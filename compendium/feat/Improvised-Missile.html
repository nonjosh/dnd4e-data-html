Improvised Missile

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Barbarian
Benefit: You can use improvised melee weapons (not including unarmed attacks) as heavy thrown weapons with a normal range of 5 squares and a long range of 10 squares.
In addition, whenever you use an improvised melee weapon this way, you gain a +2 feat bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.

Published in Primal Power, page(s) 140.
