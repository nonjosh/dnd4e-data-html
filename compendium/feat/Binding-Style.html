Binding Style [Arena Fighting]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Dex 13, Two-Weapon Fighting, any martial class
Benefit: You gain a benefit with any of the following exploits you possess.


Update (11/23/2009)
In the dual strike entry, add “secondary” in front of “target.”


Associated Powers:
Dual Strike: While you wield a spear and a flail, if both attacks hit, you can immobilize the secondary target until the end of your next turn instead of dealing damage to it.
Twin Strike: While you wield a spear and a flail, if you hit one creature with both attacks, you can slide that target 1 square.

Published in Dragon Magazine Annual, Dragon Magazine 368, page(s) 59.
