Infernal Blessing

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Tiefling, any divine class
Benefit: When you use your infernal wrath racial power, one ally adjacent to you regains hit points equal to your Charisma modifier and gains a +1 power bonus to his or her next attack roll against the target of the power before the end of that ally’s next turn.


Update (4/13/2010)
In the Benefit entry, replace “an enemy that hit you since your last turn” with “the target of the power before the end of that ally’s next turn.”

Published in Divine Power, page(s) 135.
