Rending Chains Warmaster [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Rending Chains Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a flail and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
Whenever you hit an enemy with a martial power, it takes damage equal to your Wisdom modifier if it shifts before the end of your next turn.
You can use a power associated with this feat in place of a melee basic attack.


Associated Powers: Bolstering Blow, Deadly Returns, Inspiring War Cry, Into the Breach!, Warlord's Doom, Wounding Focus

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 129.
