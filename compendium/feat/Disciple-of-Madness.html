Disciple of Madness

Prerequisite: Charisma 13
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to saving throws against effects that daze, stun, or dominate you, as well as against ongoing psychic damage. In addition, whenever you succeed on a saving throw to end one of these effects, you can shift 1 square as a free action and gain a +2 bonus to all defenses until the start of your next turn.

Published in The Book of Vile Darkness, page(s) 1-31.
