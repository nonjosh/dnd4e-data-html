Ironstar Bravo [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Ironstar Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a flail or a mace and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
Any enemy you score a critical hit against grants combat advantage until the end of your next turn.
You can use a power associated with this feat in place of a melee basic attack when charging.


Associated Powers: Daring Display, Pincer Maneuver, Provoke Overextension, Warlord's Strike

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 117.
