Winterkin Heritage [Eladrin Bloodline]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Eladrin
Benefit: You gain the winter’s shroud power. In addition, you gain resist cold equal to 5 + one-half your level.


Feat UtilityWinter's Shroud

When the enemy’s attack falls, snow swirls around you to whisk you to safety.

Encounter      Teleportation
Immediate Reaction      Personal

Trigger: An enemy damages you with an attack

Effect: You teleport 3 squares and gain concealment until the start of your next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 64.
