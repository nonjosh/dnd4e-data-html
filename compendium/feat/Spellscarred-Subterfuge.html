Spellscarred Subterfuge

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Half-elf, you have a spellscar
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Diplomacy and Bluff. In addition, you gain a +4 bonus to all attempts made to conceal or disguise your spellscar. If you have the Student of the Plague feat, increase the feat bonus to +4.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 26.
