Blade Channeling Mastery

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, Sorcerous Blade Channeling feat
Benefit: When you deliver a sorcerer ranged attack as a melee attack using Sorcerous Blade Channeling and the attack has an effect that a save can end, the target takes a -3 penalty to the first saving throw against that effect.

Published in Dragon Magazine 390.
