Energy Transmutation

Epic Tier
Prerequisite: 21st level, githzerai, iron mind racial power
Benefit: When you use iron mind triggered by an attack that has a damage type keyword, you can choose cold, fire, lightning, psychic, radiant, or thunder. Until the end of your next turn, any attack you make can deal damage of the chosen type in place of any other damage type.

Published in Dragon Magazine 378.
