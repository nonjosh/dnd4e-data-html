Hellfire Blood

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Tiefling
Benefit: You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls when you use a power that has the fire or the fear keyword.


Update (2/8/2010)
On the Benefit line, delete “feat.” This change ensures that this feat continues to provide a benefit despite changes to the Weapon Expertise and Implement Expertise feats.

Addition (7/28/2008)
Add the word "the" before fear in the Benefits line.

Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 195.
