Black Hood Butcher [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Fighter, Black Hood Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a two-handed axe and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
If you score a critical hit, each enemy adjacent to you takes damage equal to your Strength modifier.
You can use a power associated with this feat in place of a melee basic attack when making an attack as a result of your Combat Challenge class feature.


Associated Powers: Adamantine Strike, Crippling Smash, Crushing Blow, Exacting Strike, Griffon's Wrath

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 112.
