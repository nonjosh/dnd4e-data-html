Manticore's Fury

Prerequisite: Ranger
Benefit: When you hit an enemy with a melee attack, you gain a +2 bonus to the damage roll of the next ranged attack you make against that enemy before the end of your next turn. When you hit an enemy with a ranged attack, you gain a +2 bonus to the damage roll of the next melee attack you make against that enemy before the end of your next turn. This bonus increases to +4 at 11th level and +6 at 21st level.

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 136.
