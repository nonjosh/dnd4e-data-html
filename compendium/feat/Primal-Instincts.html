Primal Instincts

Epic Tier
Prerequisite: Shifter
Benefit: When you use longtooth shifting or razorclaw shifting, until you are no longer bloodied your 1st-level at-will primal attack powers deal damage equal to your Strength modifier or your Wisdom modifier on a miss if they normally do not have an effect on a miss.

Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 49.
