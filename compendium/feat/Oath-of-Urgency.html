Oath of Urgency

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger, Channel Divinity class feature, oath of enmity power
Benefit: You gain the Channel Divinity power oath of urgency.


Feat UtilityOath of Urgency

You utter a prayer of need, calling upon your god to bring swift vengeance upon a new foe.

Encounter      Divine
Minor Action      Personal

Channel Divinity: You can use only one channel divinity power per encounter.

Effect: The current target of your oath of enmity is no longer the target of that power. You regain your oath of enmity power and use it as a free action immediately.


Published in Dragon Magazine 382, page(s) 40.
