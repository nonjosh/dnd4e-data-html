Charging Admixture

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Minotaur, artificer, curative admixture power
Benefit: When you use curative admixture, the target gains a +2 bonus to damage rolls on charge attacks until the end of your next turn. At 11th level, the bonus improves to +3. At 21st level, the bonus improves to +4.

Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 33.
