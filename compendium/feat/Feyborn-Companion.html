Feyborn Companion

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Eladrin, ranger, Beast Mastery class feature
Benefit: Your beast companion gains the fey origin, instead of the natural origin, and a +5 bonus to saving throws against charm effects.
    When you use your fey step racial power, your beast companion can teleport the same distance that you teleport.

Published in Martial Power, page(s) 136.
