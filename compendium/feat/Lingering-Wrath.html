Lingering Wrath

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Tiefling, fighter, Combat Challenge class feature
Benefit: When you use the infernal wrath racial power against an enemy marked by you, you gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls against that enemy until it is no longer marked by you.


Update (4/9/2010)
In the Benefit entry, replace “the bonuses from infernal wrath” with “a +1 bonus to attack rolls.”

Published in Martial Power, page(s) 137.
