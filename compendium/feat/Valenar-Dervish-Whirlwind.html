Valenar Dervish Whirlwind

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Elf, ranger, Valenar Dervish Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a scimitar or a double scimitar and you have proficiency with that weapon, you the following two benefits.
When you hit an enemy with a martial encounter power, you can shift 1 square to a square adjacent to the enemy, then push the enemy 1 square.
When you hit an enemy with a power associated with this feat, you can shift 3 squares to a square adjacent to the enemy.


Associated Powers: Assess and Strike, Flickering Blades, Leonine Surge, Nimble Defense, Steel Breeze, Two-Weapon Eviscerate

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 65.
