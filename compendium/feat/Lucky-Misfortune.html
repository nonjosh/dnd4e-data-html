Lucky Misfortune [Tiefling Bloodline]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Tiefling, Mantle of Misfortune feat
Benefit: When you slide a creature as a result of your mantle of misfortune power, you gain a +2 bonus to your next attack roll or skill check made before the end of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 383, page(s) 71.
