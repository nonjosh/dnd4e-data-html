Explosive Rage Strike

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, barbarian, rage strike power
Benefit: When you use rage strike, you can reduce the damage it deals by 2[W]. If you do so, the power becomes a close burst 1 that targets each enemy that you can see in the burst.


Update (2/8/2010)
Replace the second sentence of the Benefit entry with the following sentence: “If you do so, the power becomes a close burst 1 that targets each enemy that you can see in the burst.” This change clarifies how changing rage strike into a close burst affects the power’s Target entry.
