Effortless Dilettante

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Half-elf, you must have chosen a melee attack power for your Dilettante racial trait
Benefit: Once per encounter, you can use the power you chose for your Dilettante racial trait as a melee basic attack against a single target as a part of a charge or opportunity attack.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 68.
