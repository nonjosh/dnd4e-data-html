Draconic Augmentation

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Dragonborn, dragon breath racial power, artificer, Augment Energy class feature
Benefit: When an ally hits with an attack benefiting from your Augment Energy class feature, one enemy adjacent to that ally takes damage equal to your Constitution modifier of a damage type matching the type you chose for your dragon breath power.

Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 33.
