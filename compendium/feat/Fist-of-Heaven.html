Fist of Heaven

Epic Tier
Prerequisite: 21st level, avenger, oath of enmity power
Benefit: Your oath of enmity target takes a -2 penalty to saving throws against your avenger powers and your avenger paragon path powers.


Update (2/8/2010)
In the Benefit entry, add “against your avenger powers and your avenger paragon path powers.” This saving throw limitation is part of a larger effort to mitigate the use of saving throw penalties to lock down enemies for the duration of an encounter.

Published in Divine Power, page(s) 141.
