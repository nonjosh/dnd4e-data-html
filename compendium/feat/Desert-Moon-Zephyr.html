Desert Moon Zephyr [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Desert Moon Student feat
Benefit: When you are wielding a heavy blade that has the high crit property and you have proficiency with that weapon, you can shift 1 square as an immediate reaction when an enemy misses you with a melee attack.
When you are attacking with a heavy blade that has the high crit property and you have proficiency with that weapon, you can use a power associated with this feat in place of a melee basic attack when making an opportunity attack.


Associated Powers: Steel Monsoon, Surprise Attack, Thunderous Fury, Wave of War, Withdrawal Gambit

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 114.
