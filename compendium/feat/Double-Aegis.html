Double Aegis

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, Swordmage
Benefit: When you use your Swordmage Aegis power, you can choose to also mark a second target within the burst.
    If both targets attack your allies, you must choose which of the targets to respond to. (You still have only one immediate action per round.)


Revision (10/13/2009)
In the first sentence of the Benefit line, replace “aegis of shielding power or aegis of assault power” with “Swordmage Aegis power.”

Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 138.
