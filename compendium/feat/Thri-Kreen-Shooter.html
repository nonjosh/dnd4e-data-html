Thri-Kreen Shooter

Prerequisite: Thri-kreen race, proficiency with hand crossbow
Benefit: While you wield a melee weapon using your upper limbs, you can use a hand crossbow wielded with your middle limbs to make ranged attacks. Ranged attacks you make in this way do not provoke opportunity attacks.
