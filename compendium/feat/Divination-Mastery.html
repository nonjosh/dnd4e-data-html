Divination Mastery

Prerequisite: Ritual Caster feat, mastery of Read Omens and one other divination ritual
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to initiative checks. You can also master and perform divination rituals as if your level were four higher. Once per day, you can ignore the component cost of a divination ritual of your level or lower that you have mastered.

Published in Dragon Magazine 405.
