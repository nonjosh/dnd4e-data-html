Balinor’s Prey [Divinity]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Channel Divinity class feature, must worship Balinor
Benefit: You gain the Channel Divinity power Balinor’s prey.


Feat UtilityBalinor’s Prey

The hunt is sweet, but the kill is far sweeter.

Encounter      Divine
Minor Action      Ranged 5

Channel Divinity: You can use only one channel divinity power per encounter

Target: One creature

Effect: The next melee or ranged attack that hits the target before the end of your next turn deals 1d6 extra damage.
Level 11: 2d6 extra damage.
Level 21: 3d6 extra damage.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 87.
