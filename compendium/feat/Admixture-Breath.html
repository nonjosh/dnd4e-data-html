Admixture Breath

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, dragonborn, dragon breath racial power, Adaptable Breath feat
Benefit: When you use your dragon breath you can choose for it to deal two types of damage. These damage types must be ones you are capable of delivering with your dragon breath.

Published in Player's Handbook Races: Dragonborn, page(s) 24, Dragon Magazine 365, page(s) 28.
