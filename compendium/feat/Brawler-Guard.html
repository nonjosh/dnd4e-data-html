Brawler Guard

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Fighter, Brawler Style class feature
Benefit: You gain a +1 shield bonus to AC and Reflex while you wield a weapon in one hand and your other hand is free or grabbing a creature.


Update (4/13/2010)
At the end of the feat’s Benefit entry, add “or grabbing a creature.”

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 131.
