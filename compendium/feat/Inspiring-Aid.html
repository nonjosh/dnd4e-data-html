Inspiring Aid

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Inspiring Presence class feature
Benefit: Whenever you or an ally who can hear you and who has line of sight to you takes the aid another, aid attack, or aid defense action, the bonus granted to the target equals +4 instead of +2. This effect replaces the normal +2 bonus to the attack roll. An ally can benefit from this feat only once per attack.
