Rattling Wrath

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Cha 15, tiefling, rogue
Benefit: When you use your infernal wrath racial power, any rattling powers you use against the target before the end of your next turn apply a -4 penalty to the target’s attack rolls instead of a -2 penalty.
