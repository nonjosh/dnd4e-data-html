Cleansing Challenge

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Cha 15, paladin, divine challenge class feature
Benefit: When you use divine challenge to mark an undead creature, you also deal 2 radiant damage to that creature. At 11th level you deal 4 radiant damage, and at 21st level you deal 6 radiant damage.

Published in Dragon Magazine 371, page(s) 8.
