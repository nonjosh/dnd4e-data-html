Scar and Fury

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Half-orc, furious assault racial power, you have a spellscar
Benefit: When you use furious assault, each creature other than you or the triggering enemy that is adjacent to you or the triggering enemy takes 2 fire damage. If you have the Student of the Plague feat, the damage increases to 5 and becomes fire and necrotic damage.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 25.
