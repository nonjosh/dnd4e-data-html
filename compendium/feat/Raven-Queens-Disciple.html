Raven Queen's Disciple

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Revenant, assassin, Bleak Disciple Guild Training
Benefit: When you gain temporary hit points from your Guild Training, increase the temporary hit points you gain by 1. If the unbloodied target you hit is undead or a shadow creature, increase the temporary hit points you gain by 2 instead.

Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 51.
