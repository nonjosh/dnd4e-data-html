Valenar Rider Training

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Elf
Benefit: You gain proficiency with all simple and military spears, plus a +2 feat bonus to damage rolls with shortbows and all simple and military spears while you are riding a mount. The bonus increases to +3 at 11th level and +4 at 21st level.

In addition, you can use your elven accuracy racial power to reroll an attack roll your mount makes.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 63.
