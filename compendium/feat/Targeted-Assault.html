Targeted Assault

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Barbarian
Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls against any creature that has you marked.


Update (11/23/2009)
On the Benefit line, replace “any creature marking you” with “any creature that has you marked.”

Explanation: The benefit is incorrect. This change represent the feat’s intent.
