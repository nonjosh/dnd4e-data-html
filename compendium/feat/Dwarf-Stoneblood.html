Dwarf Stoneblood

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Dwarf, fighter, Battlerager Vigor class feature
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to the number of temporary hit points you gain from using an invigorating power. The bonus increases to 4 at 11th level and 6 at 21st level.
