Fey Step Trailblazer

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, Eladrin
Benefit: When you use your fey step power, you can also teleport one adjacent willing creature that is your size or smaller with you at the same time. When you arrive at your destination, place the creature that traveled with you in any square adjacent to you.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 15.
