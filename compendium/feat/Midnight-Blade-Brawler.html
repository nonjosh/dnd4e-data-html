Midnight Blade Brawler [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Fighter, Midnight Blade Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a melee light blade and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
Whenever you hit an enemy with a martial encounter or daily power during your turn, you increase the distance you shift by 1 square until the end of your turn.
When you hit an enemy with a power associated with this feat, you gain a +2 bonus to the damage roll.


Associated Powers: Advance Lunge, Diamond Shield Defense, Driving Flurry, Meticulous Stab, Storm of Blows, Sudden Surge

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 121.
