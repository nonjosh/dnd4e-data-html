Psionic Vampire

Prerequisite: Vampire, Psionic Augmentation class feature
Benefit: Once per encounter, when your augmented psionic attack power or psionic encounter attack power hits at least one enemy, you gain a healing surge.
    Once per encounter, as a minor action, you can lose a healing surge to gain 2 power points that expire at the end of your turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 402.
