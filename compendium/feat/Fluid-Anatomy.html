Fluid Anatomy

Epic Tier
Prerequisite: 21st level, changeling
Benefit: Whenever a creature scores a critical hit against you, make a saving throw. On a save, the critical hit is instead a normal hit.


Update (11/23/2009)
In the feat’s Prerequisite line, replace “doppelganger” with “changeling.”

Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 94.
