Eye Wand [Vistani Bloodline]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Wizard, Wand of Accuracy class feature, Vistani Heritage
Benefit: If you miss with an attack roll modified by your Wand of Accuracy class feature, you can use evil eye of the Vistani, if available, as a free action against the target you missed.

Published in Dragon Magazine 380, page(s) 85.
