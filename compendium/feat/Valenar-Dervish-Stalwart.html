Valenar Dervish Stalwart

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Elf, fighter, Valenar Dervish Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a scimitar or a double scimitar and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
After resolving a martial encounter attack power, you can shift 2 squares, then slide one target you hit with that attack 2 squares to a square adjacent to you.
When you hit an enemy with a power associated with this feat, instead of pushing the enemy, you can slide the enemy the same distance. If the power already allows you to slide the enemy, you can slide it 1 additional square.


Associated Powers: Scattering Swing, Sweeping Slash, Twofold Torment, Vorpal Tornado, Weaponmaster's Lure

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 64.
