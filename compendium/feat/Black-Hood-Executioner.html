Black Hood Executioner [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Black Hood Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a two-handed axe and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
If you score a critical hit, each enemy adjacent to you takes damage equal to your Strength modifier.
You can use a power associated with this feat in place of a melee basic attack when making an opportunity attack.


Associated Powers: Blood Begets Blood, Deadly Inspiration, Headstrong Bravery, Inspiring War Cry, Provoke Overextension, Warlord's Doom

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 113.
