Wrathful Hunter

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, tiefling, ranger, Hunter’s Quarry class feature
Benefit: If you use your infernal wrath racial power against your quarry, you gain a +3 power bonus to your next attack against the target made before the end of your next turn.


Update (4/9/2010)
In the Benefit entry, replace “(instead of +1)” with “made before the end of your next turn.”

Published in Martial Power, page(s) 145.
