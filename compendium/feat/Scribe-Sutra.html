Scribe Sutra

Prerequisite: Runepriest
Benefit: You can master and perform rituals in the warding category, and the Brew Potion, Gentle Repose, and Magic Circle rituals, as if you had the Ritual Caster feat. Furthermore, you can create scrolls in half the normal time (that is, the same amount of time it takes to create a ritual book).

Published in Dragon Magazine 404.
