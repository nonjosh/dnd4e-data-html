Champion of Raam [Greater Pact]

Prerequisite: Warlock, Sorcerer-King Pact class feature, Favored of Raam feat
Benefit: In addition to regaining the use of your fell might, whenever you drop an enemy you have cursed to 0 hit points, you can shift a number of squares equal to 3 + your Intelligence modifier.
    You can also expend your fell might to augment any power associated with this feat. When you hit an enemy with an associated power augmented by this feat, the target cannot include you as a target in its attacks until the start of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 390.
