Mantle of Unease [Foulborn Bloodline]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Foulborn Heritage, ardent, Ardent Mantle class feature
Benefit: When an enemy not immune to fear scores a critical hit within the radius of your Ardent Mantle, it takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls until the end of its next turn. If you are bloodied, you also push the enemy 1 square.

Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 143.
