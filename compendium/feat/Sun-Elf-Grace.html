Sun Elf Grace

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Eladrin
Benefit: If you use your fey step power before you first become bloodied in an encounter, you gain a +1 bonus to all defenses until the end of your next turn. Once you’ve become bloodied in an encounter, you can’t gain this benefit even if you are healed later in the fight.
Special: You cannot select this feat if you have the Moon Elf Resilience feat.

Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 137.
