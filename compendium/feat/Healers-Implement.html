Healer’s Implement

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Cleric
Benefit: When you let a creature spend a healing surge to regain hit points with any of your cleric healing powers, add your holy symbol’s enhancement bonus to the hit points the recipient regains.


Update (4/13/2010)
In the Benefit entry of the feat, replace “grant healing” with “let a creature spend a healing surge to regain hit points.”

Published in Divine Power, page(s) 135.
