Memories of Destruction

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Deva, memory of a thousand lifetimes racial power, you have a spellscar
Benefit: Whenever you are subjected to a dazing or stunning effect that a save can end, you can expend memory of a thousand lifetimes to make a saving throw against that effect as an immediate interrupt. If you have the Student of the Plague feat, each enemy adjacent to you takes 5 fire damage when you expend memory of a thousand lifetimes in this manner.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 24.
