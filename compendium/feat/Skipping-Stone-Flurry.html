Skipping Stone Flurry

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Monk, Flurry of Blows class feature
Benefit: When you use your Flurry of Blows power and you have a sling in hand, you can replace one normal target of that power with one creature within 10 squares of you. This does not provoke opportunity attacks.

Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 134.
