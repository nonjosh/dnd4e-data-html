Merciless Killer

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, Drow
Benefit: When a bloodied foe grants combat advantage to you, you gain a +5 bonus to the damage roll against that enemy.


Addition (1/21/2009)
In the Benefit line, add “against that enemy” to the end of the sentence.

Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 138.
