Keeper of the Black Flame

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Drow, assassin, assassin’s shroud power
Benefit: When you hit a target that has at least one of your shrouds on it with your darkfire racial power the target is subject to an additional shroud.


Revision (2/24/2010)
Change benefit to read “When you hit a target that has at least one of your shrouds on it with your darkfire racial power the target is subject to an additional shroud.”

Published in Dragon Magazine 382, page(s) 30.
