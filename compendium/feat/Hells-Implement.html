Hell's Implement [Multiclass]

Prerequisite: Warlord, Pact Initiate (infernal pact)
Benefit: You can use a polearm as a warlock implement.


Update (2/8/2010)
In the Benefit entry, replace the text with “You can use a polearm as a warlock implement.” This change synchronizes this feat with Player’s Handbook 3 clarifications to implements and weapons.
