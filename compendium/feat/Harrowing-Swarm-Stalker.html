Harrowing Swarm Stalker [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Rogue, Harrowing Swarm Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a bow or a crossbow and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
You do not provoke an opportunity attack from the target of your attack.
You can use a power associated with this feat as a ranged power instead of a melee power and you can attack using a crossbow instead of a light blade.


Associated Powers: Audacious Strike, Dismaying Slash, Felling Gash, Setup Strike, Skirmishing Strike, Unbalancing Attack

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 116.
