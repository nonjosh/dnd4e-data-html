Ogremight Student [Lesser Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Fighter or warlord; proficiency with any two-handed hammer or two-handed mace
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Intimidate checks.
When you are attacking with a two-handed hammer or a two-handed mace and you have proficiency with that weapon, you also gain the following benefit.
When you hit an enemy with a power associated with this feat, that enemy takes a -1 penalty to all defenses until the end of your next turn.


Associated Powers: Brash Assault, Brash Strike

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 125.
