Phantasmal Elixir

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Gnome, fade away racial power, artificer, shielding elixir power
Benefit: You can use your fade away power when you or an ally affected by your shielding elixir power takes damage. If the ally triggered the power, both you and the ally become invisible until you attack or until the end of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 34.
