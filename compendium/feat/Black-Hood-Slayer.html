Black Hood Slayer [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Ranger, Black Hood Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a two-handed axe and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
If you score a critical hit, each enemy adjacent to you takes damage equal to your Dexterity modifier.
You can use Dexterity instead of Strength for attack rolls and damage rolls when using a power associated with this feat.


Associated Powers: Avenging Charge,
Bloodlust Strike: only when used as a melee attack
Reproving Strike: only when used as a melee attackSteel Breeze, Toppling Rush
