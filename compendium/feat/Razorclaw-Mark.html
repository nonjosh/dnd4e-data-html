Razorclaw Mark

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Razorclaw shifter, fighter
Benefit: While you are under the effect of your razorclaw shifting racial power, any creature marked by you takes a -3 penalty to attack rolls for making an attack that does not include you as a target. This penalty replaces the normal penalty for having a creature marked.

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 138.
