Hellfire Teleport

Epic Tier
Prerequisite: 21st level, tiefling
Benefit: Once per turn, when you teleport, you deal 5 + your Charisma or Intelligence modifier fire damage to each enemy in or adjacent to the square you leave.


Update (6/14/2010)
In the Benefit entry, replace “When” with “Once per turn, when.”

Published in Player's Handbook Races: Tiefling, page(s) 28.
