Hobgoblin Beast Companion

Prerequisite: Hobgoblin, ranger, Beast Mastery class feature, hobgoblin discipline racial power
Benefit: When you use hobgoblin discipline and your beast companion is adjacent to you, you can also end one effect a save can end affecting your beast companion.
    In addition, your beast companion gains a +1 feat bonus to all defenses while it is adjacent to you.

Published in Dragon Magazine 419.
