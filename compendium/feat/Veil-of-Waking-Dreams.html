Veil of Waking Dreams

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, warlock, Warlock’s Curse class feature, star pact
Benefit: Whenever you deal Warlock’s Curse damage to a target and do so with a warlock daily attack power, the target is dazed (save ends). If the power already subjects the target to a dazing effect that a save can end, the target is instead dazed and takes ongoing 5 psychic damage (save ends both).

Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 31.
