Extra Manifestation

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Genasi
Benefit: Select a new genasi elemental manifestation. When you take a short rest or an extended rest, you can adopt this new elemental manifestation instead of the one you had been exhibiting.
Special: You can take this feat multiple times, choosing a new manifestation each time. When you take a short rest or an extended rest, you can choose to switch between any of the elemental manifestations you know.

Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 133.
