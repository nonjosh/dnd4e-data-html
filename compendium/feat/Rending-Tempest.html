Rending Tempest

Epic Tier
Prerequisite: 21st level, Dex 17, fighter or ranger
Benefit: During your turn, when you hit a target with a melee attack using a power that requires the use of two weapons, the target takes 1[W] extra damage (off-hand weapon) when you hit it with any other melee attacks until the end of that turn.
