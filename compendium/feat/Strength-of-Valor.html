Strength of Valor

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Bard, Virtue of Valor class feature
Benefit: When you grant an ally temporary hit points with your Virtue of Valor, that ally also gains a +2 bonus to the next damage roll he or she makes before the end of his or her next turn.


Revision (10/13/2009)
On the Benefit line, replace “your next turn” with “his or her next turn.”

Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 189.
