Potent Restorables

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Artificer
Benefit: Your artificer healing powers restore 2 extra hit points. The extra hit points increase to 3 at 6th level, to 4 at 11th level, to 5 at 16th level, to 6 at 21st level, and to 7 at 26th level.


Update (11/23/2009)
In the first sentence of the Benefit line, add “artificer” before “healing powers.”

Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 92.
