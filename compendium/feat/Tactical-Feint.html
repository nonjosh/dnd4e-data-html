Tactical Feint

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Tactical Presence class feature
Benefit: Whenever you spend an action point to make an extra attack, one ally within 3 squares of you gains a bonus equal to one-half your Intelligence modifier to his or her next attack roll against a target of your attack before the end of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 31.
