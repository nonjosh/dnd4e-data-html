Focused Breath

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Dragonborn, dragon breath racial power
Benefit: When you use your dragon breath, you can choose to make it a close blast 1 instead of a close blast 3. If you do, add 2 to the damage roll. Increase this to 4 at 11th level, and to 6 at 21st level.

Published in Player's Handbook Races: Dragonborn, page(s) 23.
