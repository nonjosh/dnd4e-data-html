Elsir Hammer General [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Elsir Hammer Student feat
Benefit: When you are wielding a one-handed hammer or a one-handed pick and you have proficiency with that weapon, you can shift 1 square as an immediate reaction whenever an adjacent enemy that is marked by you or your ally shifts away from you.
When you are attacking with a one-handed hammer or a one-handed pick and you have proficiency with that weapon, one ally within 5 squares of you can shift 2 squares as a free action when you hit with a power associated with this feat.


Associated Powers: Chimera Battlestrike, Grim Mark, Hail of Steel, Hold the Line, Ringing Clarity, Surprise Attack

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 115.
