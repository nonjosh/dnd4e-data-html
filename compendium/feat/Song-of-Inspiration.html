Song of Inspiration

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, bard, Song of Rest class feature
Benefit: At the end of any rest during which you use your Song of Rest, you and each ally who can hear you can spend a healing surge. Each character who spends a healing surge in this manner gains temporary hit points equal to 5 + your Charisma modifier, instead of regaining hit points.

Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 133.
