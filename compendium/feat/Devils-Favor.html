Devil's Favor

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlock, Infernal Pact
Benefit: Whenever you use your second wind, you can choose to regain no hit points to instead gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls, saving throws, and damage rolls until the end of your next turn.


Deletion (2/18/2010)
Remove defenses from list of bonuses.
