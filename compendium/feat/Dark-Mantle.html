Dark Mantle

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Cleric, healing word power, must worship the Raven Queen
Benefit: Whenever you use healing word, you can forgo granting the extra hit points to instead grant resist necrotic 5 to the target until the end of your next turn. Increase the resistance to 10 at 11th level, and 20 at 21st level.

Published in Dragon Magazine 380, page(s) 64.
