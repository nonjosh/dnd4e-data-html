Distracting Companion

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, ranger, Beast Mastery class feature, Hunter’s Quarry class feature
Benefit: If your quarry is adjacent to your beast companion when you hit your quarry with an attack, your quarry takes a –2 penalty to attack rolls against you and your beast companion until the start of your next turn.

Published in Martial Power, page(s) 141.
