Spirited Shadows

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Drow, assassin, cloud of darkness power, shadow step power
Benefit: When you use shadow step while within the area of your cloud of darkness power, you can instead teleport a number of squares equal to your Dexterity modifier. One enemy adjacent to you at the end of the teleport grants combat advantage to you until the end of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 35.
