Thri-Kreen Wild Talent

Prerequisite: Thri-kreen race, Psionic Augmentation class feature, thri-kreen claws racial power
Benefit: You gain one wild talent cantrip of your choice. Additionally, once per encounter, you can lose a use of your thri-kreen claws power as a minor action to regain 1 power point.

Published in Dragon Magazine 411.
