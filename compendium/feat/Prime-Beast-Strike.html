Prime Beast Strike

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Halfling, ranger, Beast Mastery class feature
Benefit: If none of your allies is nearer to your target than either you or your beast companion, you gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls with melee and ranged attacks against that target.
