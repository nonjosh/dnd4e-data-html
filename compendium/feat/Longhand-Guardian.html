Longhand Guardian [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Fighter, Longhand Student feat
Benefit: If you are attacking with a two-handed polearm or a two-handed spear and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
When you hit an enemy with an attack power, you gain a +1 feat bonus to AC until the end of your next turn.
You can use a power associated with this feat in place of a melee basic attack when making an attack as a result of your Combat Challenge class feature.


Associated Powers: Adamantine Strike, Griffon's Wrath, Paralyzing Strike, Probing Attack, Wild Strike

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 120.
