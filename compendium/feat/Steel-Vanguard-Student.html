Steel Vanguard Student [Lesser Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Fighter or warlord; proficiency with any two-handed heavy blade
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Athletics checks.
When you are attacking with a two-handed heavy blade and you have proficiency with that weapon, you also gain the following benefit.
When attacking with a power associated with this feat, you can score a critical hit on a roll of 19–20.


Associated Powers: Cleave, Viper’s Strike

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 130.
