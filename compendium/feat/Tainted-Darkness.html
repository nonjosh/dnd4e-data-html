Tainted Darkness

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Drow, darkfire racial power, you have a spellscar
Benefit: When you hit an enemy with darkfire, each time an attack hits that enemy before the end of your next turn, the enemy takes 2 extra fire damage. If you have the Student of the Plague feat, when you hit an enemy with darkfire, all creatures have concealment from the enemy until the end of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 26.
