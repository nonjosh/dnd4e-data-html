Rites of Spirits' Blood

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Tiefling, any primal class
Benefit: Your bonus to attack rolls from your Bloodhunt racial trait increases to +2. When you use your infernal wrath racial power, you gain a +2 power bonus to damage rolls against the target until the end of your next turn.
