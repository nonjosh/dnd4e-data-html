Psionic Fury

Prerequisite: Half-orc, furious assault racial power, any psionic class
Benefit: Your furious assault power gains the augmentable keyword. When you would use furious assault, you can spend 1 power point to deal damage to each enemy adjacent to you equal to the higher of your Strength or Dexterity modifier. This damage has the same damage type as the attack that triggered this power.

Published in Dragon Magazine 389.
