Rose King's Lunge

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger, cleric, or paladin; proficiency with longsword
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Athletics checks.
Once per turn when you hit an enemy with an attack power associated with this feat while using a longsword, you can move 2 squares, even if you are restrained or immobilized.


Associated Powers: Bond of Pursuit, Righteous Brand, Valiant Strike

Published in Dragon Magazine 386, page(s) 65.
