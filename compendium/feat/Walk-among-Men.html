Walk among Men

Prerequisite: Satyr
Benefit: During a short rest, you can change your appearance to that of a male human, eladrin, or elf. Your face and build remain recognizably yours, but you otherwise appear to be a member of the chosen race. This illusion lasts until you end it as a minor action or until you die.

Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 130.
