Rending Chains Flayer [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Ranger, Rending Chains Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a flail and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
Whenever you hit an enemy with a martial power, it takes damage equal to your Wisdom modifier if it shifts before the end of your next turn.
You can use Dexterity instead of Strength for attack rolls and damage rolls when using a power associated with this feat.


Associated Powers: Armor Splinter, Claws of the Griffon, Death Rend, Leonine Surge, Nonchalant Collapse, Untamed Outburst

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 128.
