Elemental Recovery

Prerequisite: Genasi, any psionic class
Benefit: When you would take typed damage and you have not yet expended your racial power, you can expend 1 power point as an immediate interrupt to gain resist 5 to the triggering damage type until the start of your next turn.
    At 11th level, the resistance increases to 10. At 21st level, the resistance increases to 20.

Published in Dragon Magazine 389.
