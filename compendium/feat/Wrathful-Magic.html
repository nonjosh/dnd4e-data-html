Wrathful Magic

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Tiefling, any arcane class
Benefit: When you miss with an arcane attack power, you can use your infernal wrath racial power as a free action. You gain the benefits of infernal wrath against the target you missed, instead of against an enemy that hit you. Treat that target as the enemy that hit you for the purpose of other feats and abilities that modify the effects of infernal wrath.


Update (4/9/2010)
In the second sentence of the Benefit entry, delete “since your last turn.”
