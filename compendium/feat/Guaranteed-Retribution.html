Guaranteed Retribution

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger, Censure of Retribution class feature, oath of enmity power
Benefit: When any enemy other than your oath of enmity target hits you, you gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls against your oath of enmity target until the end of your next turn.


Update (2/8/2010)
In the Benefit entry, delete “feat.” This change ensures that this feat continues to provide a benefit despite changes to the Weapon Expertise feat.

Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 185.
