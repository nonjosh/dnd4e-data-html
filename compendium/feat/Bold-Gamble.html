Bold Gamble

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Bravura Presence class feature
Benefit: Whenever you spend an action point to make an extra attack, you can choose to use this feat. If the attack hits, the enemy grants combat advantage until the end of your next turn. If the attack misses, you grant combat advantage to the enemy until the end of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 29.
