Invigorated Healing

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Half-orc, cleric
Benefit: When you are first bloodied in an encounter, until the end of your next turn whenever you use a healing power, the targets of that power heal additional hit points equal to the number of temporary hit points you gained from your Half-Orc Resilience racial trait.

Published in Dragon Magazine 386, page(s) 59.
