Ruthless Killer

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Druid, grasping claws power, wild shape power
Benefit: When you hit a target with the grasping claws power, instead of slowing the target you can instead choose for the target to be immobilized until the end of your next turn. If you are ever not adjacent to the target, the immobilized effect ends.

Published in Dragon Magazine 382, page(s) 54.
