Pacifist's Reward

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Cleric
Benefit: When you hit with an attack that doesn’t deal damage, if you didn’t deal any damage on your turn, gain 2 temporary hit points at the end of the turn.
The temporary hit points increase to 3 at 11th level, and to 4 at 21st level.

Published in Dragon Magazine 379, page(s) 55.
