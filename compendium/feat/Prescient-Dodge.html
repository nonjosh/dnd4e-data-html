Prescient Dodge [Augment]

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, Psionic Augmentation class feature
Benefit: During each encounter, you gain a +2 bonus to all defenses until the start of your first turn.
    Whenever you hit an enemy with an unaugmented attack power, you can spend 1 power point to gain a +2 power bonus to all defenses until the start of your next turn.

Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 138.
