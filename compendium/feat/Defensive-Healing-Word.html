Defensive Healing Word

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Cleric, healing word power
Benefit: When you use your healing word, the target also gains a power bonus to all defenses equal to your Charisma modifier against the next attack made against him or her before the end of your next turn.


Update (4/13/2010)
In the Benefit entry, add “before the end of your next turn” to the end of the last sentence

Published in Divine Power, page(s) 131.
