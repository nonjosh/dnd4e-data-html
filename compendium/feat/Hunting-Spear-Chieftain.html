Hunting Spear Chieftain [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Hunting Spear Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a spear and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
You gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls against creatures that are larger than you.
You can use a power associated with this feat in place of a melee basic attack when charging.


Associated Powers: Deadly Inspiration, Fury of the Sirocco, Incite Heroism, Inspiring War Cry, Surround Foe, Wounding Focus

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 117.
