Infernal Sneak Attack

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, tiefling, rogue, Sneak Attack class feature
Benefit: When you deal your Sneak Attack damage, you can expend your infernal wrath to deal 1d6 extra fire damage for each die of Sneak Attack damage you deal.
