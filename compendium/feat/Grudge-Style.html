Grudge Style [Arena Fighting]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Wis 13 or dwarf race, any martial class
Benefit: You gain a benefit with any of the following exploits you possess.


Associated Powers:
Careful Attack: If the target dealt you damage since the end of your last turn, you deal extra damage with this exploit equal to your Wisdom modifier.
Furious Smash: If the target dealt you damage since the end of your last turn, you gain a +2 bonus to the attack roll with this exploit.
Riposte Strike: If you hit with the immediate interrupt attack this exploit grants, the target takes a -2 penalty to AC and Reflex defense until the end of your next turn.
Sure Strike: If the target dealt you damage since the end of your last turn, you deal extra damage with this exploit equal to your Wisdom modifier.

Published in Dragon Magazine Annual, Dragon Magazine 368, page(s) 60.
