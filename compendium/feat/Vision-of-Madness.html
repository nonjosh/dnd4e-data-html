Vision of Madness [Foulborn Bloodline]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Foulborn Heritage, psion
Benefit: When you score a critical hit against an enemy using an augmented psion at-will power, that enemy is also dazed until the end of your next turn. If the enemy is already dazed or is dazed by the power, it instead takes 5 extra psychic damage.

Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 143.
