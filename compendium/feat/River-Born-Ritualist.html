River-Born Ritualist

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Halfling
Benefit: You have mastered the Animal Messenger and Water Walk rituals, and can perform them as if you had the Ritual Caster feat. You can use these rituals without paying the component cost, each once per day.
In addition, you gain a +2 feat bonus to Nature checks.

Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 83.
