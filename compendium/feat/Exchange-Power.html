Exchange Power

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Psion, Send Thoughts power
Benefit: When you use send thoughts to deliver a message to an ally who has power points, you can lose 1 power point to allow that ally to regain 1 power point.


Update (6/14/2010)
Replace “transfer 1 power point of yours to that ally” with “lose 1 power point to allow that ally to regain 1 power point.”

Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 180.
