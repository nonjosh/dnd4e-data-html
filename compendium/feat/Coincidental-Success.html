Coincidental Success

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: Sorcerer, Wild Magic class feature
Benefit: Once per encounter when you make a skill check as a part of a skill challenge, choose 1–10 or 11–20. If the result of the d20 roll for that skill check is within the range selected, you automatically earn one success for that skill challenge.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 47.
