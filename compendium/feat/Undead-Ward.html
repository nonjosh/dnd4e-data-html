Undead Ward

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, cleric, Channel Divinity: turn undead class feature
Benefit: Your turn undead class feature gains the zone keyword. Whenever you use this power, the burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of your next turn. Any undead creature that enters or starts its turn within the zone takes 1d6 + Wisdom modifier radiant damage. A creature can take this damage only once per turn.
