Arbiter of Justice

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, avenger, oath of enmity power
Benefit: You deal +2 damage against bloodied targets. Additionally, whenever an ally bloodies or reduces your oath of enmity target to 0 hit points, you may act as though you had bloodied or reduced the target to 0 hit points.

Published in Dragon Magazine 382, page(s) 40.
