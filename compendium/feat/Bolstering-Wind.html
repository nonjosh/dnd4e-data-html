Bolstering Wind

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, ardent, Ardent Mantle class feature
Benefit: When you use your second wind, each bloodied ally within the radius of your Ardent Mantle regains hit points equal to 5 + your Wisdom modifier. The hit points regained increase to 10 + your Wisdom modifier at 21st level.

Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 135.
