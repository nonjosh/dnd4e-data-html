Reinforcing Healing

Epic Tier
Prerequisite: 21st level, artificer
Benefit: Whenever you restore hit points to an ally using an artificer power, that ally gains a +2 bonus to all defenses until the end of your next turn.


Update (11/23/2009)
In the Benefit line, add “using an artificer power” after “restore hit points to an ally.”

Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 94.
