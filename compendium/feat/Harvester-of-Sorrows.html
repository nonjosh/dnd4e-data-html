Harvester of Sorrows

Epic Tier
Prerequisite: 21st level, avenger, oath of enmity power, must worship the Raven Queen
Benefit: Whenever you reduce the target of your oath of enmity to 0 hit points, any enemy you can see within 10 squares grants combat advantage to you until the end of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 380, page(s) 65.
