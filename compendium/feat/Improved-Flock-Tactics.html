Improved Flock Tactics

Prerequisite: Kenku, flock tactics racial power
Benefit: When your flock tactics power causes an enemy to grant combat advantage to you, you also gain a +3 feat bonus to damage rolls against that enemy until the start of your next turn. When you use your flock tactics power to take the aid attack action, your ally also gains combat advantage on his or her next attack against the chosen enemy.
