Shapeshifting Contortionist

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Changeling
Benefit: You can use the escape action as a minor action instead of a move action.
In addition, you do not grant combat advantage or take a penalty to attack rolls while squeezing.


Update (11/23/2009)
In the feat's Prerequisite column, replace “Doppelganger” with “Changeling.”

Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 92.
