Godsworn Assault

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger, must worship Erathis
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Religion checks. In addition, whenever you score a critical hit, one ally you can see gains a +1 bonus to attack rolls until the end of your next turn as a godsworn boon. You can benefit from only one godsworn boon as a result of any given critical hit.

Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 42.
