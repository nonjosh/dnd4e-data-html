Godsworn Avalanche

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger, must worship Kord
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Religion checks. In addition, whenever you score a critical hit, you can also push the target a number of squares equal to your Dexterity or Intelligence modifier as a godsworn boon. You can benefit from only one godsworn boon as a result of any given critical hit.

Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 42.
