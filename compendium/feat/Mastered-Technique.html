Mastered Technique

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, fighter, Fighter Weapon Talent class feature
Benefit: You gain a +1 feat bonus to AC when you hit with an attack using a weapon of the style you chose for the Fighter Weapon Talent class feature until the end of your next turn.
