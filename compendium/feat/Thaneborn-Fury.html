Thaneborn Fury

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Barbarian, Thaneborn Triumph class feature
Benefit: When you hit a creature with a melee basic attack granted by the Rampage class feature, the target takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls until the end of your next turn. A creature that is immune to fear is not subject to this penalty.

Published in Dragon Magazine 386, page(s) 51.
