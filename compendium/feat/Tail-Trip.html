Tail Trip

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, tiefling, fighter, Combat Challenge class feature
Benefit: When you hit an enemy with an opportunity attack or an attack granted by your Combat Challenge, you can knock the target prone.


Update (9/10/2010)
Add “11th level” as a prerequisite.

Published in Player's Handbook Races: Tiefling, page(s) 28.
