Student of the Cosmos

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Sorcerer, Cosmic Magic class feature
Benefit: You gain an additional benefit based on your current phase in the cosmic cycle.
Phase of the Sun: You gain a +2 feat bonus to initiative checks and Heal checks.
Phase of the Moon: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Bluff checks and Stealth checks.
Phase of the Stars: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Insight checks and Perception checks.

Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 129.
