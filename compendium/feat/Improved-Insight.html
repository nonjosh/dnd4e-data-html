Improved Insight

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Insightful Presence class feature
Benefit: When an ally benefits from your Insightful Presence class feature, that ally can forgo the normal bonus to all defenses to instead gain a bonus to a single defense equal to your Charisma or Wisdom modifier until the end of your next turn.

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 136.
