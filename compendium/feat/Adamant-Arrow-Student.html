Adamant Arrow Student [Lesser Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Ranger or warlord; proficiency with greatbow or longbow
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Perception checks.
When you are attacking with a greatbow or a longbow and you hit an enemy with a power associated with this feat, you can push that enemy 1 square.


Associated Powers: Nimble Strike, Paint the Bulls-Eye

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 110.
