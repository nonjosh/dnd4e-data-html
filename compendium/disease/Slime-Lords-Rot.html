Level 14 DiseaseSlime Lord’s Rot

Oozing pustules herald the first stages of this disease, which ends with the victim’s collapse into a pool of ooze ready to serve its new master.

Stage 0: The target is cured.
Stage 1: Initial Effect: The target loses a healing surge that it cannot regain until cured of the disease. In addition, the target is slowed.
Stage 2: The target loses a healing surge that it cannot regain until cured of the disease. In addition, the target cannot shift.
Stage 3: Final State: The target dies and becomes a spawn of Juiblex sludge dredge.
Check: At the end of each extended rest, the target makes a check if it is at stage 1 or 2.
17 or lower: The stage of the disease increases by one.
18-22: No Change
23 or higher: The stage of the disease decreases by one.

Published in Demonomicon, page(s) 149.
