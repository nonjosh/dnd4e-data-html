Level 10 DiseaseWastelands Sickness

An essence remains of the magical doom that slew thousands.

Stage 0: The target is cured.
Stage 1: Initial Effect: The target loses a healing surge that it cannot regain until cured of the disease.
Stage 2: The target takes a -2 penalty to speed.
Stage 3: Final State: The target is weakened.
Check: At the end of each extended rest, the target makes an Endurance check if it is at stage 1 or 2.
15 or lower: The stage of the disease increases by one.
16-20: No Change
21 or higher: The stage of the disease decreases by one.

Published in Demonomicon, page(s) 73.
