Level 2 DiseaseSkinrot

Often spread by a touch of a disease-ridden corpse, skinrot withers its victim.

Stage 0: The target recovers from the disease.
Stage 1: Initial Effect: The target takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls.
Stage 2: Final State: While bloodied, the target takes a -4 penalty to attack rolls and cannot spend healing surges.
Check: At the end of each extended rest, the target makes an Endurance check if it is at stage 1 or 2.
9 or lower: The stage of the disease increases by one.
10-14: No Change
15 or higher: The stage of the disease decreases by one.

Published in Open Grave, page(s) 180.
