Level 11 DiseaseMoon Rage

Stage 0: The target recovers from the disease.
Stage 1: Initial Effect: The target takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls as its hands begin to grow fur and claws.
Stage 2: The target gains a Strength-based claw attack that deals 1d6 damage. The target can no longer wield weapons or hold implements. This effect remains as long as the subject is diseased.
