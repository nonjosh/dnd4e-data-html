At-Will TerrainLava Pit

A fiery blob of lava launches itself at a nearby target.

Minor Action

Requirement: You must be trained in Arcana and must have seen a creature be attacked with this terrain power.

Check: Arcana check (DC 13) to understand how to control and attack with the Lava Pit.

Success: You can use this terrain power automatically until the end of the encounter. Each use counts as a standard action. You must be within 3 squares of the lava to trigger the attack.

Target: One creature within 3 squares of the lava pit.

Attack: +6 vs. Reflex

Hit: 1d4 fire damage plus ongoing 3 fire damage (save ends).

Published in Dungeon Magazine 179, page(s) 35.
