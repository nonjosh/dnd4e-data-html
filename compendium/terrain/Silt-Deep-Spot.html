At-Will TerrainSilt Deep Spot

Within a shallow pool of silt, occasional deep spots can be exploited by observant combatants.

Standard Action

Check: Nature check (hard DC) to recognize a deeper spot in the silt pool.

Target: One creature you choose within a silt pool

Attack: Level + 3 vs. Reflex

Hit: The target is restrained (save ends).

Published in Dark Sun Creature Catalog, page(s) 137.
