Single-Use TerrainSalt Pile

A pile of wind-blown salt rests on the ground. A savvy combatant can use it to blind a foe or harm an injured foe.

Standard Action

Requirement: You must be adjacent to the salt pile.

Check: Athletics check (easy DC) to toss or kick a spray of particles.

Success: The salt showers the target.

Target: One creature in a close blast 3

Attack: Level + 3 vs. Reflex

Hit: The target is blinded (save ends). If the target is bloodied, it is instead blinded and slowed (save ends both).

Published in Dark Sun Creature Catalog, page(s) 136.
