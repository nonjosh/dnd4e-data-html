Single-Use TerrainTable of Combustibles

A table holds beakers and vials containing volatile combustibles that have a violent reaction when mixed together or thrown to the ground.

Standard Action

Requirement: You must be adjacent to the table.

Check: Athletics check (hard DC) to overturn the table. You can drop prone as part of the standard action to avoid the attack.

Success: The table is overturned.

Target: Each creature in a close burst 2 centered on the table

Attack: Level + 3 vs. Reflex

Hit: Ongoing 5 fire and poison damage per tier (save ends).

Published in Dungeon Master's Guide 2, page(s) 63.
