Galeb Duhr Rockcaller
Medium elemental humanoid (earth)
Level 11 Controller XP 600

Initiative +5        Senses Perception +12; tremorsense 10
HP 118; Bloodied 59
AC 25; Fortitude 26, Reflex 21, Will 22
Immune petrification, poison
Speed 4 (earth walk), burrow 6

Slam (standard, at-will)

+16 vs AC; 2d8+4 damage.

Rolling Attack (standard, at-will)

The galeb duhr rockcaller moves up to 4 squares and then attacks an adjacent target; +14 vs Fortitude; 2d8+6 damage, and the target is pushed 1 square and knocked prone.

Earthen Grasp (standard, at-will)

An earthen fist rises up to restrain the target; Ranged 10; +14 vs Fortitude; the target is restrained (save ends). The target must be in direct contact with the ground or the attack fails. The rockcaller can use earthen grasp only against one creature at a time.

Rocky Terrain (minor, at-will)

Ranged 10; up to 4 squares within range become difficult terrain. The squares need not be contiguous, but the affected terrain must consist of earth or stone
