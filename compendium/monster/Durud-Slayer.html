The durud slayer can make a basic attack, shift 1 square, or move its speed.

The durud slayer teleports 8 squares.

Slayer's Step (move; recharge ) Teleportation

The durud slayer and any creature it has grabbed teleport 8 squares. If the durud slayer has a creature grabbed, that creature must end its teleport adjacent to the slayer.
