+31 vs Reflex; 7 fire damage, and ongoing 5 fire damage (save ends).

Firestorm (standard, encounter) Fire

Area burst 1 within 10; +31 vs Reflex; ongoing 10 fire damage (save ends). A target already taking ongoing fire damage instead increases that ongoing damage by 5.

The flamebow explodes and is destroyed: close burst 5; targets enemies taking ongoing fire damage; +31 vs Fortitude; the target and all allies adjacent to the target take 14 fire damage. Miss: The target takes 7 fire damage.

Flame Step (move; at-will) Teleportation

The flamebow can teleport to within 3 squares of any fire creature within 20 squares of it.
