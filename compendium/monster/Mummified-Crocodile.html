Standard Actions

Bite At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +21 vs. AC

Minor Actions

Flesh to Sand At-Will

Triggered Actions

Trigger: An enemy within 2 squares of the crocodile makes an attack that does not include the crocodile as a target.

Effect (Immediate Reaction): The crocodile uses bite against the triggering enemy.
