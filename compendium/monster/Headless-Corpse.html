this spell's area becomes a swirling storm of force blades; Burst 2 within 10 squares. Any creature that starts its turn in this area is subject to an attack; +14 vs AC; 2d6+4 force damage, and the target is knocked prone. The headless corpse is immune to this effect. As a move action, the headless corpse can move this zone 4 squares. The zone lasts until the end of the encounter.

Phantom Step (move; recharge ) Arcane, Illusion

the headless corpse turns invisible and can move 6 squares. It remains invisible until the end of its next turn.
