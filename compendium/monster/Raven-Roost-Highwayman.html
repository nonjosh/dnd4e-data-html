Standard Actions

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +12 vs. AC

Effect: The target is marked until the end of the highwayman’s next turn.

Effect (Immediate Reaction): Bolt to the gut recharges, and the highwayman uses it against the triggering enemy.
