Dreambreath Dracolich
Huge natural magical beast (dragon, undead)
Level 19 Solo Controller XP 12000

Initiative +13        Senses Perception +13; Darkvision
HP 905; Bloodied 452
AC 36; Fortitude 34, Reflex 33, Will 35
Immune disease, fear, poison; Resist 30 necrotic; Vulnerable 10 radiant
Saving Throws +5
Speed 8, fly 10 (clumsy)
Action Points 2

Bite (standard, at-will)

Reach 3; +24 vs AC; 2d8+8 damage.

Nightmare Fury (standard, at-will)

+23 vs ; 3d6+8 damage.. The dracolich makes a bite attack against each of two different targets.

Nightmare Gaze (immediate interrupt, when an enemy attacks the dreambreath, recharge ) Fear, Gaze, Psychic

The dreambreath dracolich fixes the target with its gaze; ranged 10; targets the triggering enemy; +24 vs Will; 2d6+6 psychic damage, and the target makes a melee or ranged basic attack (dragon’s choice) as a free action against a randomly chosen adjacent ally.

Breath Weapon (standard, recharge ) Psychic, Sleep

Close blast 5; +24 vs Will; the target falls unconscious (save ends). Each Failed Saving Throw: The target takes 10 psychic damage and loses its lowest-level, unused encounter power until the end of the encounter.

Bloodied Breath (free, when first bloodied, encounter) Psychic, Sleep

The dreambreath dracolich’s breath weapon recharges, and the dracolich uses it immediately.

Nightmare Presence (standard, encounter) Fear, Sleep

Close burst 10; targets enemies; +24 vs Will; the target is immobilized (save ends). First Failed Saving Throw: The target falls unconscious (save ends).

Nightmare Roar (standard, recharge ) Fear, Psychic

Close burst 5; +24 vs Will; 2d8+8 psychic damage, and the target is pushed 5 squares and is dazed (save ends).

Nightmare Walk (move; at-will)

The dreambreath dracolich moves up to its speed. It gains the insubstantial and phasing qualities during this move.
