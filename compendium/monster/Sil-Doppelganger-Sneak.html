Shapeshifter Feint (move, at-will)

The doppelganger shifts 3 then attacks an adjacent target; +6 vs Reflex; the doppelganger gains combat advantage against the target until the end of the doppelganger's next turn.

Combat Advantage

The doppelganger sneak deals an extra 1d6 damage against any target it has combat advantage against.

Change Shape (minor; at-will) Polymorph

A doppelganger can alter its physical form to take on the appearance of any Medium humanoid, including a unique individual.
