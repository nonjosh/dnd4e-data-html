Traits

Stand the Ground

Whenever Ulruun is pulled, pushed, or slid, he can move 1 square less than the effect specifies.

Steady-Footed

Ulruun can make a saving throw to avoid falling prone.

Standard Actions

Dagger (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 4d4 + 6 damage.

Effect: The target is marked until the end of Ulruun’s next turn.

Hamstring (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +11 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + 7 damage. Until the end of Ulruun’s next turn, the target falls prone if it moves more than half its speed as part of a move action.

Minor Actions

Hurled Cane (weapon) Encounter

Attack: Ranged 5 (one creature); +11 vs. Reflex

Hit: The target falls prone and is immobilized until the end of Ulruun’s next turn.

Triggered Actions

Stay Put At-Will

Trigger: An enemy marked by Ulruun shifts away from a square that is adjacent to Ulruun.

Effect (Immediate Interrupt): Ulruun uses hamstring against the triggering enemy.
