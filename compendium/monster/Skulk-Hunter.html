Traits

Stealthy Step

The hunter does not take a penalty to Stealth checks for moving more than 2 squares or for running.

Standard Actions

Baffling Blade (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +10 vs. AC

Hit: 1d8 + 6 damage, and the hunter remains hidden from the target if it was hidden from the target when it attacked.

Effect: The target is marked until the end of the hunter’s next turn.

Invisible Blade (weapon) Recharge

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature that cannot see the hunter); +10 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 6 damage, and ongoing 5 damage (save ends).

Effect: The target is marked until the end of the hunter’s next turn.

Triggered Actions

Watchful Hunter At-Will

Trigger: An enemy marked by the hunter shifts.

Effect (Opportunity Action): The hunter can use baffling blade against the triggering enemy.
