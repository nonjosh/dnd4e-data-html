Steppe Pony
Large natural beast (mount)
Level 6 Brute XP 300

Initiative +7        Senses Perception +12; low-light vision
HP 90; Bloodied 45
AC 20; Fortitude 19, Reflex 17, Will 17
Speed 8

Kick (standard, at-will)

+9 vs AC; 1d6+6 damage.

Trample (standard, at-will)

The steppe pony can move up to its speed and enter enemies’ spaces. This movement provokes opportunity attacks, and the steppe pony must end its move in an unoccupied space. When it enters an enemy’s space, the steppe pony makes a trample attack; +7 vs Reflex; 1d8+8 damage, and the target is knocked prone.

Charger (while mounted by a friendly rider of 6th level or higher with the mounted combat feat; at-will) Mount

The steppe pony grants its rider a +1 bonus to attack rolls on charge attacks.

Con 20 (+8)      Int 5 (0)      Cha 13 (+4)
