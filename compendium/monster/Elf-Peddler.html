Elf Peddler
Medium fey humanoid
Level 2 Skirmisher (Leader) XP 125

HP 34; Bloodied 17Initiative +6
AC 16, Fortitude 12, Reflex 15, Will 15Perception+8
Speed 7

Traits

Wild Step

The peddler ignores difficult terrain when it shifts.

Standard Actions

Bone Longsword (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +7 vs. AC

Hit: 1d8 + 6 damage.

Double Dealing At-Will

Effect: The peddler uses bone longsword and then shifts half its speed. If it ends the shift in a square where it flanks an enemy other than the creature it just attacked, it uses bone longsword against the flanked enemy.

Minor Actions

Peddler's Command At-Will (1/round)

Effect: One ally within 20 squares of the peddler moves half its speed as a free action. If that ally is an elf, it instead shifts half its speed as a free action.

Free Actions

Elven Accuracy Encounter

Effect: The peddler rerolls one of its attack rolls and uses the second result.
