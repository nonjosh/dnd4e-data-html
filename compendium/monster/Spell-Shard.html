Spell Shard
Medium immortal animate
Level 24 Minion Artillery XP 1513

Initiative +18        Senses Perception +20
HP 1; a missed attack never damages a minion. ; see also energy release
AC 36; Fortitude 35, Reflex 35, Will 37
Speed 2, fly 12 (hover)

Origin Spell (standard, at-will) Fire, Force, Lightning, Thunder

When created, the spell shard knows one of the following attacks, determined randomly:
1—Ranged 10; ongoing 10 fire damage (save ends).
2—Ranged 10; 15 force damage.
3—Two attacks; 10 lightning damage.
4—Area burst 1 within 10; 10 thunder damage

Energy Release ( when the spell shard drops to 0 hit points)

The spell shard makes an origin spell attack as a free action.
