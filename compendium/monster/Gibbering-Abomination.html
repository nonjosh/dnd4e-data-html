Traits

Unnatural Utterances Aura 5

Enemies take a -2 penalty to attack rolls while in the aura.

All-Around Vision

Enemies can’t gain combat advantage by flanking the gibbering abomination.

Combat Advantage

Whenever the gibbering abomination hits a creature granting combat advantage to it with a melee or a ranged attack, the abomination deals 2d6 extra psychic damage to that creature.

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +21 vs. Will

Hit: 4d8 + 10 psychic damage, and the target is immobilized (save ends).
