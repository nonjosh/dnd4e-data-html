Description: The storm titan Mirmakur is devoted to freeing Solkara and leads the cultists who revere the Crushing Wave. His body is filthy, his hair matted into clumps, and his teeth befouled with dangling seaweed, but his eyes burn with a fanatic’s fervor. Mirmakur’s obsession has consumed his sanity, and he flies into a homicidal rage at the slightest setback. He has even slain many of his own cultists, whom he branded as heretics for failing to fulfill their sacred duty.
