Traits

Combat Advantage

Ormen’s melee attacks deal 2d6 extra damage to targets he has combat advantage against.

Standard Actions

Spear (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +14 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 5 damage.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +14 vs. AC

Hit: 1d6 + 6 damage, and ongoing 5 poison damage (save ends).

Minor Actions

Shapeshifter Feint At-Will (1/round)

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +12 vs. Reflex

Hit: The target grants combat advantage to Ormen until the end of Ormen’s next turn.

Change Shape (polymorph) At-Will

Effect: Ormen alters his physical form to appear as a Medium humanoid until he uses change shape again oruntil he drops to 0 hit points. To assume a specific individual’s form, Ormen must have seen that individual. Other creatures can make a DC 30 Insight check to discern that the form is a disguise.
