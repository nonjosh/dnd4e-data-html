Traits

Devoted of Zindriel

Any time the archer is hit by an attack against its Will, the archer takes damage as normal, but ignores all other effects of the attack.

Standard Actions

Dagger (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +15 vs. AC

Hit: 4d4+ 4 damage.

Longbow (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Ranged 20 (one creature); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 9 damage, or if the target is adjacent to Zindriel, 4d8 + 9 damage, and the archer pushes the target up to 2 squares.

Triggered Actions

Fleeing Shot (weapon) At-Will

Trigger: An enemy ends its turn adjacent to the archer.

Effect (Immediate Reaction): The archer shifts up to 3 squares, then makes the following attack.

Attack: Ranged 5 (the triggering enemy); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 9 damage, and the target falls prone.
