Bushwhack

The centurion gains a +4 bonus to attack rolls against a creature that has no allies adjacent to it.

Falchion (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +11 vs. AC

Hit: 4d4 + 4 damage, and the centurion can slide the target 1 square.

Effect: The centurion marks the target until the end of the centurion’s next turn.

Attack (Immediate Interrupt): Melee 1 (the triggering creature); +11 vs. AC

Hit: 2d4 + 4 damage, and the target is immobilized until the end of the centurion’s next turn.
