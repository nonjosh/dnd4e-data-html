AC 19; Fortitude 17, Reflex 17, Will 18

Close burst 6; +8 vs Fortitude; 1d6+4 acid damage, and the target is pushed 3 squares and blinded (save ends).

The wrack abishai turns invisible and moves 6 squares. It remains invisible until the end of its next turn.

The wrack abishai deals 1d10 extra damage to a target that cannot see it, and that target is dazed (save ends).
