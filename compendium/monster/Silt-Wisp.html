Traits

Blinding Silt Aura 1

The aura is lightly obscured to enemies. Any enemy that starts its turn in the aura takes 5 damage.

Ghostly

Any enemy that makes an area or close attack against the wisp must make the attack roll twice and take the lower result.

Silt Walk

The wisp ignores difficult terrain resulting from dirt, sand, or silt and leaves no tracks in such terrain. The wisp can move across and stand on nonsolid horizontal surfaces (such as silt or water) as if they were solid ground.
