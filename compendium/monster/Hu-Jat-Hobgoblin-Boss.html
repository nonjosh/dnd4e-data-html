Traits

Goblin Phalanx Leader

Hu-Jat gains a +2 bonus to AC while at least one goblin or hobgoblin ally is adjacent to him, and each goblin ally adjacent to him gains a +1 bonus to AC.

Standard Actions

Mace (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +7 vs. AC

Hit: 1d8 + 4 damage.

Javelin (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Ranged 10/20 (one creature); +7 vs. AC

Hit: 1d6 + 5 damage.

Minor Actions

Commander's Call (charm) At-Will

Effect: Close burst 3 (enemies in the burst). Each target is marked until the end of Hu-Jat’s next turn.

Triggered Actions

Mount's Sacrifice At-Will

Trigger: Hu-Jat is hit by an attack while mounted.

Effect (Immediate Interrupt): The attack hits Hu-Jat’s mount instead.

Rider's Clout At-Will (1/round)

Trigger: Hu-Jat’s mount leaves a square adjacent to an enemy while charging.

Effect (Free Action): Hu-Jat makes a melee basic attack against the enemy.
