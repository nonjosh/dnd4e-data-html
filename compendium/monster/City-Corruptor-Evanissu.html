Standard Actions

Hit: The target makes a melee basic attack against a creature of the city corruptor’s choice. In addition, the target grants combat advantage, cannot attack the city corruptor, and cannot move to a square not adjacent to the city corruptor (save ends all).

Effect: The city corruptor becomes invisible until the end of the encounter or until it hits or misses with an attack.
