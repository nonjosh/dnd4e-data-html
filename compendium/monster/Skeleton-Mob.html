Grasping Claws Aura 1

Any enemy that starts its turn in the aura is grabbed by the mob (escape DC 17). Until the grab ends, the enemy grants combat advantage.

Swarm

A swarm can occupy the same space as another creature, and an enemy can enter its space, which is difficult terrain. The swarm cannot be pulled, pushed, or slid by melee or ranged attacks. It can squeeze through any opening that is large enough for at least one of the creatures it comprises.
