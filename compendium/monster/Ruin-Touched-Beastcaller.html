+13 vs AC; 2d6+3 damage.

Murderous Flock (standard, at-will)

A flock of carrion birds (crows, ravens, and the like) appear from nowhere to descend briefly upon the battlefield, screeching and clawing; Burst 3 within 20; +12 vs Reflex; 2d4+5 damage, plus the target is blinded and takes ongoing 5 damage (save ends both). Miss: Half damage, and no ongoing damage or blindness.
Special: The beastcaller’s allies are immune to this attack.

Erupting Swarm (standard, recharge )

Rats, serpents, and insects boil from the earth, ravaging all in their path; Burst 3 within 10 (targets on the ground only); +12 vs Fortitude; 2d4+5 damage, plus the target is immobilized and takes ongoing 5 damage (save ends both). Miss: Half damage, and no ongoing damage or immobilization.
Special: The beastcaller’s allies are immune to this attack.
Aftereffect: The area becomes difficult terrain for the duration of the encounter.

Against the Tide (free 1/round, when the beastcaller hits with an area attack, ) Divine

The beastcaller may choose one of the targets hit by its area attack; +13 vs Fortitude; the target slides up to 3 squares or falls prone (beastcaller's choice).
