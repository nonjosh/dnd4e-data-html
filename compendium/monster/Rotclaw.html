Rotclaw
Large natural animate (dragon, undead)
Level 11 Brute XP 600

Initiative +6        Senses Perception +6; Darkvision
HP 138; Bloodied 69
AC 23; Fortitude 23, Reflex 23, Will 23
Immune disease, poison; Resist 15 necrotic; Vulnerable 20 against critical hits; radiant (a rotclaw that takes radiant damage is dazed [save ends])
Speed 6, climb 5

Bite (standard; sustain standard, at-will)

Reach 2; +14 vs AC; 3d6+6 damage.

Bullying Charge (standard, recharge )

The rotclaw makes a charge attack; +15 vs AC; 4d6+6 damage, and the target is knocked prone.

Eat the Fallen

If the rotclaw is adjacent to a prone enemy, the creature becomes immobilized and cannot attack any target other than a prone enemy. It gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls against a prone target (in addition to any bonus for combat advantage).
