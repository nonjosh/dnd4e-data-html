Rise Again

If a blightfire skeleton wretch is reduced to 0 hit points by an attack that does not deal fire damage or radiant damage, the creature falls prone and appears to be destroyed. At the start of its next turn it regains 1 hit point. However, if it is destroyed 3 times, it is destroyed for good.
