Traits

Double Actions

The giant makes two initiative checks and takes a full turn on each initiative result. The giant can take two immediate actions per round but only one between one turn and the next.

Dual Brain

At the end of each of its turns, the giant ends any dazing, stunning, or charm effect on itself.
