Shadow Jaunt (move; encounter) Teleportation

The shadar-kai gloom lord teleports 3 squares and becomes insubstantial until the start of its next turn.

Shrouded in Gloom

While shrouded in gloom, a creature takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls and gains only half the benefit of healing effects.
