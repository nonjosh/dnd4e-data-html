Traits

Drowning Embrace

A creature that ends its turn grabbed by the lacedon loses a healing surge. A creature with no remaining healing surges instead takes 10 damage.

Hit: 2d8 + 3 damage, and the ghoul grabs the target (escape DC 16).

Requirement: The target must be immobilized, stunned, or unconscious.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +12 vs. AC

Hit: 4d6 + 5 damage, and the target is dazed (save ends)

Trigger: A creature adjacent to the lacedon hits it.

Attack (Free Action): Melee 1 (the triggering creature); +12 vs. Reflex

Hit: The target falls prone.
