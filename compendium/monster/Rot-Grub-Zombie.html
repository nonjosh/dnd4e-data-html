Level 8 Skirmisher XP 350

HP 86; Bloodied 43Initiative +9
AC 22, Fortitude 20, Reflex 19, Will 17Perception+2

Standard Actions

Slam At-Will

Hit: 2d6 + 9 damage.

Flailing Slam At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 2d6 + 9 damage.

Rot Grub Hunger (disease, necrotic) Recharge

Trigger: The zombie drops to 0 hit points.

Effect (No Action): A rot grub swarm appears in an unoccupied square adjacent to the zombie.
