Forceful Silence

When the shriek takes force damage, it cannot use shrieking warp until the end of its next turn.

Standard Actions

Arm Blade At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +19 vs. AC

Hit: 3d6 + 6 damage.

Shrieking Reap (teleportation) Recharge when the shriek uses shrieking warp

Effect: The shriek uses arm blade twice against the same target. If one attack hits, the shriek teleports the target 2 squares, and the target takes ongoing 10 damage (save ends). If both attacks hit, the shriek teleports the target 5 squares and knocks it prone, and the target takes ongoing 15 damage (save ends).

Shrieking Warp (teleportation, thunder) At-Will

Effect: The shriek is removed from play. At the start of its next turn, it appears in an unoccupied square within 10 squares of its last location and makes the following attack.

Attack: Close burst 2 (enemies in the burst); +16 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + 7 thunder damage, and the shriek pushes the target 2 squares.
