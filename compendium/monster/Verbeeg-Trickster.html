Traits

Verbeeg Stealth

The trickster can make a Stealth check to become hidden as long as one square it occupies has superior cover or total concealment from the creature it is attempting to hide from.

Standard Actions

Spear (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +16 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 10 damage, and the target falls prone.

Staggering Bolt (force, implement) At-Will

Attack: Ranged 5 (one creature); +14 vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d6 + 9 force damage, and the trickster slides the target 1 square.

Blinding Blast (implement, radiant) Recharge

Attack: Close blast 5 (enemies in the blast); +14 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 1d6 + 5 radiant damage, and the target is blinded until the end of the trickster’s next turn.

Bewildering Bolt (implement, psychic) Encounter

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +14 vs. Will

Hit: 1d10 + 7 psychic damage, and the target cannot attack (save ends).

Concealing Mist (zone) Encounter

Effect: The trickster creates a zone in a close burst 2 that is lightly obscured and lasts until the end of the encounter. Any verbeeg that starts its turn within the zone gains combat advantage against creatures outside the zone until the end of the verbeeg’s turn.
