Traits

Icy Camouflage

If the pudding is in icy or snowy surroundings, until the pudding takes an action, a creature must succeed on a DC 31 Perception check to recognize that the pudding is a creature. In such surroundings, the pudding gains a +5 bonus to Stealth checks.

Mercurial Body

The pudding ignores difficult terrain and cannot be knocked prone.

Ooze

While squeezing, the pudding moves at full speed rather than half speed, it doesn’t take the -5 penalty to attack rolls, and it doesn’t grant combat advantage for squeezing.

Slam (acid, cold) At-Will
