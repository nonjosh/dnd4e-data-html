Level 5 Skirmisher XP 200

Standard Actions

Bite At-Will

Tentacles At-Will

Move Actions

Unsettling Scuttle At-Will

Effect: The carrion crawler shifts up to its speed. It can move through enemy spaces and climb during the shift. Each time the carrion crawler enters an enemy space for the first time during this movement, it makes the following attack against that enemy.

Attack: Melee 0 (creature in space); +8 vs. Reflex

Hit: The target falls prone.
