Wereboar
Large natural humanoid (shapechanger)
Level 6 Brute XP 250

Initiative +3        Senses Perception +4
HP 61; Bloodied 30
Regeneration 5
AC 17; Fortitude 21, Reflex 16, Will 17
Immune moontusk fever; Vulnerable silver (if the wereboar takes damage from a silver weapon, its regeneration does not function on its next turn)
Speed 6 (8 in boar form)

Maul (standard; usable only while in humanoid form, at-will) Weapon

+9 vs AC; 2d6+6 damage.

Gore (standard; usable only while in boar form, at-will) Disease

+9 vs AC; 1d8+6 damage, ongoing 5 damage (save ends), and the target is exposed to moontusk fever.

Death Strike ( when the werebear drops to 0 hit points, )

The wereboar makes a gore or a maul attack.

Bloodied Resilience (while bloodied)

The wereboar gains a +2 bonus to all defenses and deals ongoing 10 damage with its gore attack instead of ongoing 5 damage.

Change Shape (minor; at-will) Polymorph

A wereboar can alter its physical form to appear as a dire boar or a unique humanoid.

Alignment Evil        Languages Common
Skills Athletics +13, Endurance +11, Intimidate +8
Str 20 (+8)      Dex 10 (+3)      Wis 12 (+4)
Con 16 (+6)      Int 10 (+3)      Cha 11 (+3)

Equipment: hide armor , maul .
