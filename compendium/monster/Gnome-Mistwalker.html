Gnome Mistwalker
Small fey humanoid
Level 5 Lurker XP 200

Initiative +11        Senses Perception +3; low-light vision
HP 51; Bloodied 25
AC 20; Fortitude 16, Reflex 17, Will 16
Speed 5

War Pick (standard, at-will) Weapon

+10 vs AC; 1d8+4 damage (crit 1d8 + 12).

Mistwalker's Strike (standard, recharge ) Illusion

The gnome mistwalker becomes invisible until the end of its next turn. In addition, it shifts 2 squares and makes a war pick attack

Combat Advantage

A gnome mistwalker deals 1d6 extra damage against any creature granting combat advantage to it.

Fade Away (immediate reaction, when the gnome mistwalker takes damage; encounter) Illusion

The gnome mistwalker becomes invisible until after it hits or misses with an attack or until the end of its next turn.

Reactive Stealth

If the gnome mistwalker has cover or concealment when it rolls initiative at the start of an encounter, it can make a Stealth check to become hidden.

Blur of Movement (immediate reaction, when the gnome mistwalker is hit by an enemy; at-will) Teleportation

The mistwalker teleports 1 square.
