Effect: Ranged sight (one creature). Until the end of the encounter, the angel can teleport adjacent to the target as a move action.

Effect (Free Action): The angel becomes a 6-square-high pillar until the start of its next turn. While in this form, it is immune to all damage. When the effect ends, the angel makes the following attack.
