When an effect forces the swampvine to move through a pull, a push, or a slide, the seedling can move 3 squares fewer than the effect specifies.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +8 vs. AC

Attack: Melee 3 (one creature); +6 vs. Reflex

Hit: The swampvine pulls the target up to 2 squares, and the target is grabbed (escape DC 13).

Triggered Actions

Poison Lash (poison) At-Will

Trigger: An enemy the swampvine is grabbing uses an attack power that does not include the swampvine as a target.

Effect (Immediate Reaction): Melee 3 (triggering enemy). The target takes 5 poison damage.
