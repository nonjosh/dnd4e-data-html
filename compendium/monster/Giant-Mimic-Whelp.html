Minor Actions

Shapeshift (polymorph) At-Will (1/round)

Effect: The mimic assumes one of the following forms without changing its size. It remains in the chosen form until it uses this power again.

Ooze Form: The mimic becomes an ooze. When it squeezes while in this form, it moves its full speed rather than half speed, it doesn’t take the -5 penalty to attack rolls, and it doesn’t grant combat advantage for squeezing.

Object Form: While in this form, the mimic has resist 15 to all damage, is immobilized, and cannot attack. In addition, a creature must succeed on a DC 29 Perception check to notice that the mimic is a creature.
