Destroyed by Sunlight

Whenever the vampire starts its turn in direct sunlight, it can take only a single move action during its turn. If it ends that turn in direct sunlight, it turns to ash and is destroyed.

Bite At-Will

Hit: 10 damage, and the target is dazed until the grab ends.
