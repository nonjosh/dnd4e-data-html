Tempest Wisp
Medium elemental magical beast (air)
Level 13 Controller XP 800

HP 134; Bloodied 67Initiative +10
AC 27, Fortitude 26, Reflex 24, Will 24Perception+10
Speed 0, fly 7 (hover)
Immune disease, poison

Traits

Insubstantial

While it is not bloodied, the tempest wisp takes half damage from all attacks except those that deal force damage.

Standard Actions

Air Slash At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +16 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d8 + 12 damage.

Whistling Wind At-Will

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +16 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d10 + 10 damage, and the tempest wisp can slide the target 1 square.

Tumbling Updraft Recharge

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +16 vs. Fortitude

Hit: The target is lifted up 20 feet and restrained (save ends).

    Each Failed Saving Throw: The target is lifted up another 20 feet.

    Successful Saving Throw: The target falls.
