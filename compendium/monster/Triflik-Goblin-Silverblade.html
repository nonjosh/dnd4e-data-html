Triflik, Goblin Silverblade
Small natural humanoid
Level 2 Skirmisher XP 125

HP 38; Bloodied 19Initiative +7
AC 16, Fortitude 13, Reflex 15, Will 12Perception+2
Speed 6Low-light vision

Traits

Sneaky

When shifting, Triflik can move into a space occupied by an ally of his level or lower. The ally shifts into Triflik’s previous space as a free action.

Standard Actions

Triflik's Blade (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +7 vs. AC

Hit: 1d6 + 3 damage, or 2d6 + 3 damage if Triflik has combat advantage against the target.

Power of the Blade (weapon) Recharge if the attack misses

Effect: Triflik uses Triflik’s blade and targets his choice of Fortitude or Reflex instead of AC.

Triggered Actions

Goblin Tactics At-Will

Trigger: Triflik is missed by an attack.

Effect: Triflik shifts 1 square.
