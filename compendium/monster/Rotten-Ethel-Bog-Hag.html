Traits

Unwholesome Presence Aura 3

If an enemy in the aura spends a healing surge to regain hit points, that enemy regains only half the normal hit points.

Aquatic

Ethel can breathe underwater. In aquatic combat, she gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls against non-aquatic creatures.

Horrid Scuttle

On an initiative count of 10 + her initiative check, Ethel shifts up to half her speed and uses claw frenzy as a free action. If she cannot use a free action to make this attack due to a dominating or stunning effect, then that effect ends instead of Ethel making her attack.
