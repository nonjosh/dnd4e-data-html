Halfling Ghast
Small natural humanoid (undead)
Level 5 Skirmisher XP 200

HP 64; Bloodied 32Initiative +8
AC 19, Fortitude 18, Reflex 19, Will 17Perception+4
Speed 6Darkvision
Immune disease, poison; Resist 10 necrotic; Vulnerable 5 radiant

Traits

Stench of Death Aura 1

Any living creature that starts its turn within the aura is slowed until the end of the ghast’s next turn.

Standard Actions

Claws At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +10 vs. AC

Hit: 2d6 + 6 damage, and the target is immobilized until the end of the ghast’s next turn.

Effect: The ghast can shift 1 square before and after the attack.

Ghastly Bite At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one immobilized, restrained, stunned, or unconscious creature); +10 vs. AC

Hit: 4d6 + 6 damage, and the target is stunned and cannot regain hit points (save ends both).

Triggered Actions

Death Burst (necrotic) Encounter

Trigger: The ghast drops to 0 hit points.

Attack (No Action): Close burst 2 (living creatures in the burst); +8 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 1d12 + 8 necrotic damage, and ongoing 5 necrotic damage (save ends).
