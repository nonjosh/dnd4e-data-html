Standard Actions

Tail Slap At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 8 damage, and the naga pushes the target up to 2 squares.

Attack: Ranged 20 (one or two creatures); +17 vs. Will. If the naga targets only one creature with this power, it can make this attack against it twice.

Attack: Area burst 1 within 20 (creatures in the burst); +15 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + 9 damage, and the target is dazed (save ends).
