Traits

Regeneration

The mephit regains 5 hit points whenever it starts its turn and has at least 1 hit point. If the mephit starts its turn on the ground, its regeneration does not function that turn. When the mephit takes psychic damage, its regeneration does not function on its next turn.

Blurred Breeze

While flying to move, the mephit gains a +4 power bonus to all defenses.
