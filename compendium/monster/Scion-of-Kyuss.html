Scion of Kyuss
Gargantuan aberrant humanoid (undead)
Level 30 Elite Soldier XP 38000

Initiative +25        Senses Perception +19; Darkvision
Marking Tendrils (aura 2); writhing tendrils surround the scion of Kyuss; enemies starting their turn in the aura take 15 damage and are marked by the scion of Kyuss until the end of the scion’s next turn.
HP 552; Bloodied 276
AC 46; Fortitude 43, Reflex 40, Will 40
Immune disease, fear, poison; Resist 30 necrotic; Vulnerable 15 radiant
Saving Throws +2
Speed 6
Action Points 1

Bite (standard, at-will)

Reach 4; +35 vs AC; 2d8 + 11 damage, and the scion can choose to immobilize (save ends) or grab the target.

Lashing Tendrils (immediate interrupt, when a creature marked by the scion moves out of a square adjacent to the scion, at-will)

Reach 1; +35 vs AC; 2d8 + 11 damage, and the target is immobilized (save ends).

Swallow (standard, at-will)

Targets a bloodied Huge or smaller creature the scion of Kyuss is grabbing; +33 vs Fortitude; the target is swallowed. A swallowed target is grabbed and restrained. A swallowed creature has line of sight and line of effect only to the scion of Kyuss, and no creature has line of sight or line of effect to it. A creature that escapes the grab is no longer swallowed and appears in a space adjacent to the scion of Kyuss. A scion of Kyuss can move normally while it has a target swallowed. When the scion of Kyuss dies, the target can escape as a move action, appearing in the scion of Kyuss’s former space. Sustain Minor: The scion of Kyuss sustains the grab, and the target takes 20 damage.

Breath of Kyuss (standard, recharge ) Necrotic

Close blast 5; targets enemies only; +33 vs Reflex; 5d12 + 8 necrotic damage, and the target is slowed (save ends). Miss: Half damage, and the target is slowed until the end of the scion’s next turn.

Alignment Chaotic Evil        Languages Common
Str 32 (+26)      Dex 26 (+23)      Wis 18 (+19)
Con 28 (+24)      Int 20 (+20)      Cha 21 (+20)
