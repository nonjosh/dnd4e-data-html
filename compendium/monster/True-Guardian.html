Standard Actions

Alhulak (necrotic, weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +15 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 9 damage, or 25 damage plus 2d8 necrotic damage if the guardian scores a critical hit.

Effect: The target grants combat advantage until it hits or misses the guardian with an attack.

Minor Actions

True Devotion At-Will

Effect: The guardian can end any number of dazed, immobilized, restrained, or slowed effects, taking 5 damage for each effect ended in this way.

Triggered Actions

Alhulak Trip (necrotic, weapon) At-Will

Trigger: An enemy adjacent to the guardian willingly shifts.

Attack (Immediate Reaction): Melee 2 (the triggering enemy); +13 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d8 + 9 damage, or 25 damage plus 2d8 necrotic damage if the guardian scores a critical hit, and the target falls prone.

Defiling Strike (necrotic) Encounter

Trigger: The guardian hits with alhulak.

Effect (Free Action): The target also takes ongoing 10 necrotic damage (save ends), and the ground in a close burst 1 centered on the guardian becomes black sand.
