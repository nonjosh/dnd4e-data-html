Traits

Swarm Attack Aura 1

Any enemy that starts its turn in the aura is slowed until the start of its next turn.

Swarm

The swarm can occupy the same space as another creature, and an enemy can enter its space, which is difficult terrain. The swarm cannot be pulled, pushed, or slid by melee or ranged attacks. It can squeeze through any opening that is large enough for a Small creature.

Standard Actions

Swarm of Entrails (poison) At-Will

Attack: Close burst 2 (enemies in the burst); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 1d8 + 5 poison damage, plus 1d8 poison damage if the target is slowed. The swarm can slide the target 1 square to a square adjacent to it.
