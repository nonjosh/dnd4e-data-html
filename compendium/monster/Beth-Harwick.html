Beth Harwick
Medium natural humanoid (undead)
Level 10 Brute XP 300

HP 129; Bloodied 64Initiative +10
AC 22, Fortitude 22, Reflex 22, Will 20Perception+11
Speed 8, climb 4Darkvision
Immune disease, poison; Resist 10 necrotic; Vulnerable 5 radiant

Standard Actions

Claws At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 3d6 + 5 damage, and Beth grabs the target (escape DC 18).

Mouthful of Flesh At-Will

Requirement: The target must be immobilized, stunned, or unconscious.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 5d6 + 5 damage, and the target is dazed (save ends). Beth gains 10 temporary hit points.

Feeding Frenzy Encounter

Effect: Beth makes a mouthful of flesh attack against each enemy adjacent to her, even if the enemy is not immobilized, stunned, or unconscious.

Smash Aside At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +11 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d8 + 5 damage. Beth slides the target up to 4 squares, and the target falls prone and is dazed (save ends). Beth can use this attack in place of a basic attack as part of a charge.
