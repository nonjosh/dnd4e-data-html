Bigby's Icy Grasp (standard; requires wand; sustain minor, daily) Arcane, Cold, Conjuration, Implement

Jaccobux conjures a 5-foot tall hand of ice in an unoccupied square within range, and the hand attacks one target adjacent to it; ranged 20; +19 vs Reflex; 2d8+6 cold damage (crit 3d6 + 22) and the target is grabbed (until escape, DC 23). As a move action, Jaccobux can move the hand up to 6 squares.
When Jaccobux sustains the hand, a target it has grabbed takes 1d8 + 6 cold damage. As a standard action, Jaccobux can attack another target with the hand, but it must release a target it has grabbed.

Wand of Accuracy (free; requires wand; encounter) Implement

Jaccobux gains a +2 bonus to a single attack roll.

Blur (minor; daily) Arcane, Illusion

Until the end of the encounter, Jaccobux gains a +2 power bonus to all defenses, and enemies 5 or more squares away cannot see him.
