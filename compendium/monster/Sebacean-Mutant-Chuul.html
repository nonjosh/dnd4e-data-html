Level 21 Elite Soldier XP 6400

Initiative +16        Senses Perception +17; Darkvision
Psychic Moan (Psychic) aura 2; the chuul exudes a constant hum of psychic energy. Enemies in the aura take a -2 penalty to Will defense and gain vulnerable 5 psychic.

AC 39; Fortitude 37, Reflex 33, Will 34
