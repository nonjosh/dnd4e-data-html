Nycademon
Large elemental humanoid (demon)
Level 22 Skirmisher XP 4150

Initiative +21        Senses Perception +17
HP 210; Bloodied 105
AC 35; Fortitude 34, Reflex 35, Will 32
Resist 20 variable (2/encounter)
Speed 6, fly 6 (hover)

Wicked Axe (standard, at-will)

Reach 2; +27 vs AC; 2d8+5 damage (crit 3d8+21), and ongoing 5 damage.

Wicked Edges (standard, at-will)

The nycademon makes two wicked axe attacks

Snatch (standard, at-will)

Before or after the attack, the nycademon flies 6 squares. Reach 2; +25 vs Fortitude; the target is grabbed.

Strong Flyer

When a nycademon moves a grabbed target, it does not have to make a Strength attack. While bloodied, a nycademon can fly at full speed instead of half speed when moving a grabbed target.
