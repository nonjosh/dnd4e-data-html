Double Actions

Morrn makes two initiative checks and takes a full turn on each initiative result. He can take two immediate actions per round but only one between one turn and the next.

Insubstantial

Morrn takes half damage from all attacks, except those that deal force damage. Whenever Morrn takes radiant damage, he loses this trait until the start of his next turn.
