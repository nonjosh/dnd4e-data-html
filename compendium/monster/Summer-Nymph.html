Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +28 vs. Will

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +28 vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d6 + 3 radiant damage, and the target is blinded (save ends).

Call Storm Winds (lightning, thunder, zone) Recharge

Attack: Area burst 3 within 20 (enemies in the burst); +28 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 4d8 + 10 thunder and lightning damage, and the nymph slides the target 3 squares.

Effect: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of the nymph’s next turn.

    Sustain Minor: The zone persists, and the nymph slides each enemy within the zone 3 squares.

Omniscient Strategy At-Will
