Critical Threat

The savage can score a critical hit on a roll of 19–20. When the savage scores a critical hit with greataxe, one ally within 2 squares of it can make a melee basic attack as a free action.

Attack: Ranged 5/10 (one creature); +27 vs. AC

Hit: 12 damage.

Triggered Actions

Unbroken Spirit

Trigger: The savage drops to 0 hit points.

Effect (No Action): The savage’s nearest ally gains 10 temporary hit points and a +2 bonus to attack rolls, damage rolls, and all defenses until the end of its next turn.
