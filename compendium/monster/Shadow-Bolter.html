Shadow Bolter
Small shadow humanoid, dark one
Level 5 Artillery XP 200

HP 50; Bloodied 25Initiative +6
AC 17, Fortitude 16, Reflex 17, Will 16Perception+9
Speed 6Darkvision

Traits

Combat Advantage (necrotic)

Whenever the shadow bolter hits a creature granting combat advantage to it, the bolter deals 5 extra necrotic damage, and each of that creature’s adjacent allies takes 5 necrotic damage.

Deadly Precision

The shadow bolter’s ranged attacks have combat advantage against any enemy that the bolter’s allies are flanking.

Standard Actions

Dagger (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +10 vs. AC

Hit: 1d4 + 5 damage.

Black Bolt (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Ranged 15/30 (one creature); +10 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 1d8 + 5 damage, and the target takes 5 damage each time it provokes an opportunity attack (save ends).

Move Actions

Dark Step At-Will

Effect: The bolter moves 4 squares, gains a +4 bonus to AC against opportunity attacks provoked by this movement, and gains combat advantage against each creature that it ends its move adjacent to until the end of the bolter’s next turn.

Triggered Actions

Killing Dark

Trigger: The bolter drops to 0 hit points.

Effect (No Action): Each enemy adjacent to the bolter is blinded until the end of his or her next turn.
