Pack Attack

The gravedigger’s attacks deal 5 extra damage to any enemy adjacent to two or more of the gravedigger’s allies.

Standard Actions

Bite At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +10 vs. AC

Hit: 2d6 + 4 damage.

Snatch from Below At-Will

Requirement: The gravedigger must be burrowing and must have no creature grabbed.

Effect: The gravedigger reaches up through the ground and uses bite twice against one enemy, ignoring cover and concealment. If both attacks hit, the target is grabbed (escape DC 15). Until the grab ends, the target is restrained and takes ongoing 5 damage.
