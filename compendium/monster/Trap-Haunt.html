Traits

Insubstantial

The haunt takes half damage from all attacks, except those that deal force damage. Whenever the haunt takes radiant damage, it loses this trait until the start of its next turn.

Trapbound

The haunt cannot voluntarily move more than 20 squares from the place where it died. If it is forced beyond this range, it is weakened and unable to use ghostly possession until it moves back within range.
