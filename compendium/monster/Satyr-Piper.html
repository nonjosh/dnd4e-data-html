Effect: The satyr uses one power chosen from the list below.

    Leaping Stag Dance: Close burst 5 (nondeafened allies in the burst); The target can shift 2 squares as a free action.

    Song of Freedom: Close burst 5 (nondeafened allies in the burst); The target can make a saving throw against one effect that a save can end.
