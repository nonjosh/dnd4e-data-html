Shadow Curse (implement, necrotic) At-Will

Attack: Ranged 5 (one creature); +9 vs. Reflex

Attack: Ranged 5 (one creature); +9 vs. Will

Move Actions

Dark Step At-Will

Effect: The speaker moves 4 squares, gains a +4 bonus to AC against opportunity attacks provoked by this movement, and gains combat advantage against each creature that it ends its move adjacent to until the end of the shadow speaker’s next turn.

Triggered Actions

Killing Dark

Trigger: The speaker drops to 0 hit points.

Effect (No Action): Each enemy adjacent to the speaker is blinded until the end of his or her next turn.
