Description: Death’s malodorous emanations are noxious to the Raven Queen’s servants, thus every procession includes a censer-bearer. These hulking priests swing heavy iron censers from which intoxicating blue smoke issues, fed by burning soporific herbs and other mysterious alchemical ingredients. The cloud’s inebriating effect is useful in quieting mourners when grief interferes with proper decorum.
