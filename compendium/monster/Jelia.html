+13 vs AC; 1d8 + 5 damage, and the next creature among Jelia and her allies to hit the target gains a +3 bonus to the damage roll.

Close burst 10; +12 vs Fortitude; 1d8 + 5 force damage, and the enemy is pushed 1 square away from the primary target.

Close burst 5; allies in the burst gain +1 to AC and can end this bonus as a free action to gain 10 temporary hit points.

Close blast 5; +11 vs Fortitude; 2d10 + 5 force damage, and the target slides 2 squares. It also slides 2 squares each time an attack hits it (save ends). targets enemies

Construct Space (immediate interrupt, when hit by an attack; encounter)

Jelia switches spaces with a construct or living construct within 5 squares. That creature becomes the attack’s target.

Fey Step (move; recharges when first bloodied) Teleportation

Jelia teleports 5 squares.
