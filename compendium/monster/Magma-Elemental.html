Magma Elemental
Medium elemental magical beast (earth, fire)
Level 12 Brute XP 700

HP 150; Bloodied 75Initiative +10
AC 24, Fortitude 25, Reflex 24, Will 22Perception+6
Speed 6
Vulnerable 5 cold (see obsidian crust)

Traits

Obsidian Crust

When a magma elemental takes cold damage, it is also slowed until the end of its next turn.

Standard Actions

Slam (fire) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 4d8 + 5 fire damage

Lava Smash (fire) Recharge

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 4 fire damage and the magma elemental pushes the target 3 squares. The target takes ongoing 5 fire damage (save ends).

Triggered Actions

Magma Burst (fire) Encounter

Trigger: The magma elemental drops to 0 hit points.

Attack (No Action): Close burst 1 (creatures in the burst); +13 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d8 + 6 fire damage and the target is petrified and takes ongoing 10 fire damage (save ends both). This ongoing fire damage ignores the resistance provided by the petrified condition. The magma elemental is destroyed.
