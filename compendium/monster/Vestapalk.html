Traits

Poisonous Wounds (poison) Aura 1

While Vestapalk is bloodied, any enemy that ends its turn in the aura takes 10 poison damage.

Action Recovery

Whenever Vestapalk ends his turn, any dazing, stunning, or dominating effect on him ends.

Instinctive Flyby

Vestapalk can use a free action to fly up to his speed on an initiative of 10 + his initiative check. During the movement, Vestapalk gains a +4 bonus to all defenses against opportunity attacks and can use bite or claw once at any point during the move. If Vestapalk cannot use a free action to make this attack due to a dominating or stunning effect, then that effect ends instead of Vestapalk making the attack.
