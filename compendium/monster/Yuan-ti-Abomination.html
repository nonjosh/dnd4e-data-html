Yuan-ti Abomination
Large natural humanoid (reptile)
Level 14 Soldier XP 1000

HP 140; Bloodied 70Initiative +13
AC 30, Fortitude 28, Reflex 26, Will 25Perception+10
Speed 7, climb 7
Resist 10 poison

Standard Actions

Bastard Sword (poison, weapon) At-Will

Hit: 2d12 + 4 damage, and the yuan-ti marks the target until the end of the yuan-ti’s next turn. In addition, the target takes ongoing 5 poison damage (save ends).

Requirement: The yuan-ti must not have a creature grabbed

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +17 vs. Reflex

Hit: The yuan-ti pulls the target 1 square and then grabs it (escape DC 21).
