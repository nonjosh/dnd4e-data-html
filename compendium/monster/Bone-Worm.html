Bone Worm
Medium natural animate (undead)
Level 7 Elite Skirmisher XP 600

Initiative +10        Senses Perception +6; Darkvision
HP 158; Bloodied 79
AC 21; Fortitude 18, Reflex 21, Will 19
Immune disease, poison; Resist 10 necrotic
Saving Throws +2
Speed 7, climb 4 see also skittering rake
Action Points 1

Rake (standard, at-will) Necrotic

The bone worm shifts up to 2 squares before or after the attack; +12 vs AC; 1d8 + 5 damage, and ongoing 5 necrotic damage (save ends).

Skittering Rake (standard, recharge ) Necrotic

The bone worm shifts up to 7 squares and can make two rake attacks during this move.

Combat Advantage Necrotic

A bone worm deals +1d8 necrotic damage to creatures it has combat advantage against.

Necrotic Advantage

A bone worm gains combat advantage against any creature taking ongoing necrotic damage.
