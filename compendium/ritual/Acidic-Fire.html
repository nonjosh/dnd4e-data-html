Acidic Fire

Component Cost: See below
Market Price: 200 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Thievery (no check)Level: 5
Category: Volatile
Time: 30 minutes

The substance contained by the glass vial is an unstable soup of acid and explosive chemicals. When the container shatters, it splashes burning death in all directions.
