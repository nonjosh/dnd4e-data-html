Bravery Powder

Component Cost: See below
Market Price: 30 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Nature (no check)Level: 3
Category: Curative
Time: 15 minutes

When dried, powdered, and prepared appropriately, a rust monster's antennae can produce a deep red powder that enables a creature to resist the effects of fear.

Bravery Powder
Level 3 Uncommon

This powder, made from the antennae of a rust monster terror, helps fortify the spirit against fear effects.

Price: 30 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable): Minor Action. You or an adjacent ally can make a saving throw against a fear effect. The source of the effect must be 6th level or lower.
