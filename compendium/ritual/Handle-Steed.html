Handle Steed

Your hands and voice soothe a beast so that it will bear you as a rider.

Component Cost: 1 healing surge
Market Price: 275 gp
Key Skill: NatureLevel: 8
Category: Martial Practice
Time: 5 minutes
Duration: See table

You convince a nonhostile creature of your level or lower that has the mount keyword to bear you as a rider. If you have the Mounted Combat feat, you are treated as being three levels higher for the purpose of determining what mount benefits you gain from riding the creature. Your Nature check determines the duration for which the creature will bear you and for which you gain this benefit.
