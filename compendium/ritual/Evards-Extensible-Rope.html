Evard’s Extensible Rope

Your coil of golden rope elongates as you complete this ritual.

Component Cost: Focus (rope of climbing)
Market Price: 1000 gp
Key Skill: Arcana (no check)Level: 10
Category: Exploration
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: 4 hours or until dismissed
