Giggle Gas

“I recommend this to my bard friends. When they bomb, a little giggle gas can get the laughter started.”

Component Cost: See the item's price
Market Price: 1280 gp
Key Skill: Arcana (no check)Level: 9
Category: Volatile
Time: 30 minutes

The potent gas interferes with the thought processes of a creature so it acts in an inappropriate fashion. For intelligent creatures, this means laughter. When you cool the gas, it becomes liquid so you can transfer it into a glass bead for easy delivery.

Giggle Gas
Level 9 Uncommon

A great green cloud erupts from the shattered glass sphere and sets everyone in the area into convulsive laughter.

Price: 160 gp
Alchemical Item
