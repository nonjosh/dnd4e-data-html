Uncanny Strength

You can dig deep within yourself to find the strength you need.

Component Cost: 1 healing surge
Market Price: 100 gp
Key Skill: Athletics or Endurance (no check)Level: 5
Category: Martial Practice
Time: 5 minutes
Duration: 10 minutes

You call upon hidden reserves to gain a boost to your strength. For the duration of this practice, you can take 10 on Athletics checks.
