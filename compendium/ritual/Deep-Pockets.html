Deep Pockets

Component Cost: 50 gp and a focus
Market Price: 250 gp
Key Skill: Arcana (no check)Level: 5
Category: Creation
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: 24 hours

You cause the pockets of a garment (the focus) to hold far more than they normally can. When the ritual is performed, up to five pockets or pouches sewn into the focus can each hold up to 50 pounds in weight or 5 cubic feet in volume. Items in the affected pockets do not change the focus garment’s form or weight. The garment’s wearer can draw an item from an affected pocket as a minor action. When the ritual ends, items in the affected pockets appear in a pile at the garment-wearer’s feet.
    Focus: A fine cloth garment (50 gp).

Published in Dragon Magazine 405.
