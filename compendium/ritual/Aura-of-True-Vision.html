Aura of True Vision

With the last word of the ritual and a wave of your ring of true seeing, you banish invisibility.

Component Cost: Focus (ring of true seeing)
Market Price: 5000 gp
Key Skill: Arcana (no check)Level: 15
Category: Warding
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: 10 minutes

All invisible creatures and objects within 5 squares of the ring of true seeing become visible and cannot become invisible again while within 5 squares of the ring until the duration ends.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 28.
