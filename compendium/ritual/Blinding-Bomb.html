Blinding Bomb

Component Cost: See Alchemical Item
Market Price: 120 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Thievery (no check)Level: 3
Category: Volatile
Time: Standard

This ceramic sphere contain reagents that combine and ignite in a brilliant flash when the sphere shatters.

Blinding Bomb
Level 3 Uncommon

When thrown, this fist-sized ceramic sphere explodes in a blinding flash.

Price: 30 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable): Standard Action. Make an attack: Area burst 1 within 10; +6 vs. Fortitude; on a hit, the target treats all nonadjacent creatures as having concealment until the end of your next turn. Creatures that do not rely on sight to detect other creatures are immune to this effect.
