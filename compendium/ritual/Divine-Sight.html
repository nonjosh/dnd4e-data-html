Divine Sight

An hour of meditation and devoted prayer bestows upon you a divine vision of the world.

Component Cost: 13,000 gp
Market Price: 65,000 gp
Key Skill: ReligionLevel: 22
Category: Divination
Time: 1 hour
Duration: Special

You grant yourself the ability to discern deities and the servants of deities (such as exarchs and angels) who walk among mortals, limning those you see in a holy glow that is visible only to you. When you see such a creature, you also learn to what deity the creature serves. Objects, places, and creatures that are favored or cursed by the gods are likewise delineated, and this ritual allows you to intuit the relative importance of the object, place, or creature to its deity.
