Self-Holding Bag

Tucking your bag of holding into itself, you make it disappear completely into an impossible space.

Component Cost: Focus (bag of holding)
Market Price: 250 gp
Key Skill: Arcana (no check)Level: 5
Category: Exploration
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: 24 hours

You fold your bag of holding into itself and bind the resulting nothing to you. For the duration, you can pull the bag of holding out of an extradimensional space or put it back as a minor action. When the duration ends, the bag reappears at your feet.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 29.
