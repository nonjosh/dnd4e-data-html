Tree Shape

You assume the form of a tree, blending into the forest around you.

Component Cost: 20 gp
Market Price: 100 gp
Key Skill: Nature (no check)Level: 2
Category: Exploration
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: 6 hours

You transform into a Large tree or shrub. While you are in this state, you perceive everything around you as normal. You retain all of your statistics, though you cannot use powers or perform rituals. All your clothing and gear transforms along with you.
