Telepathic Bond

You link hands with your allies, joining your minds with a psychic bond. Now, no matter where you each are, you can speak mind to mind.

Component Cost: 1,100 gp
Market Price: 4,200 gp
Key Skill: ArcanaLevel: 14
Category: Exploration
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: Special

For the duration of the ritual, you and up to eight allies can communicate telepathically. You do not need to share a common language to communicate telepathically. Your Arcana check result determines the duration of the ritual.
