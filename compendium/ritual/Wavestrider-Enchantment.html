Wavestrider Enchantment

Your wavestrider boots allow you and your allies to walk on water.

Component Cost: Focus (wavestrider boots)
Market Price: 175 gp
Key Skill: ArcanaLevel: 4
Category: Exploration
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: 1 hour

You and each ally within 2 squares of you can move on water as if it were solid ground. Rapids or choppy seas are considered difficult terrain. The target of this ritual can end its benefit as a free action. The target can immerse itself in water or swim underwater if desired without needing to end the ritual.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 30.
