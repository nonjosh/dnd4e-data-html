Alchemist's Frost

Component Cost: See Alchemical Item
Market Price: 70 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Thievery (no check)Level: 1
Category: Volatile
Time: 30 minutes

Alchemist’s frost is sealed in an insulated ceramic vial. When it shatters, the sudden exposure to air causes the alchemist’s frost to expand and freeze.

Modification: Ammunition (level + 1). You create this item for use with a ranged weapon such as a bow, a crossbow, or a sling. Item’s range becomes the range of the weapon but continues to use the indicated attack modifier. You do not include a weapon’s proficiency bonus or enhancement bonus in the attack.
