Alchemist’s Spark

Component Cost: See below
Market Price: 120 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Thievery (no check)Level: 3
Category: Volatile
Time: 30 minutes

The ceramic container holding alchemist’s spark has two chambers. When shattered, the chemicals vaporize and mix to create a bright discharge of lightning.

Alchemist's Spark
Level 3 Uncommon

The shattered flask releases two clouds that quickly coalesce and release a dazzling blast of energy.

Price: 30 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable • Lightning): Standard Action. Make an attack: Area burst 1 within 10; targets each creature in burst; +6 vs. Reflex; 1d6 lightning damage, and the target takes a -1 penalty to attack rolls until the start of your next turn.
