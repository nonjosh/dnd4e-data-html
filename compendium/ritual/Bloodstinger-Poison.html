Bloodstinger Poison

Component Cost: See Alchemical Item
Market Price: 120 gp
Key Skill: Nature or Thievery (no check)Level: 3
Category: Poison
Time: 30 minutes

This black poison comes from the chemically enhanced poisons of spiders, centipedes, and scorpions.

Bloodstinger Poison
Level 3 Uncommon

This inky toxin inflicts wounds that burn long after the initial blow is struck.

Price: 30 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable • Poison): Standard Action. Apply the bloodstinger poison to your weapon or one piece of ammunition. Make a secondary attack against the next target you hit with the coated weapon or ammunition: +6 vs. Fortitude; on a hit, the target takes ongoing 5 poison damage (save ends).
