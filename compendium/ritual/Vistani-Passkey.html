Vistani Passkey

With a sprinkle of residuum, you make a common tool or key particularly potent for the current situation.

Component Cost: 100 gp, plus a focus worth 100 gp
Market Price: 250 gp
Key Skill: Arcana (no check)Level: 5
Category: Creation
Time: Standard action
Duration: See below

You examine a lock or trap as part of the ritual and sprinkle residuum on the focus (see below). After enacting this ritual, you can use the focus to make a Thievery check with a +5 bonus on the lock or trap you examined. Even if you fail by 5 or more, you cannot set off a trap with a check augmented by the bonus from this ritual.
