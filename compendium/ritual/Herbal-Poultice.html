Herbal Poultice

Component Cost: See Alchemical Item
Market Price: 90 gp
Key Skill: Nature (no check)Level: 3
Category: Curative
Time: 30 minutes

This collection of medicinal herbs grants the subject extra hit points when it spends a healing surge after a short rest.

Herbal Poultice
Level 3 Uncommon

This pack of specially prepared medicinal herbs increases one's natural recuperative ability.

Price: 30 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable • Healing): Standard Action. Use before you or an ally takes a short rest. The target of the herbal poultice regains an additional 2 hit points when he or she spends a healing surge at the end of the short rest.
