Spotted Toadstool Venom

Component Cost: See below
Market Price: 600 gp
Key Skill: Nature or Thievery (no check)Level: 10
Category: Poison
Time: 30 minutes

Found throughout the Q’barran swamps, the spotted toadstool is renowned for its poisonous quality. The local lizardfolk make a venomous paste that they use to coat their weapons.

Spotted Toadstool Venom
Level 10 Uncommon

This green-gray poison paste robs its victim of strength.

Price: 200 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable • Poison): Standard Action. Apply the spotted toadstool venom to your weapon or to one piece of ammunition. Make a secondary attack against the next target you hit with the coated weapon or ammunition: +13 vs. Fortitude; the target is weakened until the end of your next turn.
