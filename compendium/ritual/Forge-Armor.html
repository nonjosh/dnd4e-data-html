Forge Armor

Although your means are mundane, the armor you forge is enchanted through your superior skill.

Component Cost: See table
Market Price: 175 gp
Key Skill: Athletics (no check)Level: 4
Category: Martial Practice
Time: 1 day
Duration: Permanent

You construct magic armor. Your level determines the highest enhancement bonus the armor can have (see the table). You can create only magic armor. Armor you forge with this martial practice can be masterwork, provided the armor meets the minimum enhancement bonus.
The component cost is equal to the price of the magic armor you create.
