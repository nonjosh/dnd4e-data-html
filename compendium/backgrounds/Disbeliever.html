Disbeliever

Type: Divine
Campaign Setting: General

In your early life, you never had much respect for gods, but something changed your mind. You still don’t know if you can trust the gods you pray to, but you can’t turn away from the powers they give. What happened in the past that made you wary of religion? What happened to provoke your return to worship? Are you a true believer now, or are you doing the god’s work because of what you hope to gain?
    Associated Skills: Bluff, Insight

Published in Divine Power, page(s) 128.
