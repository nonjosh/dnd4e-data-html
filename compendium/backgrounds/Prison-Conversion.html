Prison Conversion

The scars are just evidence of a wayward youth. I’m actually a testament to the power of prison ministry.

Type: Campaign
Campaign Setting: Scales of War Adventure Path

You did something bad enough to result in your imprisonment, but while there, your life changed. Upon your release, you took up your new life with a crusader’s zeal. For what crime were you imprisoned? How did you attain your release? Do you have difficulty reconciling your old life with your new calling? How do you react when your old lifere-emerges unexpectedly?
Good Background: Paladin.
    Benefit: You add Streetwise to your class skill list, and you gain a +3 bonus to Streetwise checks.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 59.
