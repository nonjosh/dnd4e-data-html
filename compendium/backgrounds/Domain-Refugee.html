Domain Refugee

Campaign Setting: General

You were born in a domain of dread, a region shaped and controlled by a dark power whose corruption and evil is often boundless. Who was this dark master and what was his or her crime? Since few escape these domains, what was your story? How did you escape? Did you leave anyone behind you? Once you were free, where did you go?
    Associated Skills: Bluff, Stealth
